DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Role Purpose:
To support IPCA’s Pharmaceutical R&D initiatives by developing cost-effective, scalable, and regulatory-compliant chemical processes for APIs and intermediates, and contributing to high-impact research projects aligned with global regulatory requirements.


Key Responsibilities:


Chemical Research & Process Development
1. Develop new and alternative cost-effective synthetic routes for APIs and key intermediates
2. Design and optimize non-infringing processes suitable for large-scale manufacturing
3. Work on green chemistry initiatives to improve sustainability and plant friendliness
4. Develop and study novel polymorphs and derivatives
5. Improve process productivity and yield enhancement


Scale-up & Technology Transfer
1. Support scale-up of processes from grams to kilo and pilot-plant levels
2. Ensure smooth technology transfer from lab to plant
3. Collaborate closely with manufacturing and engineering teams
4. Regulatory & Documentation


Generate data for Drug Master Files (DMFs) and regulatory submissions
1. Ensure compliance with global regulatory standards
2. Prepare technical documentation, reports, and experimental records
3. Cross-functional Collaboration
4. Work closely with product development, analytical, and marketing teams
5. Align R&D efforts with market needs and therapeutic domain strategies
