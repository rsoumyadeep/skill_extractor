DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
You are:
• An M.E. / M. Tech in Material science or Mechanical Engineering with specialization in Materials
• Min. 65% aggregate
• Graduating in 2026 without any backlogs
Your typical day:
Position will be owning, executing & realizing results per allocated Project & contribute to short-term & long-term dept. goals & objectives, major responsibilities include.
• Understands & attains execution know-how of all Lam Business Processes, Standards & Compliances, Best Practices.
• Effectively works in the matrix organization by aligning with all stakeholders without compromising on “Quality, Delivery & Cost (QDC)” to meet dept. Goals & Objective
• Leading & realizing Project or Program commitments in consistent with established business processes & by adhering to all engineering due diligence (DFMA, dFMEA, tolerance, calculations, FEA – static, dynamic, thermal, DFX.
• Contribute to concept/design maturity using NX & documentation release (Electro-mechanical systems) .
• Long Term (>2 years):
o Responsible & Accountable to execute multi-discipline (Mechanical, Thermal) & multi-domain (CFD, Ceramic materials, Coatings) engineering problems by sub-systems as things stabilize with full ownership - Concept maturity, Engineering, Testing & Deployment
o Continually works to expand ownership on adjacent modules by acquiring new capabilities
o Participate & contribute to Idea generation forums and contribute to innovative solutions
You need to know:
• Knowledge of various Materials (Al, Steel, SS, Ceramic, Coatings & Treatment) in terms of Properties, Vacuum applications & its compatibility with various chemicals etc
• Understand application of different materials, their behavior at range of temperatures and Pressure applications, Understands various failure modes leading to failure in vacuum applications (Contamination, Degassing etc)
• Application of Materials, Strength of Materials, Theory of Machines, Heat and Mass Transfer and Fluid Mechanics “know-how, concepts & engineering approach” to solve Engineering problems
• Knowledge of machining, welding, molding, sheet-metal fabrication & casting processes
• Hands-on Projects that include Engineering Maturity, Prototyping, Build and Prove.
Nice to know:
• Advanced manufacturing methods – Additive Manufacturing and 3D printing, Water jet machining, Ceramic manufacturing
• Experience with concept development using any 3D CAD - Modelling, Assembly & dwg
• Use of simulation tools such as Ansys, Nastran, Creo Simulate etc.
