DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Role 1 -
Job Title : Engineer  - Green Product Certification (https://ciigreenpro.com/)
Job Description : Developing rating standards & technical documentation on environmental sustainability; assessment & rating of products and technologies based on rating standards; coordinating with expert assessors, conformity agencies; advising national & International clients on environmental aspects of products & Technologies; handling national & International projects; facilitate building and industrial product manufacturers in adopting Green Product Certification (GreenPro), conduct GreenPro assessments, feasibility studies, development of standards, etc.
Education Qualification & Skills Required - PhD / ME / M Tech (Civil / Chemical / Environment / Mechanical engineering) preferred. – Minimum 65% or above

Role 2 - 

Job Title : Engineer
Job Description :    Developing standards & technical documentation on environmental sustainability; assessment & rating of companies on their performance on environmental sustainability and coordinating with expert assessors; advising national & international clients on energy, environmental and sustainable development technologies; technical feasibility studies and handling national & international projects; interaction with key policy makers & government officials on sustainability as part of this profile. Carrying out detailed energy audits, process improvement studies / Carrying out Carbon Footprint / LCA studies. Conducting training programmes related to energy efficiency, Implementation of projects related climate change / sustainability with activities including primary and secondary research, analysis of data, organization of workshops and other meetings, development of reports. 
Education Qualification & Skills Required - PhD / ME / M Tech (Civil / Chemical / Environment / Mechanical engineering) preferred. – Minimum 65% or above
