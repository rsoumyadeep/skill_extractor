DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Detailed Job Description: We are seeking a skilled C++ Application Developer to design and develop a high-performance Ground Control Station (GCS) for unmanned systems. The role involves building real-time mission-critical software that enables reliable command, control, telemetry, and monitoring of UAVs and autonomous platforms. The candidate will work closely with embedded, avionics, networking, and systems teams to deliver a robust and scalable GCS solution.
