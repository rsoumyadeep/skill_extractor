DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Greetings from Molecular Connections!


Molecular Connections is hiring chemistry graduates from NIT and other tier 1 institutes for a high impact Scientific Analyst role in our AI driven chemistry data team, with 35 openings, flexible work options (onsite/hybrid/remote).


About Molecular Connections
Molecular Connections is a leading AI/ML driven data science and content solutions company partnering with global leaders in scholarly publishing, STEM, and pharma.


Headquartered in Bengaluru with multiple delivery centres across South India, the company has over two decades of experience in structuring scientific information using proprietary AI models and knowledge graphs.


What You Will Do


Transform experimental chemistry data from journals and patents into structured, searchable knowledge powering our chemistry intelligence platforms.


Extract, interpret, and curate reactions, substances, and property data, and annotate them using internal curation tools and cheminformatics systems (e.g., SMILES, InChI, structure editors).


Ensure accuracy, consistency, and standardization of data across multiple sources to build high quality chemistry databases used by scientists worldwide.


Stay updated on evolving chemical databases, data standards, and digital chemistry trends, while collaborating with cross functional experts in data science and product.


Who Should Apply


Master’s degree (or Integrated M.Sc.) in Chemistry, Pharmaceutical Sciences, Chemical Engineering, or closely related fields from NITs, IISERs, IITs, and other tier 1 institutes.


Strong understanding of organic and inorganic chemistry, reaction mechanisms, and physicochemical/biological properties of compounds, with the ability to interpret primary literature and patents.


Added advantages (not mandatory): Familiarity with cheminformatics concepts/tools (SMILES, InChI, structure editors), basic coding/scripting, or exposure to chemical databases.


Detail oriented, systematic approach to scientific information, with strong written and verbal communication skills for accurate documentation of insights.
