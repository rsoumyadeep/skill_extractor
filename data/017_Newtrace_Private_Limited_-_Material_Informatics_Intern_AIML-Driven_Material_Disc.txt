DETAILED INTERNSHIP DESCRIPTION
Detailed Internship Description: The primary focus of this role is the discovery of novel materials for alkaline electrolyzer catalysts using a combination of high-throughput computational screening and experimental validation.


Key Responsibilities:
· Novel Material Discovery: Implement Material Informatics workflows to identify and screen potential catalyst candidates from vast chemical spaces.
· Modeling & Simulation: Utilize AI/ML-driven field modeling for Mass Transport and Electrochemical Reaction Kinetics alongside Density Functional Theory (DFT) simulations to predict the activity and electrochemical stability of new compounds.
· Informatics Pipelines: Use open-source tools to query and manipulate data from global repositories to accelerate the discovery phase.
· Experimental Feedback Loop: Integrate analytical data from Microscopy (SEM), XRF, and ICP into the AI/ML model to refine predictions and provide fine diagnostics for material performance.
· Stability Prediction: Develop AI/ML models to predict the long-term degradation and overpotential evolution of novel electrode materials based on Cyclic Voltammetry data.
