Role Overview
As a Machine Learning Scientist, you will work on developing and experimenting with AI models under expert mentorship. You’ll gain exposure to real-world AI applications and get hands-on experience with data pipelines, foundational model training, and research-oriented problem-solving.

Responsibilities
Assist in research and development of AI models in NLP, Computer Vision, or Multimodal AI.
Experiment with deep learning techniques using frameworks like PyTorch or TensorFlow.
Support senior engineers in building and fine-tuning foundational models.
Work with large datasets — cleaning, preprocessing, and preparing data for model training.
Contribute to documentation and presentation of research findings.
Collaborate with a cross-functional team of engineers, designers, and product managers.

Who Can Apply
Strong foundation in mathematics, statistics, linear algebra, and probability.
Familiarity with Python and basic ML libraries (NumPy, pandas, scikit-learn, PyTorch, TensorFlow, etc.).
Passionate about AI research, model building, and exploring new technologies.
Strong analytical thinking, curiosity, and eagerness to learn.

Bonus Points If You Have
Academic or personal projects in AI/ML, NLP, or computer vision.
Exposure to LLMs, transformers, or generative AI tools.
Participation in hackathons, Kaggle competitions, or open-source projects.


Skills
Strong foundation in Python, Machine Learning, Deep Learning basics, NLP or Computer Vision, Data Analysis, Statistics, Linear Algebra, Problem Solving, and a keen Research & Learning mindset.
