DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
About the Role
As a Digital Design Engineer, you will lead block-level designs and verification efforts. You'll be responsible for complex digital systems, timing closure, and power optimization while mentoring junior engineers. This position requires deep understanding of digital design methodologies and the ability to interact with customers to gather requirements and provide technical solutions.
Key Responsibilities
*Lead block-level digital designs and verification activities
*Develop and implement complex digital systems with focus on performance optimization
* Ensure timing closure and power optimization for critical design blocks
* Mentor junior engineers and provide technical guidance
*Interface with customers to understand requirements and present technical solutions
*Drive cross-functional collaboration to ensure cohesive system design


Must Have Skills
1. Digital Circuit Design: Advanced expertise in complex digital circuit design and implementation
2. Timing Closure: Strong capability in achieving timing closure on complex designs
3. Power Optimization: Practical experience optimizing digital designs for power efficiency
4. Mixed-Signal Integration: Ability to integrate digital blocks with analog/mixed-signal environments
5. Digital Architecture: Competence in block-level architecture design and implementation
6. Verification Methodologies: Thorough understanding of digital verification approaches and tools
