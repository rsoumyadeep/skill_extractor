DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
a. Design, develop, and optimize AI and machine learning algorithms for UAV autopilot
systems, including perception, control, and decision-making processes.
b. Use of AI/ML algorithms for route optimization in real-time, adaptive path planning.
c. Collect, preprocess, and analyze data from UAV sensors and simulation environments to
train and validate ML models.
d. Develop and train ML models for tasks such as object detection, obstacle avoidance in
order to achieve autonomous navigation.
e. Using AI techniques to combine data from multiple sensors (e.g., GPS, IMUs, cameras) to
create a comprehensive understanding of the UAV’s environment (self & situational
awareness).
f.
Using ML algorithms to adapt control parameters in real-time.
g. Work closely with hardware and software engineers to integrate AI/ML algorithms into the
UAV autopilot system, ensuring seamless performance and reliability.
h. Utilize simulation tools to test and refine AI/ML algorithms in virtual environments before
deployment on physical UAVs.
i.
j.
Continuously monitor and improve the performance of AI/ML models, addressing issues
related to accuracy, efficiency, and real-time processing.
Stay up to date with the latest advancements in AI/ML and UAV technologies, applying
cutting-edge techniques to enhance autopilot systems.
k. Maintain clear and detailed documentation of algorithms, models, and development
processes.
