DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
**Core Mission:** To support the research team by executing well-defined experiments, diligently managing data and results, and learning the core research stack and methodologies.
- **Key Responsibilities:**
    - Experimental Execution: Implement, run, and meticulously log assigned NLP experiments (e.g., fine-tuning runs, prompt engineering tests, data augmentation impacts) under the close supervision of senior researchers
    - Literature Review: Conduct thorough literature reviews on specific, assigned NLP topics (e.g., new transformer architectures, domain adaptation techniques, or evaluation metrics) to gather SOTA information and support new research hypotheses
    - Data Curation: Assist in cleaning, augmenting, and maintaining the large-scale text corpora used for training and for our internal NLP benchmarks
    - Code Maintenance: Help debug, document, and maintain the team's shared research codebases, particularly those related to data processing, model implementation (e.g., custom model layers), and evaluation scripts.
- **Key Qualifications:**
    - Bachelor's, Master's or Doctorate degree in Computer Science, ML, Statistics, or a related quantitative field.
    - Strong theoretical understanding of fundamental ML concepts (e.g., cross-validation, gradient descent, model evaluation).
    - Proficiency in Python and a major deep learning framework (e.g., PyTorch, TensorFlow).
    - High attention to detail, strong organizational skills, and an eagerness to learn.
- **Primary KPIs:** Task completion rate; Quality and reproducibility of experimental logs.
