ADDITIONAL DETAILS
As a Data Engineer, you are expected to address the following responsibilities:
• AI Solution Innovation, Prototyping, and POCs
o Research and experiment with cutting-edge AI techniques to develop novel
blueprints that address complex business challenges.
o Rapid Proof-of-Concept models and develop scalable AI solutions that can
be implemented in production or new technology systems.
o Participate in translating business problems into technical AI solutions.
o Document experiments, assumptions, and version histories.
o Work on end-to-end solution implementation integrating both technical and
non-technical considerations.
• Innovation-driven Data Engineering and Data Governance Solutions
o Explore and implement modern data technologies (e.g., Delta Lake, Iceberg,
etc.).
o Propose innovative data infrastructure solutions to improve performance,
scalability, and flexibility.
o Participate in planning and development of data models for advanced
analytics, machine learning, or near real-time decision-making.
o Collaborate with cross-functional teams to design and implement end-to
end scalable data pipelines and solutions.
• Solution Prototyping and POCs
o Conduct experiments and Proofs of Concept (POCs) to demonstrate AI
solutions and validate hypotheses.
o Develop prototypes and simulations to translate findings into tangible
applications.
• Data Preparation and Processing
o Collect, transform, and prepare structured and unstructured data for model
training and analytics.
o Support data wrangling tasks using tools like Pandas, NumPy, and basic
SQL.
o Work closely with data engineers to understand data pipelines and
integration points.
• Ensure Data Quality, Reliability, and Governance
o Implement monitoring and validation processes to ensure data accuracy
and freshness.
o Ensure adherence to data security, privacy, and compliance (e.g., GDPR,
HIPAA) as needed.
• Model Development and Support
o Design and implement AI/ML models to solve complex problems and
optimize performance.
o Assist in developing, training, and validating machine learning or deep
learning models under the guidance of senior engineers.
o Experiment with open-source models (e.g., scikit-learn, TensorFlow,
PyTorch) and adapt models for specific client applications (e.g., NLP, CV, or
predictive analytics).
• Continuous Learning and Contribution
o Stay updated with the latest trends in data engineering, cloud computing,
and AI/ML innovation.
o Work in cross-functional teams including software developers, domain
experts, and project managers to define and refine requirements.
o Contribute to innovation initiatives, hackathons, or R&D projects within the
firm
• Application Engineering and Tools
o Apply programming languages and tools for digital transformation and AI
solution deployment.
o Strong hands-on experience with Python, Java, or C# (as per project need).
o Developing Digital solutions using Hyperautomation skills including UiPath,
BluePrism, Automation Anywhere, etc., and integration with AI, Analytics,
Cloud, and Automation platforms.
o Working knowledge of Microsoft Technologies (Visual Studio/.NET,
ASP.NET, API Integration).
o Exposure to Conversational AI, NLP/NLU models, and Omni-channel
Chatbots.
Primary Interactions
• **Internal: ** Collaborate across Data and Digital teams and relevant stakeholders
based on project requirements.
• **External (Depending on Client Project assigned): ** Interface with client-side
business and technology teams to understand requirements and deliver solutions.
Engage with third-party vendors, including product and service partners, for project
specific needs
