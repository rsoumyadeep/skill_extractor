DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
JOB DESCRIPTION
SCIENTIST - INJECTABLE FORMULATION R&D
MAIVA PHARMA PRIVATE LIMITED Hosur, Tamil Nadu, India
ABOUT MAIVA PHARMA
Maiva Pharma is a leading manufacturer of sterile injectables with USFDA, EU-GMP, Health Canada, and PIC/S approved facility in Hosur, Tamil Nadu. We specialize in pharmaceutical development and manufacturing of sterile formulations for global markets.
POSITION OVERVIEW
We are seeking a highly skilled Organic Chemist with a PhD in Organic Chemistry to join our Injectable R&D team. The role focuses on innovative formulation development, particularly converting lyophilized products to stable liquid solutions, understanding and controlling drug degradation pathways, extending product shelf life, and providing organic chemistry expertise to support formulation development activities.
KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
• Work on formulation development and optimization of sterile injectable products
• Work on converting lyophilized (freeze-dried) products into stable liquid injectable solutions
• Convert cold chain products (2-8°C refrigerated storage) to room temperature stable formulations
• Study drug stability and degradation behavior under various conditions
• Design experiments/formulations to improve product performance and shelf life
• Optimize formulation parameters including pH, excipients, and process conditions
• Convert cold chain products (refrigerated storage) to controlled room temperature or ambient storage
• Support method development and validation activities
• Provide chemistry expertise to various R&D projects
• Prepare technical reports, protocols, and regulatory documentation
• Collaborate with cross-functional teams including Quality, Production, and Regulatory
• Stay updated with current scientific literature and pharmaceutical technologies
REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS
Education:
• PhD in Chemistry (Organic)


Skills:
• Strong foundation in chemistry principles and research methodology
• Good experimental design and problem-solving abilities
• Excellent written and verbal communication skills
• Ability to work independently and in teams
• Proficiency in data analysis and scientific documentation
• Willingness to learn pharmaceutical development and regulatory requirements
PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
• Publication record in peer-reviewed journals
• Knowledge of pharmaceutical sciences or formulation development
• Familiarity with ICH stability guidelines
WHAT WE OFFER
• Opportunity to work on cutting-edge innovative formulation projects
• Exposure to end-to-end drug development in a globally approved facility
• Training in pharmaceutical development, cGMP, and regulatory requirements
• Collaborative work environment with experienced professionals
• Career growth opportunities in R&D
• Work in a globally approved (USFDA, EU-GMP) facility
COMPENSATION
CTC: ₹15 Lakhs per annum
LOCATION
Our facility which is located in Hosur, Tamilnadu (India), which is 44 miles (70 Km) south of Bengaluru International Airport and 193 miles (310 Km) east of Chennai Port.
