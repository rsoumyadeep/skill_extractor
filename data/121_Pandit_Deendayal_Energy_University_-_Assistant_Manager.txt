DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
The University envisions a vibrant environment where every faculty member can realize their full potential and actively contribute to institutional growth. Faculty are expected to embody the University’s mission, vision, and values; demonstrate strong personal and interpersonal skills; and show a deep commitment to teaching, research, and service. They are also expected to engage students through innovative, learner-centric teaching methods while maintaining a strong vigour for high-quality research. The specific responsibilities assigned to each faculty member will remain dynamic, guided by the University’s needs as well as the individual’s interests and capabilities.


The University is looking for bright, dynamic and highly motivated Ph.Ds for the post of Assistant Professor  in each of the disciplines of
•Computer Engineering
