DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Digital Signal Processing Engineer


Description
We take great pride in developing cutting-edge millimeter wave wireless systems ground up, to solve problems in the rural and urban telecom infrastructure. To this end, we are looking for strongly motivated candidates who have hands-on experience in developing baseband algorithms for wireless communication. The work will involve the following:
As a developer, you will need to design, develop, test, debug, update and maintain algorithms related to digital signal processing.
Design the overall baseband processing algorithm stack in Python and C++ which can deliver on performance parameters like efficiency, latency, throughput etc.
Optimize the implementation to minimize resource consumption
Develop novel algorithms and techniques that can provide an edge over the industry standard
Design the baseband stack so that it can be converted to RTL with minimum effort


Requirements
Be capable of understanding the latest technologies and go beyond the standards.
Understanding of communications protocol stacks like 3GPP, DVBS2, DVBS2-X
Track record of developing novel DSP algorithms, including filter design and Fourier transforms.
A willingness and ability to get down to the hardware-level debugging as needed
Understanding of FPGA debugging, timing analysis, and logic optimization principles
Have an understanding of DSP algorithms like APSK, LDPC, OFDMA
Have expertise in Multi-Rate Systems, Feedback Control Systems, Estimation and Detection
Have knowledge of DSP algorithm implementation on FPGA using System Verilog
Design and develop advanced DSP algorithms for communication systems, focusing on modulation, demodulation, channel estimation, and error correction
Be an expert in digital signal processing techniques such as fast Fourier transform, and have a good handle on linear algebra and complex algebra
