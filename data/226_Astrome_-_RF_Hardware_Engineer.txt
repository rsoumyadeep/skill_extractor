DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
RF Hardware Engineer
Have you experienced building a communication system by putting together different
ICs ground-up on a PCB? Do you like to make your mark in building next generation
5G hardware? We guarantee maximum fun and deep satisfaction. We are looking for
a dynamic candidate with: Experience in RF and mixed-signal design for wireless
systems and likes to do hands-on design, implementation and testing.


Requirement:


Good understanding of RF wireless system concepts.
Good RF and Mixed Signal hardware debug skills.
Experience in designing RF wireless communication system.
Experience in using high speed RF sampling DACs and ADCs.
Experience in bringup and testing of Mixed signal and RF systems.
Experience in phased array systems is desirable.
Experience in using JESD204B is desirable.
Experience in Ku-band satcom and mmWave frequencies is a plus.
Experience in using ADS/HFSS is required.
Experience in designing phased array antenna systems is a plus.
