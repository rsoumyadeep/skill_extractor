DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Data Scientist I / ML Engineer I - InMobi/Glance
Join InMobi/Glance, a leading mobile advertising and content platform, and impact millions of
users. We're building the future of digital engagement through innovative products like Glance
(smart lock screen content) and Roposo (live experiences platform). We offer a fast-paced,
collaborative environment where you can make an immediate impact, using cutting-edge
technology.
What should you know about joining Inmobi or Glance?
Here, we walk the talk – free yourself, dream big, and chase your passion! On joining, you’ll have
opportunities to make an immediate impact on mission-critical projects, as you work with
highly capable and ambitious peer groups.
Be rewarded for your autonomy even as you collaborate. Ideate, innovate, and inspire by
leveraging bleeding-edge tech to disrupt consumer experiences.
While you work, we’ll take care of nourishing your body, mind, and soul. This includes daily
meals, gym, trainings, tech tools, and regular unwind sessions. Also, feel free to bring your kids
– even the furry ones – to the office!
What you'll do:
Architect and implement solutions for accelerated experimentation and model building. Design
and implement innovative products and proof-of-concepts. Deploy data solutions and pipelines
for efficient ML workflows. Collaborate with engineering, product, and newsroom teams to
deliver ML/AI products. Explore and integrate new platforms, technologies, and algorithms.
Manage project priorities, deadlines, and deliverables.
Qualifications:
• 1+ years in Big Data Software Development or ML Engineering role.
• Experience managing and deploying ML models.
• Bachelor's or Master’s in Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, or related field.
• Proficiency in Python, Scala, Spark, or similar tools with data science libraries.
• Strong background in math and statistics.
• Linux command line proficiency.
• Understanding of data structures and transformation methods.
• Knowledge of ML techniques (clustering, decision trees, neural networks, etc.).
Preferred:
• Advanced degree in statistics, machine learning, or data analysis.
• Experience in research-oriented data science roles.
• Ability to collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams.
