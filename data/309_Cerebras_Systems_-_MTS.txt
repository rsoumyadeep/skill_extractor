DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Cerebras is developing a radically new chip and system to dramatically accelerate deep learning applications. Our system runs
training and inference workloads orders of magnitude faster than contemporary machines, fundamentally changing the way ML
researchers work and pursue AI innovation.
We are innovating at every level of the stack – from chip, to microcode, to power delivery and cooling, to new algorithms and
network architectures at the cutting edge of ML research. Our fully-integrated system delivers unprecedented performance
because it is built from the ground up for the deep learning workload.
Cerebras is building a team of exceptional people to work together on big problems. Join us!
About The Role
As a core software developer, you will directly impact how deep learning models are executed on our hardware and be
responsible for enabling next-generation AI applications. In this position, you will develop software for compilation,
optimization, execution, acceleration, debugging, profiling, and/or integration of deep learning models that run on our systems.
Responsibilities
• Develop software in C++ or Python to optimize, compile, and/or execute Deep Learning models
• Measure, analyze, debug, and improve the software stack that supports these models
• Integrate and deploy successful improvements into production software releases and to customers
Requirements
• Strong proficiency in C/C++ or Python
• Ability to operate at multiple levels of abstraction in a complex software system
• Engineering, Computer Science, Mathematics or related educational background. 
Preferred Skills
• Familiarity with Deep Learning model architectures
• Familiarity with TensorFlow or PyTorch internals
• Familiarity with LLVM or MLIR compiler technology
• Strong knowledge of computer science fundamentals (graph theory, data structures, optimization algorithms)
