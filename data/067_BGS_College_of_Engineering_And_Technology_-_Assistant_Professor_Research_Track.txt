DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
The MBA Program at BGSCET, Bengaluru, invites applications for the position of Assistant Professor (Research Track) in the following areas:
• Finance
• Marketing
• Digital Marketing
• Supply Chain Management
• Strategy
• Operations
This is a research-oriented faculty position, open to candidates holding a PhD, with appointment beginning in Academic Year 2026–27. The reporting/start month may fall between April and August, depending on the area and mutual convenience.
The position is open with an internal tenure-style review after a defined review cycle, as part of BGSCET MBA’s effort to institutionalize a research-track faculty system within its existing academic framework.

---

ADDITIONAL DETAILS
Position Responsibilities
1. Research
• Maintain an active research agenda in the candidate’s disciplinary area
• Build a publication pipeline toward A and A* journals listed in the ABDC journal ranking (or equivalent discipline-appropriate lists)
• Present research at national and international conferences
• Engage in collaborative, applied, or policy-relevant research where appropriate
While immediate A/A* publications are not mandatory at entry, clear evidence of research capability, trajectory, and seriousness is essential.
2. Teaching
• Teach three (3) MBA-level courses per semester
• Deliver courses aligned with the candidate’s disciplinary expertise
• Contribute to curriculum development and pedagogical improvement
• Maintain high standards of classroom engagement, evaluation, and mentoring
3. Institutional Contribution
• Contribute to the development of research culture within the MBA program
• Mentor students on academic or applied research projects
• Participate in limited academic or program-level service, as required
Qualifications
• PhD completed in Finance, Marketing, Digital Marketing, Operations, Strategy, Supply Chain Management, or a closely related discipline
• Strong alignment between doctoral training and intended teaching/research area
• Demonstrated research capability through publications, working papers, or a well-defined research pipeline
• Ability to teach effectively at the postgraduate (MBA) level
Appointment & Review
• Initial appointment will be fixed-term, with continuation subject to annual performance review
• A tenure-style internal review will be conducted after a defined review cycle, based on:
o Research output and trajectory
o Teaching quality
o Institutional contribution
