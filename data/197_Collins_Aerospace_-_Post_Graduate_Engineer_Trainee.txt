DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Skill sets:
• Python (Pandas, Numpy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Scikit learn, Tensorflow)
• Well versed with PHM (Prognostic Health Monitoring) concepts of feature engineering, anomaly detection, model performance metrics, signal processing, statistical analysis
• Knowledge on Physics based analytics & physics informed Machine Learning approaches
• Solid foundation in machine learning algorithms (regression, classification, anomaly detection, time-series forecasting).
Job Description:
• Develop physics based analytics & physics informed machine learning–based analytics for sensory data from aircraft systems (e.g., engines, actuators, fuel and air conditioning systems).
• Work closely with SMEs, design engineers, and PHM teams to understand the physical behavior of systems and translate it into analytical models.
• Analyze and process large-scale time-series data using Databricks, PySpark, and distributed computing frameworks.
• Develop fault detection, degradation tracking, and anomaly detection algorithms using statistical and ML methods.
• Integrate physics-based features (derived from thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, or system modeling) with ML models for improved interpretability and accuracy.
• Perform feature engineering, dimensionality reduction, and signal fusion across multiple sensors and systems.
• Validate and verify analytics using test data and SME feedback, ensuring physical consistency and robustness.
• Collaborate in the development of digital twins, prognostics and health management (PHM) models, and predictive maintenance frameworks.
Other Preferred skill sets that are nice to have:
• Experience developing physics-informed neural networks (PINNs) or hybrid physics + ML models.
• Knowledge of prognostics and health management (PHM), RUL (Remaining Useful Life) estimation, and system-level health modeling.
• Familiarity with simulation tools (MATLAB/Simulink, Modelica, NPSS) for validation and model correlation.
• Experience with digital twin frameworks or model-based system engineering (MBSE) environments.
• Exposure to cloud-based analytics, CI/CD pipelines, and MLflow for model management.
