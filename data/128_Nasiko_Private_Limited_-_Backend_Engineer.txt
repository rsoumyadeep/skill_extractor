DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
As a Backend Engineer at Nasiko, you’ll be responsible for building and maintaining high-performance backend systems using Golang, while collaborating closely with ML and platform engineers to deploy AI-powered services and intelligent features. You’ll design distributed infrastructure, optimize real-time inference pipelines, and create APIs that serve models and data at scale. Your work will directly support the development of AI-driven products and internal tooling across Nasiko’s core platform.
