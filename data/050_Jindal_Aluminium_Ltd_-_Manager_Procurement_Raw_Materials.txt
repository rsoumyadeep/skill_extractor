DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
JOB DESCRIPTION – MANAGER – PROCUREMENT (RAW MATERIALS)
(For company HR records, hiring, and role clarity)
Location Bangalore
Division Corporate
Group (Office/Factory) Office
Department Procurement (Raw Materials)
Sub- Function
1. Job Summary
Responsible for ensuring uninterrupted availability of raw materials through strategic sourcing, supplier development, cost optimization, and compliance-driven procurement practices. The role focuses on vendor management, contract negotiations, risk mitigation, inventory optimization, and alignment of procurement strategies with business, production, and financial objectives. The position plays a critical role in driving cost competitiveness, supply continuity, and governance across procurement operations..
1.1 Reports to: AGM / AVP – Supply Chain / Procurement
1.2 Direct Reportees: Procurement Executives / Buyers / Coordinators
1.3 Working Relationships
Internal: Production, Quality, Finance, Logistics, Planning, Stores, IAD, Safety, Legal, IT/SAP External: Raw Material Suppliers, Transporters, Traders, Auditors, Regulatory Authorities, Consultants
   2. Key Responsibilities
• Develop and execute procurement strategies for raw materials aligned with production plans and business forecasts.
• Ensure uninterrupted supply of raw materials while maintaining optimal inventory levels and working capital efficiency.
• Lead supplier identification, evaluation, onboarding, and performance management.
• Negotiate contracts, pricing, delivery schedules, and commercial terms to achieve cost and service objectives.
• Drive cost reduction initiatives through value engineering, alternate sourcing, and long-term contracts.
• Act as the procurement SPOC for SAP/MM and other digital procurement platforms, ensuring system compliance and data
• Monitor supplier quality, delivery performance, and compliance with agreed specifications and SLAs.
• Collaborate with Quality and Production teams to resolve material quality issues and ensure continuous improvement.
• Ensure adherence to procurement policies, delegation of authority, and audit requirements.
• Manage procurement risks related to price volatility, supply disruptions, and regulatory changes.
• Prepare and review MIS reports covering spend analysis, supplier performance, savings, and inventory trends.
• Support budgeting, forecasting, and cost control initiatives in coordination with Finance.
• Drive digitization and standardization of procurement processes, SOPs, and documentation.
• Ensure compliance with statutory, safety, ESG, and ethical sourcing requirements.
3. Required Qualifications
• BE / BTech / MBA (Materials Management, Supply Chain, Operations, or equivalent)
• 6–10 years of experience in Raw Material Procurement / Strategic Sourcing in manufacturing or industrial environments
