DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Join us in designing and optimising advanced optical systems for space communication applications. You will work with telescope systems, infrared optics, and precision optical instruments across various projects. This role combines expertise in optical design, astronomical instrumentation, and systems engineering to develop cutting-edge communication technologies for terrestrial and space-based platforms.

---

ADDITIONAL DETAILS
What You'll Do


• Design and optimise telescope systems for free-space optical communication (Cassegrain, Newtonian, and custom configurations)
• Develop infrared optical systems and lens designs for 980nm and 1550nm wavelengths
• Characterise telescope performance: aberrations, wavefront quality, throughput, and pointing accuracy
• Perform optical system simulations using ray tracing and diffraction analysis software
• Design optical layouts for beam expanders, collimators, and receiver optics
• Conduct atmospheric turbulence analysis and adaptive optics system design
• Optimise optical link budgets considering atmospheric effects, telescope aperture, and detector sensitivity
• Perform precision optical alignment and testing using interferometry and wavefront sensors
• Integrate optical systems with mechanical mounts and tracking platforms
• Support field testing and outdoor optical link experiments
• Document optical designs, test procedures, and performance specifications
