DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Qualifications & Skills:
• Bachelor’s, Master’s or Dual Degree in Mechanical, Aerospace, Electronics, Instrumentation
Engineering or closely related fields.
• Good understanding of instrumentation and control systems for engines or similar systems.
• Experience with sensors, their installation, routing, and Data Acquisition Systems (DAQ).
• Strong communication skills, attention to detail, and teamwork.
Key Responsibilities:
• Design and implement control and instrumentation systems for gas turbine engines.
• Calibrate and maintain sensors and instrumentation equipment, support CAD team to prepare
instrumentation drawings.
• Lead installation and commissioning of systems in test rigs and engine environments.
