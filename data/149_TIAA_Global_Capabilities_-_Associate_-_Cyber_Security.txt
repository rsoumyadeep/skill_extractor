DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
About TIAA
TIAA(Teachers Insurance and Annuity Association)is a leading provider of financial services founded in 1918 by Andrew Carnegie. With over $1.3 trillion in assets under management(as of September 30,2024),TIAA serves more than 5million active and retired employees in academic, research, medical, cultural, and government fields. The company offers a wide range of financial products and services, including retirement plans, investment solutions, banking services, IRAs, life insurance, and wealth management services. Known for its nonprofit heritage and commitment to sustainable investing, TIAA has consistently received high ratings for financial strength and stability from independent rating agencies.


About TIAA Global Capabilities
TIAA Global Capabilities delivers high-quality global technology, operations and shared services to TIAA's businesses and associates. TIAA Global Capabilities' associates work closely with colleagues in the U.S. and around the world to meet the needs of its ever-growing client base. Since its inception, we have played a vital role within the broader organization and has delivered exceptional performance with highest level of operational excellence.



Join TIAA's Dynamic Cybersecurity Team and Launch Your Career in Digital Defense
Are you passionate about protecting digital assets and staying ahead of cyber threats? TIAA's Cybersecurity department is seeking talented recent graduates to join our comprehensive campus hire program. You'll gain hands-on experience across multiple cybersecurity domains while contributing to the protection of our organization's critical infrastructure and sensitive data.
What You'll Do
Threat Monitoring & Detection
• Monitor security information and event management (SIEM) platforms for suspicious activities and potential threats
• Analyze security alerts and logs to identify legitimate security incidents versus false positives
• Perform initial triage and classification of security events based on severity and impact
• Document findings and escalate confirmed threats to senior analysts and incident response teams
• Utilize various security tools and technologies to identify indicators of compromise (IOCs)
• Conduct basic forensic analysis on security incidents under guidance from senior team members
• Research emerging threats, attack patterns, and tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs)
• Assist in developing and refining detection rules and signatures
Vulnerability Management
• Execute vulnerability scans across enterprise networks, systems, and applications using automated tools
• Configure and maintain vulnerability scanning tools and policies to ensure comprehensive coverage
• Analyze scan results to identify genuine vulnerabilities versus false positives
• Prioritize vulnerabilities based on risk ratings, exploitability, and business impact
• Generate detailed vulnerability reports for technical teams and management & remediation planning and partnership with app & platform teams
• Support remediation validation after vulnerabilities are fixed
• Execute web application security tests using tools like Burp Suite or OWASP ZAP


Data Loss Prevention
• Monitor and analyze DLP alerts across email, web, endpoint, and network channels for potential data exfiltration
• Review and classify sensitive data incidents to determine severity and appropriate response actions
• Configure and tune DLP policies to reduce false positives while maintaining effective protection
• Investigate data movement patterns and user behaviors that may indicate unauthorized data access or transfer
• Collaborate with business units to understand legitimate data usage patterns and refine detection rules
• Perform routine system maintenance tasks such as log rotation, database cleanup, and system health checks
Threat Intelligence
• Conduct daily intelligence assessments and monitor threat detection systems
• Create threat briefings, intelligence reports, and security metrics dashboards
• Correlate threat data with organizational assets and potential impact assessments
• Participate in threat intelligence sharing communities and industry groups
• Support briefings to management and technical teams on current threat trends
• Support intelligence automation initiatives to enhance operational efficiency
• Provide 24/7 support for critical security incidents and crisis response situations
Network Security
• Contribute to the design and implementation of secure network architectures and database systems
• Participate in comprehensive vulnerability assessments and malware defense evaluations
• Document network security risks and recommend mitigation strategies
• Troubleshoot complex network security issues and develop resolution procedures
• Help develop security standards and procedures that align with organizational policies
Identity & Access Management (IAM)
• Manage user access requests for centralized applications within established SLA requirements
• Ensure proper approval workflows are followed for all access provisioning
• Process access requests while maintaining strict security controls
• Educate end users on security protocols and proper access request procedures
• Analyze access patterns to identify process improvements and develop standard operating procedures
• Build, scale and mature the Identity Systems and Processes
• Provide Product Lifecycle Engineering support for IAM / PAM / IGA Platforms and Systems
• Work on Break-Fixes and Enhancement request to target 100% automation for Access Provisioning and deprovisioning tasks
• Collaborate with Cloud, DevOps, Service, AD, Azure Entra, SOLO, SoC teams for problem resolution and solution delivery
• Drive the IAM Compliance as per TIAA Security standards and policies.
• Actively participate in PoCs, Testing, UAT signoff, Product Evaluation, solutioning.
• Help applications onboarding on to PAM and IGA platforms
Security Engineering
Key Responsibilities
Network Security Engineering
• Assist in design, configuration, and maintenance of network security devices including firewalls, IPS/IDS, and network segmentation solutions
• Monitor network traffic patterns and analyze security logs to identify potential threats and configuration improvements
• Support implementation of network access control (NAC) policies and zero-trust architecture principles
• Participate in network security assessments and vulnerability remediation efforts
• Document network security configurations and maintain topology diagrams and security policies
Data Loss Prevention (DLP) Implementation
• Configure and deploy DLP policies across email, web, endpoint, and network channels
• Fine-tune DLP rules to balance security effectiveness with business productivity requirements
• Integrate DLP solutions with existing security infrastructure and data classification systems
• Develop custom content inspection rules and data identification patterns
• Support DLP policy testing and validation processes across different business units
Endpoint Security Management
• Manage endpoint detection and response (EDR) solutions across enterprise devices
• Configure endpoint protection policies including antivirus, application control, and device encryption
• Implement and maintain mobile device management (MDM) and unified endpoint management (UEM) solutions
• Support endpoint security incident response and forensic data collection activities
• Automate endpoint security deployment and configuration management processes
SIEM Platform Engineering (Splunk)
• Design and implement Splunk data ingestion pipelines from various security tools and infrastructure components
• Develop custom dashboards, reports, and alerts for security monitoring and compliance reporting
• Create and optimize Search Processing Language (SPL) queries for threat detection and investigation
• Build correlation rules and automated response workflows within the Splunk environment
• Support Splunk infrastructure maintenance including indexer clustering and search head management
Web Application Firewall (WAF) Engineering /Operations
• Deploy and configure WAF solutions to protect web applications from common attacks (OWASP Top 10)
• Develop custom WAF rules and signatures based on application-specific requirements and threat intelligence
• Integrate WAF logging and alerting with SIEM platforms for centralized monitoring
• Perform WAF policy tuning to minimize false positives while maintaining security effectiveness
• Support web application security assessments and remediation implementation
•
Cybersecurity Development
• Design, build, test, and support security applications aligned with business requirements
• Contribute to custom security solutions and infrastructure development projects
• Build APIs and integrations between security tools to create unified security workflows and data sharing
• Develop security dashboards, reporting tools, and visualization applications for security operations teams
• Create automated security testing tools and vulnerability assessment applications
• Implement secure coding practices and conduct security code reviews for internal applications
Cyber Risk & IT Risk Management
• Establish baseline technology controls based on industry standards and best practices
• Support comprehensive IT risk assessments across diverse technology platforms
• Conduct network, infrastructure, subsidiary, and supplier risk evaluations
• Manage IT issues and findings through validation, mitigation planning, and exception processes
• Support application risk assessments including SaaS and cloud-based solutions
• Develop automated risk assessment capabilities using data analytics


What You'll Bring
Technical Foundation
• Strong understanding of data structures, algorithms, and database management (RDBMS & SQL).
• Knowledge of operating systems and network fundamentals
• Programming experience in one or more languages: C/C++, C#, Python, .NET Core, Angular JS.
• Understanding of web application architecture and development frameworks.
• Familiarity with Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) and Agile/Waterfall methodologies.
• Experience with automation tools (Selenium, Cucumber, QTP) is a plus.
Cyber for AI / AI for Cyber
• Develop, train, and deploy machine learning models for anomaly detection, malware identification, and intrusion detection.
• Work with large datasets from security tools (firewalls, SIEMs) to build and refine AI-driven threat intelligence.
• Create AI-powered automation scripts to streamline incident response and security operations.
• Analyze user and network behavior using AI to establish baselines and detect unusual, potentially malicious activity.
• Collaborate with security analysts and incident responders to integrate AI models into existing security platforms.
• Research and apply the latest AI techniques, including large language models (LLMs), to enhance cyber defenses.
• Implement and leverage AI techniques to detect and analyze anomalies, predict threats, and enhance security operations center (SOC) capabilities.
