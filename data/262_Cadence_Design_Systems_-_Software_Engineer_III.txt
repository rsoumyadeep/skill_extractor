DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
We’re excited to be hiring in Bangalore for the Genus High-Level-Optimization team.
We're looking for engineers with a strong background in C/C++, Data Structures and
Algorithms. Genus HLO team is working on new, cutting edge
synthesis optimizations with deeply algorithmic solutions. Looking forward to
hiring candidates who are interested in driving and defining the next generation
product roadmap.

Bachelors of Engineering degree or Master of Engineering degree with hands-on
programming experience
 Strong C/C++ development skills with a good understanding of object-oriented design
 Strong background of computer science fundamentals (data structures, algorithms) and
Math
 Experience with development on Unix
 Passionate to learn and explore new technologies and demonstrates good analysis,
problem-solving, and debugging skills
 Good written and verbal communication skills, should be a quick learner and a team
player
 Knowledge of Verilog and experience of EDA tool development is desirable, but
optional
