DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
SEDEMAC Mechatronics Limited


Lead Engineer, Electronic Hardware


Job Description:


We at SEDEMAC are on the look-out for a Hardware Design Engineer to join our stellar Electronic Hardware team.


Major Responsibilities:


• The candidate is expected to lead a small team of Hardware engineers working closely with cross functions such as Systems/Software Engineering, Product Validation, Manufacturing, Product Costing etc.
• Single point of contact for cross functional engineering team for product specific hardware development initiatives.
• Defines and manages changes to hardware product requirements.
• Leads product feasibility assessment activities.
• Selection of major components considering competing constraints such as costing, performance, reliability, availability etc.
• Proactively assesses and treats high severity failure modes during the design phase to achieve industry leading product reliability.
• Performs worst case design simulations / calculations & prototype studies to ensure compliance to requirements.
• Defines and communicates HW-SW, HW-Mechanical interface requirements and constraints.
• Assesses and communicates risks associated with design trade-offs.
• Defines PCB constraints.
• Supports development of manufacturing SOPs, EOL test rigs.
• Defines hardware verification plan & amp; performs V&V activities.
• Leads design implementation activities working closely with CAD engineers.
• Represents HW team for RCA activities and ensures implementation of CAPA during the complete HW product lifecycle.
• Proactively identifies and implements opportunities to minimize BOM cost and improve product robustness and reliability.
• Technically mentors junior team members.
• Conducts internal product training & sharing of LLBP and technical knowhow.


Technical Skills & Experience:


• Requirements analysis.
• Product architecture definition, Component selection.
• Design calculation & simulation, Worst Case Analysis, Component derating.
• Thermal analysis, Signal Integrity Analysis.
• Functional/Piece Part FMEA, DRBFM.
• PCB design, DFM, DFT, DFR aspects.
• Usage of lab equipment like DSO/MSO, Function generators, Power supplies etc.
• Failure root cause analysis - 8D, 3x5 Why methodologies.
• Experience in hands-on development of analog/embedded circuits, validation, industrialization, and series maintenance of medium/high complexity products, preferably in Automotive / Transportation / Aerospace or similar industries.
• Hands on experience in reliability prediction, compliance testing is preferable.
• Exposure to development of High Power DC-DC Converters, Motor drives is an added advantage.


Managerial Skills & Experience:


• Collaborative and effective teamwork.
• Ownership and sense of urgency.
• Good judgement and attention to details.
• Good written and verbal communication.


Information on Selection Process:


• The selection process will be multi-phased tests plus interviews.
• There will be a focus on evaluation of the candidate’s understanding of electronic Hardware Design, analytical & logical skills.


Educational Requirement:


• PhD in Electrical / Electronics Engineering (CGPA 6.0+ or 60% throughout)


About SEDEMAC


At its core, SEDEMAC is the convergence of talented engineers committed to the development and large-scale deployment of innovative control technologies and products. As one of the fastest-growing Tier-1 suppliers in the automotive space, we work with vehicle, motor, and engine manufacturers who are leaders in their own space to deliver fundamental control solutions with real-world impact. Our engineer-led organization prides itself on creating technology propositions and taking them from the drawing board to high-volume production with operational excellence. We foster a culture that values creativity, honesty, and respect—where ideas are judged by their merit, not by hierarchy. With a strong foundation and ambitious growth plans, SEDEMAC is uniquely positioned to shape the future of controls.
