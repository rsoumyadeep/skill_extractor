DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Role Overview:


The HR Strategic Partner program is designed to build future-ready HR leaders who will drive
strategic initiatives across TCS business units. Selected candidates will undergo a structured
rotational program across core HR functions such as Employee Engagement and Staffing,
spending six months in each area. Post rotations, they will align with a Business Group HR team
for shaping HR strategies and enabling business transformation.


Key Responsibilities (depending on HR function assigned to)
• Strategic Advisory: Partner with BG leadership teams to design and implement HR
strategies aligned with business objectives.
• Talent Management: Drive workforce planning, talent acquisition strategies, and
succession planning for critical roles.
• Employee Engagement: Develop and execute engagement frameworks to enhance
employee experience and retention.
• Change Management: Lead HR transformation initiatives, ensuring smooth adoption of
new processes and technologies.
• Data-Driven Insights: Leverage analytics to provide actionable insights on workforce
trends, productivity, and engagement.
• Stakeholder Collaboration: Work closely with senior executives to influence decisions
and deliver HR solutions that enable growth.


Must Have:
• MBA/PGDM from premium institutes with specialization in HR or related fields.
• Strong understanding of HR fundamentals and business strategy.
• Excellent communication, presentation, and stakeholder management skills.
• Analytical mindset with ability to interpret data and drive decisions.
• Proactive, adaptable, and capable of working in dynamic environments.


Good to Have:
• Exposure to HR technology platforms and digital HR solutions.
• Knowledge of organizational design, workforce analytics, and change management
frameworks.
• Prior internship or experience in strategic HR projects.


Career Path:
Successful candidates will transition into Strategic HR Leadership roles, driving transformation
across TCS business units globally.
