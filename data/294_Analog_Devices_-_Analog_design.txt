DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Product Applications Engineer – M.Tech / B.Tech
Analog Devices is seeking highly motivated and skilled Engineers with strong foundation
in embedded systems, hardware/software development, and communication skills. This
role is a fantastic opportunity for a professional to work in a dynamic environment and
collaborate across teams to support the adoption of our products across wide range of
applications, including Automotive, Consumer, Industrial, and multimarket.
Roles & Responsibilities:
• Design and develop comprehensive reference designs, including both software
and hardware solutions, for emerging interfaces on ADI products.
• Perform functional and system-level validation, with a focus on compliance
testing for high-speed interfaces such as DDR, USB, SPDIF, and Ethernet.
• Customize reference designs to meet customer-specific requirements, ensuring
seamless integration with ADI processors.
• Collaborate directly with Tier-1 and OEM customers (on-site or off-site) to resolve
technical challenges and accelerate their transition to production.
• Create technical documentation, articles, proof of concept platforms, and
deliver training sessions to customers and internal ADI sales/support teams.
• Act as a key liaison between customers and our product development teams,
providing valuable feedback for the definition of next-generation products.
• Analyze system-level requirements for targeted markets and applications,
working with cross-functional teams to drive product definition and execution.
Candidate Qualifications:
1. Education: Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Electrical/Electronics Engineering,
with strong fundamentals in Digital Signal Processing and
microprocessors/microcontrollers.
2. Core Skills:
1. Strong foundational knowledge of Digital Signal Processing (DSP) and
microprocessors/microcontrollers.
2. Proficiency in C programming is mandatory.
3. Solid understanding of electronics engineering and familiarity with standard
communication protocols such as I2C, SPI, and UART.
3. Desired Experience:
1. Hands-on experience with DSP algorithm implementation and optimization
on processors.
2. Experience with Python and/or assembly programming
3. Familiarity with tools like MATLAB.
4. Personal Attributes
1. Exceptional interpersonal, teamwork, and communication skills.
2. Excellent organizational skills, with the ability to manage multiple projects
and prioritize effectively.
3. Self-motivated, innovative, and a positive team contributor.
5. Travel: Willingness to travel domestically or internationally (10%–15%).
