DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
As a Quantum Research Engineer, you will be working on the ongoing Quantum Information Sciences and Quantum Machine Learning projects.
As a deep tech startup, QAIG Pvt Ltd has a portfolio of IPs driven by fundamental research, which are being converted to products through innovation. As a Quantum Research Engineer, you'll set up tests and deploy promising ideas quickly and broadly, managing deadlines and deliverables while applying the latest theories to develop improved products. From creating experiments and prototyping implementations to designing new architectures, you will work on real-world challenges that span the breadth of Quantum Information Science along with Machine and Deep Learning, Data Mining and hardware/software performance analysis.
