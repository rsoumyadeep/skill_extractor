DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
About Bajaj Auto-
From 1945 to today, from “Hamara Bajaj" to “The World’s Favourite Indian"- the most loved motorcycle brand globally. We are one of the world’s most admired two- and three-wheeler manufacturers and India’s No.1 motorcycle exporter, with products sold in over 70 countries. With more than 18 million motorcycles on roads worldwide, Bajaj Auto is the first two-wheeler and three-wheeler company in the world to have reached a market capitalisation of INR one trillion and continues to be the world’s most valuable two and three-wheeler company. 


Bajaj Auto is on an exciting journey to strengthen its digital foundation and accelerate innovation through the creation of the Digital & Information Technology Organization (DIO). The DIO integrates Digital & Analytics, MIS, and R&D IT functions into a unified and agile structure — enabling a seamless flow of data, insight, and technology across the enterprise. With this integrated setup, the DIO will drive business transformation through advanced digital capabilities, harnessing the power of AI, next-gen platforms, data-driven insights, and modern enterprise architecture. Our vision is to deliver cutting-edge technology solutions that empower innovation, improve efficiency, and enhance the overall customer and business experience.


Key Responsibilities
As a Graduate Trainee Engineer (GTE) in Bajaj Auto’s Digital & Information Technology Organization, you will be part of a high-impact team driving the company’s digital transformation journey. You will:
1. Work on technology-driven business transformation initiatives that connect digital innovation with real-world impact.
2. Contribute to projects leveraging AI, ML, data analytics, cloud platforms, and automation to enhance enterprise efficiency and scalability.
3. Support the integration of IT systems across R&D, Manufacturing, and Business Functions to ensure data consistency and agility.
4. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to design and deploy digital solutions that improve operational performance and customer experience.
5. Participate in architecture modernization projects, enhancing system reliability, security, and speed.
6. Analyze business problems, recommend digital interventions, and contribute to continuous improvement initiatives.
7. Learn from industry experts and technology leaders, gaining exposure to global best practices in digital transformation.


What’s in it for you?
Accelerated Learning Curve: Exposure to diverse technologies and real-world projects from day one.
Expert Mentorship: Guidance from senior leaders and digital transformation experts across domains.
Innovation-Centric Culture: Work in an agile, technology-forward environment that values creativity and experimentation.
Cross-Functional Exposure: Collaborate with teams in R&D, Manufacturing, Supply Chain, and Corporate Functions.
Global-Scale Impact: Be part of initiatives that enhance Bajaj Auto’s global competitiveness and digital excellence.
Career Growth: Build a strong foundation for a long-term career in technology, digital strategy, and business transformation.
