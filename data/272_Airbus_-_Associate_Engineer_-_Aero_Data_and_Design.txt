DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Qualification: PhD or M.Tech with 3-5 yrs. exp in Aerospace / Mechanical Engineering

Flight Physics Mission
Flight Physics delivers optimized overall aircraft solutions for the customer preferred aircraft through driving and developing world class Flight Physics solutions at the core of Airbus aircraft architecture and integration. We are the actors in shaping the future of sustainable aviation.

Brief Description :
Within the perimeter of the Flight Physics Center of Competence, the jobholder is responsible for:
Delivering assigning activities at the right time, cost, quality.
Demonstrate and implement digital skills/emerging technologies to improve existing way of working
Live/demonstrate/promote airbus values and culture in daily life

Main Responsibilities are as follows:
You will be involved in the design and analysis of aerodynamics shapes.
You will be required to design aerodynamics shapes, determine their aerodynamics characteristics using CFD, windtunnel testing, and flight testing; and validate numerical results.

Requirements:
Strong background in CAD
Good competence in CFD
Basic programming languages skills (Python/Matlab)
Overall understanding of data analytics, Machine learning
Proficient problem solving capability
High focus on teamwork and collaboration
High autonomy and creativity
Be strongly motivated
Good communication skills
Ability to prioritize
Advanced level of English
