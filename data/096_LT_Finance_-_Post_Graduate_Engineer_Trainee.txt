DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
JOB PURPOSE:-
You’ll play a key role in supporting the LTF Cloud Integration Platform, ensuring it runs at peak performance.
Your mission: identify and enhance availability, resiliency, reliability, and stability across the board. You’ll also
provide technical guidance, drive smart solutions, and help shape processes that deliver operational
excellence.
PRINCIPAL ACCOUNTABILITIES:-
• Keep our production environment healthy by monitoring availability and maintaining a complete view of
system performance.
• Design and build software and tools to manage platform infrastructure and applications with ease.
• Boost reliability, quality, and speed-to-market for our suite of software solutions.
• Measure, analyse, and optimize system performance to stay ahead of customer needs and drive
continuous innovation.
• Provide core operational support and engineering expertise for large-scale distributed applications.
• Track and analyse metrics from operating systems and applications to fine-tune performance and
troubleshoot issues.
• Collaborate with development teams to make services more reliable through testing, automation, and
streamlined release cycles.
• Contribute to system design discussions, platform management, and capacity planning.
INTERNAL
• Create sustainable, automated systems that simplify operations and reduce manual effort.
• Balance rapid feature delivery with rock-solid reliability by adhering to clear service-level objectives.
• Manage configuration, build-and-release cycles, and CI processes to keep everything running smoothly.
