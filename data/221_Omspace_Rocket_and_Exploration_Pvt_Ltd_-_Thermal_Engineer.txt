DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Job Title: Thermal Engineer


Job Summary
We are seeking a Thermal Engineer for the aerospace industry. The role involves designing, analyzing, and validating thermal control systems for spacecraft systems. The candidate will work on heat transfer modeling, thermal simulations, and material selection to ensure system reliability in extreme space environments.

Key Responsibilities
Design and analyze thermal protection systems for aerospace vehicles and subsystems.
Perform thermal modeling and simulations covering conduction, convection, and radiation.
Conduct temperature predictions, thermal balance testing, and thermal vacuum testing.
Collaborate with structural, propulsion, and systems teams for integrated thermal solutions.
Select and evaluate materials, coatings, and insulation suitable for aerospace environments.
Prepare thermal design documentation, reports, and reviews in line with aerospace standards.
Support failure analysis, risk assessments, and mission assurance activities related to thermal performance.

---

ADDITIONAL DETAILS
Preferred Skills
1.Experience with cryogenic systems, propulsion cooling, or re-entry thermal analysis.
2.Knowledge of multiphysics simulation tools (CFD + thermal coupling).
3.Familiarity with space environment conditions (vacuum, radiation, extreme temperature cycles).
4.Exposure to system-level design trade-offs involving thermal performance.
5.Strong collaboration, documentation, and presentation abilities.
