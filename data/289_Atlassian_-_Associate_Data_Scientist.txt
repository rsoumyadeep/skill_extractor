DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
About Atlassian
At Atlassian, we're motivated by a common goal: to unleash the potential of every team. Our software products help teams all over the planet, and our solutions are designed for all types of work. Team collaboration through our tools makes what may be impossible alone, possible together.


We believe that the unique contributions of all Atlassians create our success. To ensure that our products and culture continue to incorporate everyone's perspectives and experience, we never discriminate based on race, religion, national origin, gender identity or expression, sexual orientation, age, or marital, veteran, or disability status. All your information will be kept confidential according to EEO guidelines.


To learn more about our culture and hiring process, visit go.atlassian.com/crh.


Working at Atlassian
Atlassians have the flexibility to work where they can collaborate most effectively with their team, whether at home, one of our global offices, or both. As a distributed-first company, all interviews are conducted virtually. We hire in any country where we have a legal entity. Learn how we work at go.atlassian.com/distributed.


Your Future Org


Does the thought of being able to influence business decisions excite you? Do you love solving tough problems, and flourishing with variety? Come join Atlassian's growing Analytics & Data Science team and use your skills to have an impact on millions of teams around the world! Atlassian makes it easier for the world to collaborate and build together, and YOU could help Atlassian do that! We’re growing fast, so there’ll be tons of opportunities for you to make an impact and take charge of the direction of your work.


In the Analytics and Data Science team, Your Opportunities are BIG.
Using your eye for business and creative problem-solving, you will build data products and generate insights allowing our business and product leaders to make better-informed decisions. 


Unearth insights from large volumes data and communicate those insights in a way that persuades action by senior leaders, product managers, designers, marketers and engineers.


You'll use SQL, R, and/or Python often, but you won't be at your desk all day. Part of your job is to improve the data literacy of your stakeholders teaching people when, where, and how to harness the data available.


Super-launch your career on a high-performing team while gaining an invaluable introduction to the role of analytics and data science at a fast-growing global technology giant


On your first day, we'll expect you to:
Have experience manipulating data proficiently using 1) SQL, 2) Python, or R Demonstrate the ability to translate business questions into technical solutions, and communicate analytical results to broad audiences


Have a passion for collaborating with cross-functional team members, like engineers, product managers, designers, and other analysts


Qualifications:
2026 Graduate


6+ CGPA (or equivalent)


Bachelors or Masters in a Quantitative discipline (e.g. Engineering, Statistics, Mathematics, Physics, Econometrics, or Computer Science)


No current/active backlogs
