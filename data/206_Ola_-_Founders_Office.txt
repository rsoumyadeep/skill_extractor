DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Job Title:
Founder’s Office - Business Strategy & Tech Strategy
Location:
Bangalore




At Ola, the vision is to build the technologies of the future. With technology at its core, the company is focused on creating businesses of global impact out of India, for the world. It is believed that India has a golden opportunity and a destined responsibility to develop world-class products, and it is this generation’s duty to turn that vision into reality for the nation. 
Ola's brand ethos is centered around impact and purpose-driven business development. The company fosters a culture of impact and empowers its colleagues with the spirit of true entrepreneurship. It is committed to discovering the best talent and providing them with diverse opportunities to lead high-impact projects. 
A purpose-driven individual is being sought to join the office and work closely with the leadership team, contributing to the company's goals. The aim is to build a team of co-pilots who will collaborate with leadership across verticals, working with a highly skilled talent pool to drive the development of cutting-edge products and services. 
Working at Ola would mean that you will define the company's vision & strategy as well as help make it a reality. With immense growth opportunities, you will be an integral part of the decision-making team at the leadership level, helping us achieve speed and scale of operations. 
If you ever dream of taking the plunge as a future leader to bring in a change and create history, come join us! #BuildTheFuture with Ola. 
Key Requirements 
We are looking for dynamic, purpose-driven individuals to join the Founder’s Office and work closely with Ola’s leadership team across Ola Cabs, Ola Electric, and Krutrim. As a part of this role, you will: 
● Directly report to Bhavish Aggarwal and collaborate with senior leaders who bring 25-30+ years of industry experience. 
● Work across multiple business verticals, driving strategic initiatives, problem-solving, and decision-making at the highest level. 
● Engage in cross-functional projects that demand critical thinking, structured problem-solving, and execution excellence. 
● Be at the forefront of innovation and impact, shaping Ola’s future in Mobility, EV, and AI.


This is a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to work directly with industry veterans, drive large-scale impact, and be at the heart of Ola’s next phase of growth.

ADDITIONAL DETAILS
Selection Process


To identify the best talent for this high-impact role, we will follow a structured four-stage selection process: 1. Profile-Based Shortlisting 
Candidates will be shortlisted based on: 
➔ Statement of Purpose (SOP): Showcasing your purpose, resilience, and achievements. ➔ Work Experience: Demonstrating leadership potential and problem-solving ability. 
➔ Academic & Extracurricular Achievements: Highlighting strategic thinking and execution skills.
 2. Group Discussion (GD) 
Shortlisted candidates will participate in a GD round, where we assess: 
➔ Clarity of Thought: How well you articulate and structure your ideas. 
➔ Analytical & Strategic Thinking: Your ability to evaluate and break down complex problems. ➔ Leadership & Collaboration: How effectively you engage with peers in a high-stakes discussion. 
3. Interview Rounds with Senior Leadership 
Candidates clearing the GD will undergo three rounds of interviews with senior leaders from different business verticals, focusing on: 
➔ Business Acumen & Problem-Solving 
➔ Cross-Functional Collaboration 
➔ Execution & Impact-Driven Thinking 
4. Final Interaction with Bhavish Aggarwal 
➔ In the final stage, shortlisted candidates will have a one-on-one interaction with Bhavish Aggarwal to assess their alignment with Ola’s vision, leadership expectations, and strategic goals. This discussion will also provide insights into the company’s long-term objectives and the candidate’s potential impact.
