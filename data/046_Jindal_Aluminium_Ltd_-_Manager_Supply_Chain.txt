DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
JOB DESCRIPTION – MANAGER – SUPPLY CHAIN MANAGEMENT
(For company HR records, hiring, and role clarity)
Location Bangalore
Division Corporate
Group (Office/Factory) Office
Department Supply Chain Management
Sub- Function
1. Job Summary
Responsible for end-to-end supply chain planning, procurement coordination, inventory optimization, logistics efficiency, and digital enablement of supply chain processes. The role ensures seamless material availability, cost optimization, compliance with procurement and logistics policies, and alignment with production, sales, and finance objectives. The position plays a critical role in strengthening supply chain resilience, process standardization, and data-driven decision-making across the organization.
1.1 Reports to: Head – Supply Chain / AVP – Operations / AGM – SCM
1.2 Direct Reportees: Executives / Officers – Planning, Procurement, Stores, Logistics
1.3 Working Relationships
Internal: Production, Marketing & Sales, Finance, Quality, IT/SAP, Stores, IAD, Safety, Admin External: Vendors, Transporters, CHA, Freight Forwarders, Warehousing Partners, Auditors, Consultants
   2. Key Responsibilities
• Lead end-to-end supply chain planning covering procurement, inventory, logistics, and distribution operations.
• Ensure uninterrupted material availability to meet production and customer delivery commitments.
• Drive demand planning and supply planning alignment with Sales, Marketing, and Production teams.
• Optimize inventory levels through scientific planning, ABC analysis, and working capital controls.
• Govern procurement operations ensuring cost competitiveness, quality compliance, and timely sourcing.
• Act as business SPOC for SAP/MM, PP, and SCM-related system enhancements and digital initiatives.
• Ensure adherence to procurement policies, vendor onboarding norms, and contract compliance.
• Drive logistics optimization including freight cost control, route rationalization, and transit time reduction.
• Establish structured MIS dashboards for supply chain KPIs such as OTIF, inventory turns, cost savings, and service levels.
• Develop and standardize SOPs, process workflows, and documentation across SCM functions.
• Support strategic sourcing initiatives, vendor development, and alternate supplier identification.
• Ensure audit readiness and compliance with internal, statutory, and customer audits.
• Collaborate with Finance for accurate costing, GRN reconciliation, and budget adherence.
• Lead continuous improvement initiatives focused on digitization, automation, and process excellence within SCM.
3. Required Qualifications
• MBA / BE / BTech (Operations, Supply Chain, Industrial Engineering, or equivalent)
• 6–10 years of experience in Supply Chain Management, Procurement, Planning, or Logistics roles
