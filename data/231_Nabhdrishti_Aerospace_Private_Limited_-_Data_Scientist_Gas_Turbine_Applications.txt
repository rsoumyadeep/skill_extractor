DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Qualifications & Skills:
• Bachelor’s, Master’s or Dual Degree in Mechanical Engineering, Aerospace Engineering,
Computer Science or closely related fields.
• Strong foundation in data analytics, machine learning, and signal processing.
• Proficiency in Python (NumPy, Pandas, SciPy, scikit-learn), R, or MATLAB.
• Experience with predictive modeling, digital twins, or physics-informed ML is desirable.
• Knowledge of gas turbine components and systems is a strong plus.
• Strong analytical and communication skills with a learning mindset.
Key Responsibilities:
• Analyze large datasets from gas turbine engines, including sensor and test data.
• Develop ML models for anomaly detection, performance prediction, and failure forecasting.
• Contribute to digital twin development and deployment.
• Monitor and refine ML models to enhance reliability and accuracy.
• Collaborate with engineering, testing, and operations teams to translate engineering problems
into data-driven solutions
