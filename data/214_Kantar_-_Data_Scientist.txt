DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Business Overview:


The Global Data Science and Innovation (DSI) unit of KANTAR, nested within its Analytics Practice (https://www.kantar.com/expertise/analytics), is a fast-growing team of niche, elite data scientists responsible for all data science led innovation within KANTAR. The unit has a strong internal and external reputation with global stakeholders and clients, of handling sophisticated cutting-edge analytics, using state-of-the-art techniques and deep mathematical / statistical rigor. The unit is responsible for bringing in a lot of latest developments in the field of Machine Learning, AI, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, NLP, Optimization, etc. to solve complex business problems in marketing analytics and consulting and build products that empower our colleagues around the world.


Job Profile Summary:
As part of a high-profile team, the position offers a unique opportunity to work first-hand on some of the most unique, exciting, and challenging projects within the organization, and be part of a fast-paced, entrepreneurial environment. One gets to work on and influence cutting-edge work with peers from top universities from India, US, and other parts of the world.
You will be constantly learning and trying out new things, dealing with complex technical problems, thinking on first principles, using the latest in the fields of Data Science. You don’t need to know everything yet, but you DO need to have a passion for learning and the flexibility and stamina to do so. You should be able to quickly bring several creative technical ideas to fruition - knowing fully well some may work, and some won’t. You need to be flexible, work well in teams, own your work, and be a good collaborator. Nothing gets done in isolation at Kantar. As such, you need to be a fast learner, a very good problem solver, and an incredibly good coder. These skills are arguably more important than the specific methodologies or technologies that you may know.
If this sounds like you, we look forward to meeting you soon at your campus.


Job Details:
✓ Designation: Data Scientist
✓ CTC -18.5 LPA (fixed).
✓ Location: Chennai


Detailed Job Description
Candidate will be responsible for the following.
✓ Application of sophisticated data science techniques to address a variety of business / marketing problems.
✓ Build, implement, deploy, and maintain state-of-the-art statistical models including machine learning, text analytics, computer vision,
Generative AI etc. across many different industries.
✓ Work with any kind of data - structured (CRM, survey, billing, behavioral etc.) or unstructured (text, image, audio, video etc.).
✓ Using software such as R, Python, SAS, Gauss etc. to analyze different kinds of data (structured or unstructured).
✓ Should be able to appreciate high-level business objectives, and how that translates to technical questions, and execute with efficiency and
impact.
✓ Interpreting results of quantitative analyses and creating recommendations that link modelling results to business issues.
✓ Collaborate in a multi-functional team (dev ops, data engineers, front-end developers, cloud architects, operations, researchers, and product
owners) and diverse global stakeholders to develop scalable processes and protocols for executing analytics at scale.
✓ Attitudinally entrepreneurial, willing to try out new things, explore. Keep abreast of latest developments in the industry and bring in those
learnings from allied fields into our context.


Expected Skills and Proficiencies:
✓ 0 to 3 years’ experience with strong written and verbal communication skills. Meticulous and deep attention to detail.
✓ Proficiency in programming is a must (R / Python at least at an intermediate level). More than the libraries you have used, we are interested in
your ability to develop solutions and translate ideas into code quickly and effectively.
✓ A healthy mix of strong technical depth in data science, as well as consultative capabilities preferred – (we are a technical team, so your
technical competence will be given more weightage!).
✓ Knowledge / Experience in building applications on cloud platforms like Azure/AWS/Google Cloud using serverless architecture is an added
advantage.
✓ In general, a natural ability to learn and deploy new technologies in the field of Data Science, cloud computing, and application development.
✓ Strong statistics background would be a distinct advantage. Some database background (SQL, Hadoop etc.) will be an added advantage.
✓ Be able to appreciate ‘big picture’ and business perspective along with strong focus on execution.


Why join Kantar?
We shape the brands of tomorrow by better understanding people everywhere. By understanding people, we can understand what drives their
decisions, actions, and aspirations on a global scale. And by amplifying our in-depth expertise of human understanding alongside groundbreaking
technology, we can help brands find concrete insights that will help them succeed in our fast-paced, ever shifting world.
And because we know people, we like to make sure our own people are being looked after as well. Equality of opportunity for everyone is our
highest priority and we support our colleagues to work in a way that supports their health and wellbeing. While we encourage teams to spend
part of their working week in the office, we understand no one size fits all; our approach is flexible to ensure everybody feels included,
accepted, and that we can win together. We’re dedicated to creating an inclusive culture and value the diversity of our people, clients, suppliers
and communities, and we encourage applications from all backgrounds and sections of society. Even if you feel like you’re not an exact match,
we’d love to receive your application and talk to you about this job or others at Kantar.
