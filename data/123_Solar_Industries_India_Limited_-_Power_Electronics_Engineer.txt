DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
 Design, develop, and manage the power distribution system ensuring reliable power
supply to all subsystems.
 Integrate electrical systems to ensure seamless functionality, including power
electronics, power converters, and power management systems.
 Ensure the integration of efficient power distribution systems for optimal UAV
performance.
 Handle electrical integration, ensuring that all components such as alternators, rectifiers,
inverters and other electrical and electronic systems work efficiently and without
interference.
 Develop strategies for energy efficiency and battery management systems.
 Monitor and improve power distribution performance over time, identifying and
resolving issues related to power loss, efficiency, and integration.
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define and implement electrical system
requirements.
 Perform tests to evaluate the performance of power systems, identifying potential issues
and ensuring compliance with electrical standards.
Prepare and maintain technical documentation related to electrical designs, system
specifications, and testing results.
