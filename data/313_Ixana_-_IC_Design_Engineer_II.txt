DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Minimum Qualifications and Skills:


Strong background in analog-mixed circuit design and circuit analysis, especially in wireless communications


Working knowledge of integration of analog IPs into SOCs, including verification


Experience recruiting and mentoring junior and experienced engineers


Broad technical knowledge with the ability to learn quickly.


Preferred Qualifications and Skills:


Experience using Circuit Testing equipment (e.g., Oscilloscope, Signal generators, DC sources)


Knowledge of Circuit Design software tools (e.g., Advanced Design System (ADS), LT Spice etc.)


Excellent board-level and firmware debug skills


Experience using PCB design software (Eagle, Altium Designer, OrCAD)


Knowledge of test automation, using scripting languages (e.g., Python and LabVIEW)


Knowledge of different wireless communication circuits is a plus


Outstanding communication and documentation skills
