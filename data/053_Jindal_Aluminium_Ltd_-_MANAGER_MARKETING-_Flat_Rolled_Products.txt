DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
JOB DESCRIPTION – MANAGER – MARKETING-ROLLING
(For company HR records, hiring, and role clarity)
Location Bangalore
Division Corporate
Group (Office/Factory) Office
Department Marketing
Sub- Function Rolling
1. Job Summary
responsible for driving marketing, sales support, and business development activities for Rolling products across domestic and export markets. The role focuses on demand generation, customer engagement, pricing coordination, market intelligence, and order support while ensuring alignment with production capabilities, commercial policies, and corporate brand standards.
The position plays a critical role in translating market requirements into actionable business opportunities and supporting revenue growth through structured marketing processes and cross-functional coordination.
1.1 Reports to: AGM / AVP – Marketing-Rolling
1.2 Direct Reportees: Marketing Executives / Product Marketing
1.3 Working Relationships
Internal: Sales, Production (Rolling Mill), Quality, Commercial, Supply Chain, Finance, Exports, Logistics, Planning, Corporate Communications External: Customers, Dealers, Distributors, Channel Partners, Market Research Agencies
   2. Key Responsibilities
• Drive marketing and sales support activities for Rolling products across assigned markets and segments.
• Coordinate customer inquiries, techno-commercial discussions, and order finalization in alignment with internal teams.
• Support pricing strategies, margin analysis, and approvals in coordination with Commercial and Finance.
• Develop and execute market development plans to enhance product penetration and customer base.
• Manage customer relationships to ensure satisfaction, retention, and repeat business.
• Track market trends, competitor activity, and pricing benchmarks for Rolling products.
• Prepare sales forecasts, demand plans, and periodic MIS reports for management review.
• Coordinate with production and planning teams to align customer requirements with manufacturing capabilities.
• Ensure accurate documentation related to quotations, orders, contracts, and customer communications.
• Support export marketing activities including customer coordination, documentation inputs, and compliance alignment.
• Ensure adherence to corporate brand guidelines in all marketing communications and customer-facing materials.
• Support resolution of customer complaints and quality issues in coordination with Quality and Operations.
• Participate in customer meetings, audits, and technical discussions as required.
• Ensure process compliance, audit readiness, and documentation discipline within marketing operations.
3. Required Qualifications
• MBA (Marketing) / BE / BTech (preferred)
• 5–9 years of experience in Marketing / Sales / Business Development, preferably in Rolling Mills, Metals, or Manufacturing industry
