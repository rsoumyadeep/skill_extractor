DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Responsibilities
• You’ll assist project managers / technical leads with the execution of a broad range of hydrogeological/hydrological technical services which may include involvement in the groundwater components of Environmental Impact Assessments, operational water management and closure/reclamation for oil sands/mining industry
• You’ll provide technical support for geological and hydrogeological data analyses, visualization and development of conceptual site models.
• You’ll apply hydrogeological principles to groundwater projects using conceptual and numerical modelling software (groundwater and integrated surface water-groundwater models).
• You’ll prepare technical documentation to support analytical and numerical studies.
Qualifications
• Environmental / Mining Engineering or related field.
• With Minimum 2 to 7 years if experience.
Mandatory Skills
• An understanding of numerical methods including finite difference, finite element and control volume finite element approaches.
• Proficiency in the use of commercial numerical groundwater modelling codes is an asset (FEFLOW, MODFLOW, Hydro Geosphere, etc.);
• Experience with visualization software (GIS, Surfer, Leapfrog, CAD, etc.);
• Experience with general-purpose scripting/programming is an asset (Python, VBA, etc.)
