DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
STRUCTURAL ENGINEER


Job Location: Bengaluru, Karnataka


About Us:
We are a fast-growing deep-tech aerospace company building the world’s most advanced aero engines for both military and civilian applications. Our current focus includes jet fuel-based propulsion systems for military UAVs, as well as pioneering green hydrogen–powered aero engine R&D for future aviation. Our team culture revolves around first-principles thinking, scientific rigor, and rapid innovation, and we are looking for engineers who are open-minded, technically curious, and ready to solve some of the toughest challenges in aerospace propulsion.


Responsibilities:
• Perform structural analysis of composite structures and rapid design iterations for high-speed airframes. Demonstrate clear understanding of aircraft design concepts and structural analysis especially for high speed military applications
• Calculate aerodynamic stresses as a function of aircraft manoeuvres
• Perform calculations of aero-structure interaction loads, static and dynamic loads, stresses and fatigue using analytical methods as well as detailed FEA models. Implement efficient optimization workflows for rapid design iterations.
• Collaborate with other team members and account for interdependencies of different components on airframe integration and resulting performance and impact on weight.
• Determine dynamic properties of composite structures to estimate damaging modes of operations. Carry out modal analysis and evaluate aerodynamic flutter at high speed conditions.
• Assist in designing vibration experiments, analysing data. Coordinate with manufacturing team with aspects related to composites manufacturing


Qualifications:
• Understanding of basic principles of composite structures
• Understanding of aircraft design, aero structures and aerodynamic load calculations
• Bachelor's degree/MS in aerospace or mechanical engineering; master's preferred.
• Relevant, hands-on experience in structural design and analysis in composite structures
• Understanding of aerodynamics and its impact of aero structural stresses
• Proficiency in structural analysis software (e.g., ANSYS, Abaqus) for FEA analysis
• Proficiency in first principles based analytical calculations and modelling
• Basic understanding of optimization algorithms
• Basic understanding of manufacturing methods for composite structures


What will you gain from this job:
• End-to-end exposure to real-world jet engine development, including control systems, hardware realization, and flight testing.
• Mentorship from experienced aerospace engineers and hands-on work with cutting-edge propulsion technologies.
• Opportunity to work on advanced aerospace R&D in high-speed transonic regimes, using both scientific and practical engineering approaches.
• A strong stepping stone toward advanced careers in propulsion, flight control, embedded systems, or aerospace research.
• Professional work environment with a five-day work week, standard benefits, and collaboration on world-class projects.
