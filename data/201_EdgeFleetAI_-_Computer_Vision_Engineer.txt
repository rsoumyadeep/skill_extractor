DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
🚀 EdgeFleet.AI is Calling the Visionaries
Where Machines Don’t Just See — They Understand.


🧭 About EdgeFleet.AI
At EdgeFleet.AI, we are building something extraordinary —an intelligence that transcends boundaries, disciplines, and machines.
Think of it as a shared brain for autonomous systems —where drones, robots, and fleets don’t just operate… they perceive, predict, and understand the world around them.
We’re pushing the limits of computer vision, from recognition to reconstruction, prediction, and spatial reasoning — creating the eyes and instincts of next-gen autonomy.


💼 Role: Computer Vision Engineer
      Location: New Delhi, India.
      Type : Full Time (On-Site)


🧠 What You’ll Work On


You will be at the core of EdgeFleet.AI’s perception systems — designing how autonomous machines see and understand reality.


Your Mission:
Build real-time 3D perception pipelines (SLAM, SfM, NeRF, Gaussian Splatting).
Develop self-supervised learning and multi-view geometry systems.
Implement object tracking, depth estimation, and pose prediction for real-world Robots.
Collaborate on sensor fusion, reconstruction, and AI-based spatial understanding.
Contribute to open research and prototype breakthroughs in computer vision.

🎓 Eligibility Criteria
Education:
B.Tech / B.E / M.Tech / M.S / Ph.D. in
Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, or Electronics.
Minimum CGPA: 8.0 or above


🧩 Skill Set You’ll Need
Core Competencies
Computer Vision & Deep Learning (CNNs, Transformers, Self-Supervised Learning)
SLAM, SfM, NeRF, Gaussian Splatting, 3D Reconstruction
Multi-View Geometry, Epipolar Geometry, Depth & Pose Estimation
Python, PyTorch / TensorFlow, OpenCV, NumPy, CUDA
2D to 3D Conversion techniques
Camera calibration Techniques


Bonus Skills
ROS, Blender / Unreal / Unity for simulation
Differentiable rendering frameworks (e.g., Instant-NGP, Nerfstudio, COLMAP)

💡 What We Value
A visionary mindset — seeing systems, not just scripts.
Curiosity and boldness to explore uncharted research directions.
Strong mathematical reasoning and coding discipline.
Passion for robotics, drones, and spatial AI.
Desire to build and lead — not just follow.

🌌 Why Join Us
Build the perception backbone of future fleets and robots.
Blend research + real-world impact in one role.
Work with visionaries in AI, robotics, and computer vision.
Push the limits of spatial intelligence — and your own imagination.

✨ At EdgeFleet.AI, you won’t just be coding vision — you’ll be architecting perception itself.
Join us. Let’s make machines not just see… but perceive with purpose.
