DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
(Job Description)
This position offers opportunities to work across a wide range of IT and business domains within the Dai-ichi Life Group. After joining, the successful candidate will be assigned to one of the following areas, with the possibility of future rotation depending on business needs and individual development:
Planning and consulting for business development using advanced technologies
Research, planning, and operation of cybersecurity initiatives
Planning, governance, and management of overall IT business operations
Planning, development, and operation of individual insurance systems (e.g., new contracts, premium collection, claims, accounting, rate/statistics, sales HR, partnerships)
Planning, development, and operation of corporate insurance systems (e.g., group insurance, corporate pensions)
Planning, development, and operation of accounting, HR, and asset management systems
Planning, development, and operation of infrastructure systems
Design, construction, and operation of network systems
Candidates should be flexible, proactive, and eager to contribute to digital transformation efforts. This role provides a dynamic environment for career growth and cross-functional experience.
