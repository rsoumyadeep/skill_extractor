DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
About the company:

We are one of the world's largest commercial vehicle manufacturers, with over 40 production sites around the globe and more than 100,000 employees. We offer light, medium and heavy-duty trucks, city and intercity buses, coaches and bus chassis. Tailored financial services are also part of our portfolio. DTICI headquartered in Bengaluru, Karnataka is a 100% subsidiary of Daimler Truck AG. Our Ambition is to be an Integrated Engineering & IT Centre of Daimler Truck AG focused on Product Innovation.

• Daimler Truck AG - https://www.daimlertruck.com/en/
• DTICI - https://www.linkedin.com/company/daimler-truck-innovation-center-india/
• Link to our Corporate video here
• DTICI, is the 2nd biggest R&D Centre for Daimler Truck AG outside of Germany focusing on the software Capabilities.

Key Tasks & Responsibilities:

• Design and development of Powertrain components (Engine, Transmission & Axles) for electric & conventional vehicles.
• Carryout modeling of Thermal systems (Heat exchangers, Battery/Fuel cell systems) followed by flow-thermal evaluation of systems
• Digital validation using Analytical methods
• Conceptualize, design, and deliver high-quality solutions and insightful analysis
• Adhere to compliance & processes

Education Qualification:

• ME/ M.Tech Thermal, Mechanical and allied branches equivalent fulltime degree
• Consistent academic record
• No backlogs as on Feb 2026
