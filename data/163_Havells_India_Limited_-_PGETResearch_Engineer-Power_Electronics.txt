DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Job Summary:  
We are seeking a talented Electronics Hardware Design Engineer to join our dynamic engineering team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in electronics circuit and power Converter design, experience with circuitry and PCB layout, and a passion for innovation. As a Power Electronics Hardware Design Engineer, you will play a critical role in developing cutting-edge electronic products that meet our customers' needs and industry standards. 
 
Educational Qualifications and Key Skills: 
Master's (M.E./MTech.) or Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering or Electronics Engineering discipline
Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to work both independently and collaboratively in a team environment
Strong understanding of analog, digital electronics, signal processing, and microcontroller-based systems
Strong communication skills to convey complex technical concepts to both technical and non-technical stakeholders 
Attention to detail and a passion for producing high-quality, reliable electronic products
Experience with testing and debugging electronic circuits using lab equipment (oscilloscopes, power supplies, Power analyzers, etc.). 
Experience with embedded systems, firmware development (a plus). 
Familiarity with simulation tools (e.g., PSPICE, MATLAB) and analysis techniques for electronics design. 
Knowledge of PCB layout design and manufacturing processes, assembly, and fabrication techniques. 
 
 Job Responsibilities, but not limited to: 
Design and develop analog, digital and power electronic circuits for various applications, such as Motor drive Controller, LLC, PFC, Bi-directional Converters. Grid Tie Inverters etc. 
Conducting design simulations and analyses to optimize circuit performance and ensure compliance with industry standards. 
Ensuring system performance, reliability, and manufacturability while system design. 
Create schematic diagrams and guide the PCB layout design team, considering factors such as thermal management, and EMI/EMC compliance. 
Collaborate with cross-functional teams including software engineers, mechanical engineers, and product managers to define hardware requirements and specifications. 
Built Prototype and test electronic circuits, identify and troubleshoot issues, and implement necessary design improvements. 
Research and develop various Power converter topologies, select appropriate components, including microcontrollers, sensors, connectors, and other electronic parts, considering cost, availability, and performance. 
Perform design validation and verification tests to ensure products meet design specifications and regulatory requirements. 
Stay updated on industry trends, emerging technologies, and best practices in electronics design using GaN, SiC devices.
