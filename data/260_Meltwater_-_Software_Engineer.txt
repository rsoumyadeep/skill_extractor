DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
What we are looking for:
We are looking for a talented Software Engineer to join our team. This is an exciting
opportunity to work on designing and building state-of-the-art systems, contributing to impactful
projects, and solving challenging technical problems in a product-focused environment. If you
thrive on building robust, scalable architecture and love hands-on development, we want to
hear from you!
What You&#39;ll Do:
 Collaborating with senior developers and team leads to design, develop, and maintain
scalable software solutions.
 Write clean, efficient, and well-documented code following industry best practices.
 Participate in code reviews, debugging, and unit testing to ensure software quality.
 Learn and adapt to new technologies, tools, and development practices in a fast-paced
environment.
 Contribute to all phases of the software development lifecycle, from requirement
gathering to deployment.
 Work closely with cross-functional teams, including QA, Product Management, and
DevOps, to deliver high-quality products.
 Continuously improve your coding skills and stay updated with emerging technologies.
What You&#39;ll Bring:
 A Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related
field (or final year student with a confirmed graduation timeline).
 Strong understanding of programming fundamentals, object-oriented design,
and data structures.
 Familiarity with at least one programming language such as Java, Python, C++,
or JavaScript.
 Basic knowledge of databases, web technologies, and software development
methodologies (Agile/Scrum preferred).
 Enthusiasm to learn new technologies and a passion for building software
solutions.
 Good problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
 Excellent communication and teamwork skills.
 A positive attitude, curiosity, and willingness to take initiative.
Why Join Us?
 Work on impactful projects with cutting-edge technology.
 Be part of a collaborative and innovative team.
 Opportunity to grow your technical expertise and career in a product-driven company.
If you’re excited about designing and building modern systems and want to work in a
fast-paced, engineering-focused environment, apply now!
What We Offer:
● Enjoy flexible paid time off options for enhanced work-life balance.
● Comprehensive health insurance tailored for you.
● Employee assistance programs cover mental health, legal, financial, wellness, and
behaviour areas to ensure your overall well-being.
● Complimentary CalmApp subscription for you and your loved ones, because mental
wellness matters.
● Energetic work environment with a hybrid work style, providing the balance you need.
● Benefit from our family leave program, which grows with your tenure at Meltwater.
● Thrive within our inclusive community and seize ongoing professional development
opportunities to elevate your career.
