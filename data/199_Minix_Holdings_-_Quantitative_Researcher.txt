DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Job Profile – Quantitative Researcher
About Us
Minix Fintech Pvt. Ltd. (A Bilakhia Group of Companies) is an innovative and fast-growing Quantitative Trading firm at the forefront of quantitative finance. With a strong focus on AI- driven quantitative trading strategies, we are building a next-generation trading powerhouse that seamlessly integrates cutting-edge technology, advanced research, and algorithmic precision. Our mission is to harness the power of artificial intelligence and ultra-low-latency systems to navigate global financial markets with speed, efficiency, and unparalleled accuracy.


Know more about us:


Bilakhia Group - https://www.bilakhiaholdings.com/
Minix FinTech - https://www.minix.one/


Role Overview
As a Quantitative Researcher, you will be responsible for developing and implementing mathematical models and algorithms to drive our trading strategies. You will work closely with traders, engineers, and other researchers to analyze data, optimize performance, and uncover new opportunities in the financial markets.


Key Responsibilities:
• Develop and implement advanced mathematical models and algorithms for high- frequency trading strategies.
• Analyze large datasets to identify patterns, trends, and potential trading opportunities.
• Collaborate with traders and engineers to integrate models into our trading systems.
• Conduct rigorous backtesting and performance analysis of trading strategies.
• Continuously research and apply new mathematical techniques and theories to enhance trading strategies.
• Monitor and analyze real-time market data to adapt strategies and optimize performance.
• Communicate complex mathematical concepts and findings to a non-technical audience.


Qualifications:
• PhD in Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, Physics, or a related field.
• Strong background in probability, statistics, stochastic processes, and numerical methods.
• Understanding of programming languages such as Python, C++, or R is a plus.
• Experience with data analysis, machine learning, and statistical modeling.
• Excellent problem-solving skills and ability to think critically and creatively.
• Strong communication skills and ability to work collaboratively in a team environment.
• Prior experience in finance or high-frequency trading is a plus, but not required.


Needful Qualities:
• Well-organized, detail-oriented; able to multi-task and keep track of various deadlines in a fast-paced, high-demand environment.
• Highest level of integrity, empathy, and character.
• Mature and thoughtful, with the ability to operate in a collaborative, team-oriented culture.


Why Join Us?
• Be part of a fast-growing, innovative firm at the forefront of financial technology.
• Work on challenging, high-impact projects with opportunities for rapid learning and career growth.
• Competitive compensation package with performance-based incentives.
• A dynamic work environment with exposure to cutting-edge technologies in trading.
