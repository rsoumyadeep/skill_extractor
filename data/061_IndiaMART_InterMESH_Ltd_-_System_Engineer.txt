About the role
We are looking for motivated System Engineers to work on scalable
systems, backend platforms, and cloud-native services that support
millions of users.
The role offers exposure to modern system design, DevOps practices,
and AI-enabled solutions, solving real-world business problems on a
scale.
You'll Learn and Contribute To:
● Participate in all phases of the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC)
● Design, develop, test, deploy, and maintain backend and system components
● Collaborate with cross-functional teams (Product, QA, DevOps, Business)
● Monitor, troubleshoot, and improve system performance and reliability
● Contribute to automation, scalability, and security initiatives
● Support and integrate AI-driven features and intelligent workflows
● Follow engineering best practices including code reviews and documentation
Key Technical & Functional Skills:
● Strong programming fundamentals (Python / Java / Go / C++)
● Solid understanding of Data Structures, Algorithms, and OOP concepts
● Good understanding of Linux/Unix systems
● Knowledge of Databases (SQL / NoSQL)
● Awareness of AI / Machine Learning fundamentals
● Comfort with ambiguity and fast-paced learning
