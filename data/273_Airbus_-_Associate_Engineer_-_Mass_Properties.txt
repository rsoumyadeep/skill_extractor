DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Qualification: M.Tech in Aerospace / Mechanical Engineering


Job scope:
To support the design and development of Overall Aircraft level target weights and to track aircraft weight and CG evolution throughout the development phase.
Develop mass models for loads calculation, inertia models for aircraft simulation for different configurations and support special requests
Perform weight and CG estimations to support aircraft sales campaigns
Drive the designers, risk sharing partners of Airbus and vendors for the design with respect to weight targets.
Prepare component weight and CG data for Weight review meetings and design progress meetings.
Support Aircraft delivery weighing inspection, calculating delivery aircraft weight and supporting technical reports for weight and balance manual.
Calculate weight and CG impact for Customer modifications, Service bulletins, Weight repercussions & Technical repercussions
Alert Weight and Balance (W&B) Specialist on any important discrepancy between Estimated, Calculated, and Weighed values and verify that corrections are applied.
Above all to drive innovation and bring new technology adaptation to aircraft design.

Required Domain Expertise (skills):
Good logical reasoning, analytical skills,( Data Analytics preferable)
Basic understanding of Aircraft design and sub-systems.


Tools: Catia V5, PDM systems, Programming skills (PYTHON or MATLAB preferable)


Soft skills:
Agile and reliable, interested in working in a transnational empowered team
Ability to communicate effectively (oral, writing & presentation)
Flexibility & adaptability
Ability to accept responsibility of task
Capacity to innovate & make proposal for improvements
