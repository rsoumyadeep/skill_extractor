DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Job Summary
To conduct exploratory approach for Synthetic route design, route screening and evaluation. Fair knowledge to adopt the Process chemistry principle for the identified route in small scale synthesis. This role is aimed at developing Novel route and collaborate with partners to develop the process for NCE
Roles & Responsibilities
1. Literature Search
• Propose retrosynthetic approach and alternate routes through literature searches to enable the synthetic route design
 2. Feasibility Study and Process Optimization
• Conduct laboratory experiments while ensuring safety and quality.
• Phase appropriate development for NCE compounds.
• Troubleshoot at lab levels, including impurities investigation, and contribute to updates and reports
• Expertise in interpreting analytical techniques comprising NMR, LC-MS,HR-MS, HPLC,Thermal analysis etc
• Provide scientific and logical conclusions to lab experiment. Provide mass balance/kinetics postulation of the executed experiments.
3. Documentation
• Record all experimental data in the ELN/Laboratory Record Book as per guidelines.
• Compile reports, maintain laboratory records, and organize project-related information.
• Provide data to the Lab Manager for preparing technical reports and other project-related documentation, including impurity profile compilations lab experimental batch data analysis and developmental reports.
5. Laboratory Maintenance and Compliance
• Maintain laboratory equipment, glassware, and chemicals as per compatibility requirements.
• Archive project-related samples.
• Ensure adherence to safety, cGLP, ISMS, and quality guidelines by following all relevant SOPs.
• Comply with audit requirements.
Competencies and Skills
• Strong knowledge of chemical process development and retrosynthetic route design.
• Understanding of reaction mechanism/pathways, data backed logic postulating impurities structures and logical conclusion of experiments.
• Proactive planning of parallel experiments with scientific rational.
• Proficiency in technical documentation and reporting. (Weekly technical updates, monthly reports, campaign summary, productivity matrix)
• Ability to adapt and evolve.
• Interface with customer on project scientific related discussions
• Ability to collaborate effectively with collaborators and functional teams.
• Familiarity with cGLP,ISMS, and regulatory standards.
