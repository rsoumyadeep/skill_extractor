JOB DESCRIPTION – MANAGER – OPERATIONS
(For company HR records, hiring, and role clarity)
Location Bangalore
Division Corporate
Group (Office/Factory) Office
Department Operations
Sub- Function
1. Job Summary
responsible for driving operational efficiency, process standardization, governance, and performance optimization across functions. The role focuses on aligning operational processes with business objectives, improving productivity, ensuring compliance readiness, and supporting leadership through structured reviews, MIS, and execution of critical operational initiatives. The position plays a key role in strengthening operational discipline, cross-functional coordination, and continuous improvement across the organization.
1.1 Reports to: AGM / AVP / Head – Operations
1.2 Direct Reportees: Operational executives, coordinators, or project-based team members
1.3 Working Relationships
Internal: Production, Quality, Maintenance, Supply Chain, Planning, HR, Finance, IT/SAP, Safety, IAD, Admin External: Vendors, Contractors, Auditors, Consultants, Service Providers
   2. Key Responsibilities
• Drive operational excellence initiatives to improve productivity, efficiency, and cost optimization.
• Ensure standardization and implementation of SOPs, work instructions, and operational guidelines across functions.
• Monitor and govern end-to-end operational workflows to ensure adherence to defined processes.
• Coordinate with cross-functional teams to resolve operational bottlenecks and ensure smooth execution.
• Establish and track KPIs, SLAs, and performance metrics aligned with business objectives.
• Lead periodic operational reviews, MIS reporting, and dashboard presentations for leadership.
• Support leadership-driven operational and strategic initiatives with structured execution plans.
• Ensure audit readiness by maintaining documentation, compliance records, and process evidence.
• Drive continuous improvement initiatives using root cause analysis and corrective action frameworks.
• Coordinate with IT/SAP teams for operational system enhancements and requirement validations.
• Oversee adherence to safety, quality, and statutory compliance requirements in operations.
• Support capacity planning, manpower optimization, and resource utilization initiatives.
• Facilitate change management and adoption of new processes or systems across operations.
• Act as a governance anchor for operational projects, ensuring timelines, quality, and zero escalations.
3. Required Qualifications
• MBA / BE / BTech / Diploma (Operations, Industrial Engineering, Mechanical, or equivalent)
• 6–10 years of experience in Operations Management, Manufacturing Operations, or Business Operations
