DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Vervesemi Microelectronics requires Digital IC Design Engineer. The requirement involves architecture exploration, signal processing blocks design such as Digital filters, digital blocks such as SPI interfaces, high speed digtal design and digital for full product. The job involves MATLAB, RTL, Synthesis, verifications and complete digital ASIC flow including P&R and to work in the complete project cycle until tape-out.
