DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Qualification: M.Tech in Aerospace / Mechanical / Civil Engineering

Translate capability needs of Flight Physics into method and tool specifications.
Design, develop, and validate Flight Physics methods and tools.
Ensure methods development for Flight physics and the multidisciplinary integration of these capabilities to meet technical and user requirements.

Your main tasks and responsibilities:
Analyze the business context, design and document the solution, present that solution to stakeholders and support the project team with its implementation.
Ensure strategic alignment of software development activities in terms of process management, methodology, rules and tools (tool chain).
Conduct feasibility studies in complex, heterogeneous project environments
Work closely in a cross functional team, collaborate and foster team spirit

Your boarding pass:
Analytical, creative, and open to learn new technologies
Experience in Behavioural Modelling & Simulation and familiarity with requirements specifications
Experience with Geometric modelling, Analytical Geometry, Linear Algebra
Experience with automation of workflows
Previous experience with Python
Ability to learn fast, comfortable working in full autonomy
Fluent in English

Nice to have:
Previous experience with languages, such as C++, Java (eclipse RCP), Javascript, etc.
SAFe, Agile ways of working
Experience with high performance computing, remote desktops and job batching
Experience with Cloud-based programming
programming on CATIA or working with geometries
