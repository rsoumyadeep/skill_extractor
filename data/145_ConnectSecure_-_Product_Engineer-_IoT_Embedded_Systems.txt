DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Internship/Full Time Opportunity 
Location:Bangalore
We are looking for candidates who will be available for a full-time internship at our Bangalore office from January to June 2026. 
Upon successful completion of the internship, candidates will transition to full-time employment with us after graduation. 


Domain- IOT
Key Responsibilities
• Design and develop embedded IoT systems for automation and monitoring.
• Build, test, and deploy Arduino-based prototypes for industrial use cases.
• Develop firmware using C/C++ for microcontrollers and sensor-based systems.
• Integrate communication modules such as LoRa, BLE, and Wi-Fi.
• Collaborate with hardware, software, and testing teams to ensure reliable performance.
• Support continuous improvements and field validation for deployed systems


Required Skills
• Strong experience in C/C++ development for embedded environments.
• Hands-on experience with Arduino IDE and related development tools.
• Familiarity with LoRa, BLE, and Wi-Fi communication protocols.
• Knowledge of IoT architectures, data collection, and edge communication.
• Solid understanding of hardware–software integration and sensor interfaces.
• Ready to travel to RO or site locations based on business need.
