DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Job Title
Research Scientist / Data Scientist (PhD – Data Science, AI & Advanced Analytics)
________________________________________
Role Purpose
We are seeking a highly motivated PhD-level Data Scientist / Research Scientist to drive advanced analytics, machine learning, and data-driven research initiatives. The role focuses on translating complex data and research outcomes into scalable, real-world solutions that enable business and scientific impact.
________________________________________
Key Responsibilities
• Lead applied research using advanced statistical, ML, and AI techniques
• Design, develop, and validate predictive, prescriptive, and optimization models
• Analyze large, complex, structured and unstructured datasets
• Translate research findings into production-ready models and insights
• Collaborate with cross-functional teams (Product, Engineering, Business, R&D)
• Publish or present research outcomes (internal / external, where applicable)
• Stay current with emerging trends in AI, ML, GenAI, and data science research
________________________________________
Required Qualifications
• PhD in Data Science, Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Engineering, or related field
• Strong foundation in machine learning, statistical modeling, and data analysis
• Hands-on experience with Python/R and ML libraries (e.g., scikit-learn, PyTorch, TensorFlow)
• Experience working with large-scale datasets and cloud-based data platforms
• Ability to clearly communicate complex concepts to non-technical stakeholders
________________________________________
Preferred Qualifications
• Postdoctoral or industry research experience
• Experience with Deep Learning, NLP, Computer Vision, or Generative AI
• Exposure to MLOps, model deployment, and scalable architectures
• Publications in reputed journals or conferences
• Experience in regulated or research-intensive industries (Life Sciences, Healthcare, Finance, etc.)
________________________________________
Key Skills & Competencies
• Advanced analytical and problem-solving skills
• Strong research mindset with business orientation
• Collaboration and stakeholder management
• Scientific rigor combined with practical execution
• Curiosity, learning agility, and innovation mindset
