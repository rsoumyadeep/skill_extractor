DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
JD for ASSISTANT PROFESSOR - DSU


Qualifications and Experience Required:
● Ph.D. or M.Tech with First Class or equivalent (in terms of grades, etc.) in the preceding degree, and a good academic record throughout.
● A minimum of 0-2 years teaching/research/professional experience. In exceptional cases, candidates with less than requisite experience will also be considered.
● Candidates should preferably be below 35 years of age.


Key requirements:
1. Good academic record with at least 60% marks (or an equivalent grade in a seven-point scale wherever grading system is followed) at the Master’s Degree level in a relevant subject.
2. Good academic record with a Ph.D. Degree in the relevant disciplines.
3. Curriculum Development
4. Good record in Research and innovation


Knowledge and Skills Required:
1. A keen sense of enquiry to result in innovation with a view to create an impact
2.A teacher-researcher (or vise-versa) with an openness to attain proficiency in the other area
3.A willingness to unlearn and re-learn
4.Faith and commitment to an interdisciplinary approach to learning and teaching
5.Well networked with respected senior academicians in ones area of interest both in India and abroad
6.Experience in and/or keenness towards Institution building activities
7.Excitement about research towards offering composite solutions to the industry
8.Connecting closely with the industry with a view to swiftly adapt syllabus and/or content and in so doing, making our students job ready and distinctly unique viz-a viz their peers
9.Create and deliver globally relevant courses with emphasis on experiential learning
10.Collaborative approach towards learning, teaching, research and functioning.
11.Be instrumental in challenging the way students think, learn and develop their understanding of academic disciplines.
12.Develop core curriculum and deliver course material effectively.
