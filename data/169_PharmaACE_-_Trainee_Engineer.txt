DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
PharmaACE is a fast-growing global Healthcare Consulting, Analytics, and Software product development firm that is revolutionizing the Pharma Lifesciences landscape. We specialize in delivering cutting-edge Generative AI and Agentic AI solutions that empower organizations to make smarter, faster, and more impactful decisions across the pharmaceutical value chain. With a strong and expanding presence in the United States, Canada, Europe, and India, PharmaACE serves adverse range of Pharma clients—from top-tier multinational to agile, innovation-driven life sciences startups. Our mission is to create Data-driven, AI-enabled products and solutions that drive measurable business outcomes and improve patient care globally.


Purpose of Role
We are seeking passionate AI Trainee Engineers (Frontend / Backend) eager to dive deep into AI-driven solution and product development. This role goes far beyond traditional coding — it’s a rare opportunity to work hands-on with real-world Gen AI / Agentic AI systems, exploring advanced LLMs, NLP pipelines, and RAG architectures for solving complex healthcare analytics problems. You will work with senior AI engineers and researchers to develop real-world NLP systems for enterprise use cases tailored to the Pharmaceutical
Industry.


Responsibilities
• Experience in using different open-source libraries Pandas, Tensorflow, PyTorch. Research and experiment with cutting-edge GenAI and Agentic AI models for real-world use cases
• Collaborate in building scalable AI-based applications — from intuitive frontends to efficient backends
• Work on data extraction, NER, text summarization, and semantic search problems using advanced
NLP tools
• Integrate LLMs (OpenAI, Gemini, Cohere, DeepSeek, etc.) into custom applications for Chatbots, Question Answering, and Document Understanding
• Apply prompt engineering, embedding techniques, and RAG strategies for building intelligent AI
agents
• Clean and structure unstructured text data from varied sources like PDFs, PPTs, and web pages using tools like PyMuPDF, PDFMiner, Tesseract, BeautifulSoup, Selenium, etc
• Contribute to the AI infrastructure stack involving vector databases (Pinecone, FAISS, ChromaDB),
transformer models (BERT, T5, RoBERTa), and backend APIs


Required Skills & Technologies
Strong fundamentals in Python and libraries like:
Pandas, Tensorflow, PyTorch, Transformers, Keras, PDFPlumber, PyPDF2, Selenium, BeautifulSoup,
Python-pptx, Tesseract, etc.
Experience/knowledge of:
o NER, Text Cleaning, Summarization, QA, Topic Modeling
o Transformers & Fine-tuning LLMs
o RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation)
o Prompt Engineering for LLMs
o Vector stores: Pinecone, FAISS, ChromaDB


Understanding of frontend/backend development and willingness to work in full-stack setups


Qualifications Required
• Degree: BE/BTech/MTech/MCA in Computer Science, Data Science, or related fields.
• Freshers (0–3 years) from CDAC (DAI, DBDA, DAC) or equivalent AI/ML-focused certifications encouraged to apply
• Strong analytical mindset and willingness to work on real AI problems in the healthcare domain
• Bonus: Exposure to Pharma/Life Sciences/Clinical or RWE data


Why Join PharmaACE?
• Your gateway to the practical world of AI
• Hands-on experience in Python using different embedding techniques
• Experience/Knowledge of Pharma/Life sciences/Healthcare data will be an added advantage
• Be part of a global AI team shaping the next generation of healthcare solutions
• Get mentored by industry experts in LLMs, NLP, and Generative AI
• Work on impactful AI use cases across US & Global Pharma clients
• Build real-world Agentic AI applications — not just PoCs
