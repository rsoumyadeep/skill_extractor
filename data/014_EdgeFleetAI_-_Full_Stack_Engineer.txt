DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
EdgeFleet.AI is Hiring – Full-Stack Engineer
Where intelligent systems meet scalable software.
Build the backbone of next-generation AI products powering vision, language, and real-time intelligence at the edge.
🚀 About EdgeFleet.AI
At EdgeFleet.AI, we are building a unified intelligence layer for autonomous and edge systems — where AI models, real-time data, and user-facing platforms come together seamlessly.
Our products sit at the intersection of Computer Vision, AI infrastructure, and scalable web platforms, enabling machines to not just perceive the world, but to reason, communicate, and act.
🚀 Role Details
Location: New Delhi, India
Type: Full-Time (On-site)
🚀 About the Role
We’re looking for a curious, driven Full-Stack Engineer who enjoys building end-to-end systems — from robust backend services to intuitive frontend interfaces.
This role is ideal for someone who:
Loves owning features from idea deployment→
Enjoys working close to AI/ML systems and real-world data
Values clean code, performance, and scalability
Thrives in a fast-moving, collaborative startup environment
You’ll work closely with AI engineers, product teams, and designers to build platforms that power EdgeFleet’s AI-driven products.🛠️
What You’ll Do
Design, develop, and maintain scalable backend services and REST APIs
Build and integrate frontend interfaces with backend and AI systems
Work closely with AI/ML teams to expose models via APIs and dashboards
Implement real-time and data-driven features for AI-powered applications
Write clean, readable, and maintainable code
Optimize system performance, reliability, and scalability
Debug issues, refactor legacy components, and improve overall code quality
Participate in architecture discussions and technical decision-making
✅ What We’re Looking For
Strong fundamentals in programming, data structures, and problem-solving
Solid understanding of web applications end-to-end
Experience with JavaScript / TypeScript
Hands-on with frontend frameworks (React, Next.js, or similar)
Experience building RESTful APIs and working with asynchronous systems
Familiarity with databases (PostgreSQL, MySQL, MongoDB, etc.)
Working knowledge of Git and collaborative workflows
Comfortable working in fast-paced, evolving environments
Clear communication and a strong team-first mindset
Openness to learning new tools, frameworks, and technologies
🚀 Bonus (Nice to Have)
Exposure to Python-based backends (FastAPI, Flask, Django)
Experience integrating with AI/ML services or inference pipelines
Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure)
Basic understanding of Docker / containerized deployments
 Interest in AI, Computer Vision, or real-time systems
