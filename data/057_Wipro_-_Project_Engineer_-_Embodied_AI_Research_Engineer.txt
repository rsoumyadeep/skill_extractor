DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Job Role: Embodied AI Research Engineer
Position Overview
Wipro Innovation Network labs Robotics & Embodied AI team to work at the intersection of computer vision, sensor fusion, and autonomous robotic systems. As a Robotics Research Engineer you will build and experiment with perception algorithms, multi-sensor pipelines, and visual-language–action (VLA) models to power next-gen autonomous robots.
This role gives you access to robotic simulators, multi-modal datasets, real-world robotic platforms, and mentorship from senior researchers.
Key Responsibilities
• Develop perception algorithms for detection, tracking, segmentation, SLAM, and scene understanding.
• Work with multi-modal sensor data: camera, LiDAR, radar, IMU.
• Build and test perception models in robotic simulators (e.g., Isaac Sim, Gazebo) and real robotic platforms.
• Integrate perception modules with planning, control, and manipulation systems.
• Explore Visual–Language–Action (VLA) models and multi-modal AI for robotics tasks.
• Collaborate with engineers and researchers across AI, hardware, and controls.
• Implement and optimise algorithms in Python / C++, on Linux/Ubuntu environments.
Explore and contribute to experiments in robot autonomy and embodied intelligence.
Required Qualifications & Skills
• Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in CS, Robotics, ECE, EE, or related fields.
• Strong programming skills in Python (C++ is a plus).
• Good understanding of Computer vision fundamentals
• Deep learning basics (PyTorch/TensorFlow)
• SLAM, sensor fusion, or VIO concepts
• Familiarity with Linux/Ubuntu and basic command line workflows.
• Curiosity and foundational exposure to robotics platforms or simulations (course projects acceptable).
• Ability to analyse data, debug perception pipelines, and improve model performance.
• Strong problem-solving skills and willingness to experiment.
Preferred Attributes
• Experience with ROS (Robot Operating System).
• Exposure to robotic simulators (Isaac Sim, Gazebo, Webots).
• Academic or personal projects in computer vision, robotics, or multi-modal AI.
• Participation in robotics clubs, competitions, hackathons (e.g., e-Yantra, RoboSub, DRDO challenges).
• Understanding of LiDAR/IMU data processing.
• Interest in embodied AI, VLA models, and robot autonomy.
