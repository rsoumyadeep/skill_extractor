DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
This position will be based out of Bangalore, directly reporting into the leader of Supplier Quality Manager in India, and closely working with Supplier and Global stakeholders (Supply Chain, Product Group, Manufacturing). This individual would be responsible for supporting strategical projects, continuous improvement (CI) initiatives and meet Project/Quality metrics of Surface Treatment and Coatings commodity (STACC). Individual’s primary job responsibilities are listed below:
• Support Primary SE for assessing STACC commodity suppliers for capability, capacity, quality systems, and compliance with industry standards. Support supplier audits and qualification processes
• Collaborate with suppliers to understand, develop, refine, and optimize Surface treatment and cleaning processes to meet quality, cost, and delivery requirements. Identify opportunities for process improvements and efficiency gains.
• Support Global SE in Providing technical support to suppliers on STACC commodity best practices. Troubleshoot process and product-related issues to ensure timely resolution.
• Support in Maintaining the accurate documentation of supplier qualifications, process specifications, test procedures, and quality records.
• Support commodity projects like regionalization, dual sourcing, new supplier qualifications and obtains CFT approvals thru design reviews. Support on commodity initiatives like NCe reduction programs to improve the supplier quality.
• Collaborate cross functionally and globally to support strategies execution and clearly communicate across engineering specializations and different levels of the organization for strategy implementation.
Mandatory Skills required to perform the job:
• Basic understanding of supplier manufacturing processes, Metallurgy, Different material analysis, etc.
• Basic understanding of Surface treatment and coatings process,
• Basic knowledge on problem solving process and tools
• Excellent communication and Basics of presentation.
• Knowledge on MS office tools (ppt, excel, project),
Desirable Skills:
• Knowledge on Material analysis, understanding of Metal microstructure, Chemical composition
• Knowledge on different Surface treatment and coatings processes,
• Knowledge on manufacturing advance materials like Ceramics, Quartz, Alumina, etc.
• Basics of 8D and Project Management skills
