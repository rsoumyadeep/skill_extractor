DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Reliance is building the world's most comprehensive, integrated, and future-ready New Energy ecosystem to drive India's journey towards energy independence and global sustainability leadership. As part of our strategic commitment, Reliance has pledged to achieve Net-Zero Carbon status by 2035 — one of the most ambitious climate goals globally.
We aim to enable India’s shift from an energy-importing nation to a global clean-energy powerhouse and empower MSMEs, startups, academia and industry partners to participate in the green-tech ecosystem. Our New Energy Giga Complex spans Navi Mumbai, Jamnagar, Dahej, Kutch and Bengaluru with full-stack engineering, commissioning, R&D, and operations capability.
Work Locations (Full-time office): Navi Mumbai, Jamnagar, Dahej, Kutch, Bengaluru and PAN-India project sites.
Job Role
Manager – Team Member (Electrical/Electronics) Engineering
Project Execution | Technology Deployment | New Energy Manufacturing
Role Summary
Contribute to engineering, commissioning, scale‑up, and optimization of cutting‑edge New Energy facilities including Solar PV, Hydrogen systems, Electrolysers, Energy Storage, Power Electronics, and Advanced Materials. Work in multi‑disciplinary environments supporting commissioning, operations, safety, quality, and technology deployment.
Key Responsibilities
• Power systems engineering, substation and switchgear work
• Instrumentation & control logic, PLC/DCS/SCADA commissioning
• Electrical safety studies, grounding, load flow
• Battery, solar inverter, hydrogen plant electrical systems
Skills & Knowledge
• Power distribution, protection systems
• Automation tools, Digital control, sensor systems
• Exposure to power electronics preferred
