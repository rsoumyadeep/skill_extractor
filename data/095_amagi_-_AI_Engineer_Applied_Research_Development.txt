DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
About the team: From your first day at Amagi, you will meet great technical minds, work on the most
interesting problems in the video domain. We are India’s number one SaaS company . We offer you the
opportunity to work on cutting edge technologies that shape the way viewers watch streaming TV.
Job Profile: We are looking for an ML Engineers to work on applied R&D problems across vision, NLP,
and agentic AI systems. This role is hands-on and deeply technical—you will design, implement, train, and
iterate on models from first principles, and translate research ideas into robust, working systems. You will
work closely with senior researchers and engineers on real-world, high-impact AI systems rather than toy
problems or narrow benchmarks.
Work Mode: In Office
Location: Bengaluru (Bannerghatta Road)


Key Responsibilities
● Design and implement neural architectures from scratch for vision, NLP, or multimodal
problems
● Train, evaluate, and iterate on deep learning models using PyTorch
● Build agentic systems that combine reasoning, planning, memory, and tool use
● Develop advanced LLM-based systems, including:
○ Sophisticated context engineering
○ Prompt strategies beyond basic prompting (multi-stage, self-reflective, tool-augmented
flows)
● Work extensively in Jupyter notebooks for experimentation, analysis, and rapid prototyping
● Conduct experiments, analyze failure modes, and improve models systematically
● Read and implement ideas from recent research papers when needed
● Collaborate with product and systems teams to move from prototype to usable systems
Required Qualifications
● Fresher/ 2–3 years of industry experience working in one or more of:Computer Vision
(image / video)
● NLP / Language Models
● Multimodal or agentic AI systems
● Strong experience building models from scratch, not just using pre-trained APIs
● Deep hands-on expertise in:
○ Python
○ PyTorch
○ Jupyter notebooks
● Solid understanding of:Neural network architectures
● Training dynamics, optimization, and eval\ation
● Practical experience with LLMs beyond basic usage
● Strong problem-solving ability and comfort with ambiguity
● Undergraduate/ Post Graduate from B.Tech/M.Tech/PhD Research in Computer Science
(or closely related field) from a top-tier institution


Good to Have
● Experience with video understanding or temporal models
● Sufficient background to read, comprehend, and apply ideas from latest research papers in
the AI/ML domain
● Familiarity with research workflows and ablation-driven experimentation
● Prior work on productionizing research prototypes
● Comfort working in fast-moving, research-heavy environments




What We Value
● First-principles thinking over library-driven development
● Depth of understanding over surface-level familiarity
● Engineers who can reason about models, not just run them
● Curiosity, rigor, and ownership of complex problems
Benefits: Our benefits are designed to support not just your role, but your life beyond it. From
comprehensive insurance coverage and inclusive leave policies to wellness programs that focus
on mental, physical, and financial well-being, Employees also enjoy on-site meals at the office,
creating a more comfortable and energising workday. We aim to create an environment that
enables you to thrive.
Amagi Culture: At Amagi, our culture is defined by the "Amagi Way"—a high-performance
environment where Goodness Wins and collective success takes precedence over individual
ego. We are a team of independent thinkers who think Macro, Do The Micro, combining vision
with a relentless obsession for the technical details that power the new video economy. We
believe that a lean group of elite talent achieves more than massive, fragmented teams, and we
foster this by encouraging everyone to Be Vulnerable, Be You—bringing your authentic self to
a space built on trust and transparency. We move fast, act pragmatically, and Have Fun with
Challenges, ensuring that every innovative solution we dream up is built, delivered, and
impactful. Joining Amagi means embracing the freedom to innovate while being part of a global
mission rooted in integrity and transformative purpose.
