DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
JOB Description - AI & Firmware Engineer
Embedded Intelligence, Edge AI & Cyber-Physical Systems
Leumas · Bengaluru
Leumas is seeking an AI & Firmware Engineer in Residence to design, develop, and deploy intelligent control and monitoring systems for autonomous manufacturing platforms. This role sits at the intersection of embedded firmware, edge intelligence, and system integration. The position is responsible for translating AI/ML models into reliable, real-time, production-grade behaviour on constrained hardware, while maintaining engineering rigor and operational stability.


About Leumas
Leumas is a Bangalore-based deep-tech manufacturing company building a software-defined production platform for healthcare manufacturing. Its proprietary, full-stack Manufacturing Operating System orchestrates the entire manufacturing lifecycle - from formulation logic and material flow to production execution, quality control, and traceability - enabling modular, reconfigurable factories that scale with demand.
Leumas addresses structural challenges in global supply chains, rigidity, long lead times, and capital intensive, step function scale - through a modular, distributed manufacturing architecture that enables faster deployment, higher asset utilization, and materially improved business outcomes across product life cycle Leumas supports two primary deployment models. For enterprise customers, it enables dedicated manufacturing suites that provide high levels of supply-chain control, compliance, and flexibility without the burden of traditional greenfield capex or non-resilient supply chains from traditional CDMOs. For healthcare innovators, the platform allows rapid configuration of production lines through software, enabling faster product launches, tighter quality control, and responsive scale-up or scale down.
Leumas’ production systems currently support more than a dozen finished formulations and consumer health formats, including semi-solids (ointments, gels, lotions), oral solid dosage forms (tablets, capsules), and liquid formulations, with integrated QA and inspection capabilities.


The Role
As AI & Firmware Engineer in Residence, you will own the embedded firmware foundation and edge intelligence layer of Leumas’ manufacturing systems.
You will be responsible for building real-time firmware, integrating AI/ML models with physical systems, and ensuring reliable closed-loop operation across sensing, control, and optimization workflows.
This role requires strong technical depth, hands-on execution capability, and the ability to operate across firmware, data, and systems integration layers.


What You Will Own
As AI & Firmware Engineer in Residence, you will be accountable for the design, deployment, and continuous improvement of intelligent embedded systems.
1. Embedded Firmware Development
• Design and develop firmware using C/C++, RTOS, and microcontroller platforms.
• Implement device drivers, hardware abstraction layers, and system services.
• Ensure real-time performance, stability, and fault tolerance.
2. Edge AI & Model Integration
• Integrate AI/ML models into embedded and edge devices.
• Optimize models for constrained hardware environments.
• Implement monitoring, fallback, and fail-safe mechanisms.
3. Sensor Data & Real-Time Processing Pipelines
• Design and maintain sensor data pipelines for real-time acquisition and processing.
• Enable deterministic communication between sensing, inference, and control layers.
• Support closed-loop system validation and tuning.
4. AI-Driven Control & Optimization Systems
• Develop AI-based control, vision, anomaly detection, and optimization modules.
• Deploy intelligent monitoring and diagnostic frameworks.
• Support commissioning and stabilization of autonomous functions.
5. System Integration & Engineering Governance
• Work across firmware, edge intelligence, and system integration layers.
• Maintain structured documentation, validation records, and release packages.
• Participate in design, safety, and technical reviews.
