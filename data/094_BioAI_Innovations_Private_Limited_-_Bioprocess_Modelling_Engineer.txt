DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
About Lemnisca
India is at an inflection point in manufacturing and sustainability. Over the next decade, a large share of global production will shift towards cleaner, local, and more resilient supply chains. Biomanufacturing will be a core pillar of that shift.
Lemnisca is building the AI-first backbone for this transition – starting with precision fermentation. We combine biology, chemical engineering and data/AI to make fermentation processes:
• Faster to develop
• Smoother to scale
• More predictable across sites and scales
Our goal is to enable companies in India and globally to manufacture high-value molecules from renewable feedstocks, reliably and competitively. From personal care to food and health, we want everyday products to be made more sustainably – with India not just participating, but leading.
As part of the early team, you will help turn this vision into operating reality: real processes, real customers, real plants.


Mission for this role
The Bioprocess Modeling lead will develop Lemnisca’s bioprocess modeling capability, focused on supporting fermentation and downstream process development across the lab-to-industrial continuum. The mission of this role is to build robust computational models that help bridge experimental results and reliable process operation.
This is a founding leadership role with significant influence on technical direction. You will work closely with the Bioengineering team to establish effective practices for integrating experimental data and computational models, ensuring that modeling outputs directly inform process development and decision-making.


What you will do
• Develop and maintain bioprocess models that support fermentation and downstream process development across laboratory and early industrial settings.
• Work closely with the Bioengineering team to define modeling questions, design informative experiments, and interpret experimental data through a modeling lens.
• Translate experimental and process data into predictive insights that guide development decisions and reduce uncertainty during process scale-up.
• Establish standards for model development, validation, documentation, and reuse within the company.
• Build and maintain data workflows that connect experimental results with computational analysis in a reliable and traceable manner.


What you will get
You will help build an IP-led Indian company aiming to make biomanufacturing the default way materials are produced, with your work directly shaping how processes are developed and operated.
You will be part of the leadership team, influencing technical direction, modeling strategy, and how computational tools are used across the company.
As a founding member, you will own the development of Lemnisca’s bioprocess modeling capability end-to-end, with the autonomy to define standards, tools, and ways of working as the platform evolves.
