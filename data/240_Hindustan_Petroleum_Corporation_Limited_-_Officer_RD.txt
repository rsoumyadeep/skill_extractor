DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
• Undertake and execute specific research projects leading to development of new
 technologies / products and their commercialization.
 • Carry out experiments and operate the state-of-the-art lab equipment and pilot plants, as per
 safety practices and protocols.
 • Maintain the lab equipment, facilities and ensure reliability through health monitoring and
 periodic maintenance, calibration.
 • Procurement, installation and commissioning of new equipment / lab facilities as per
 requirement.
 • Provide advanced technical support and troubleshooting services to the operating plants at
 different HPCL sites.
 • Coordinate research activities with internal teams and external collaborators
