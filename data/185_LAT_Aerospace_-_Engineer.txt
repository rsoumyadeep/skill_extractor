DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
LAT Aerospace | Graduate Engineers
Location: Gurgaon (on-site test locations may apply) 
Type: Full-time | Entry-level


We’re hiring engineers who build first, ask later.


At LAT, we’re not just building an aircraft – we’re building a culture. One where the best ideas win, where speed matters more than perfection, and where engineers don’t wait for instructions—they chase breakthroughs. We’re developing a new kind of short takeoff and landing (STOL) aircraft with a hybrid-electric powertrain. To do that, we’re putting together one of the most hands-on, high-agency engineering teams in the country. If you’ve ever debugged a motor controller at 2 AM or reverse-engineered a problem no textbook could solve, you’ll fit right in.


Choose Your Track:


The Airshapers
- Shape aerodynamic performance, stability, and control 
- Build aero databases, validate CFD, wind tunnel, and flight test data
Thrustsmiths
- Design and model electric/hybrid propulsion systems 
- Integrate motors, batteries, cooling, and thermal protection
Signal Crew 
- Develop flight computers, data buses, and sensor networks 
- Build and test embedded avionics hardware
Autopilots 
- Design guidance laws and autopilot loops 
- Build estimators, filters, and sensor fusion pipelines
Firmware Forgers 
- Write low-level code for critical systems 
- Interface with sensors, actuators, and real-time controls
Loadbearers 
- Run FEA and stress analysis for airframe components 
- Design and validate composite structures
Builders' Guild 
- Develop tooling, jigs, and fast prototyping processes 
- Select and test aerospace-grade materials


Who you are 
- You don’t wait for permission to start solving problems 
- You’ve built more than simulations: rigs, drones, bikes, rockets 
- You can dive deep in one domain but jump across others when needed 
- You thrive in chaos and learn fast when things break


Bonus if you have 
- Built in student tech teams: UAV, FSAE, Rocketry, Hyperloop, Satellite, etc. 
- Tinkering experience: 3D printing, soldering, machining, hacking 
- Projects in Python, Simulink, MATLAB, C/C++, CAD, FEA, RTOS Self-initiated builds, open-source contributions, or sub-team leadership


Why LAT?
- Core team opportunity at a high-conviction deep-tech startup
- Real aircraft, real flying hardware, real impact
- Build the future of sustainable aviation in India
