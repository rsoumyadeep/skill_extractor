DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Responsibilities:
• Research and experiment with state-of-the-art AI/LLM models (OpenAI, Gemini, Cohere,
DeepSeek, Claude etc.).
• Build prototypes for NLP tasks such as NER, summarization, semantic search, and document
understanding.
• Explore transformer models (BERT, RoBERTa, T5, LLaMA) and conduct comparative
evaluations.
• Work on RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) pipelines, embeddings, and prompt
engineering.
• Clean and process unstructured text from PDFs, PPTs, and webpages using PyMuPDF,
PDFMiner, Tesseract, BeautifulSoup, Selenium, etc.
• Collaborate with senior AI researchers to convert research ideas into working demos and
applications.
• Contribute to building AI evaluation datasets, benchmarks, and analytics framework
