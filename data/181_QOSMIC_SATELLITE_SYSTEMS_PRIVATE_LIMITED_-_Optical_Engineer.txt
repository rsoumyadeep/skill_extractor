DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Join us in developing cutting-edge optical communication systems for space applications. You will work hands-on with lasers, telescopes, detectors, and precision alignment systems across various projects, including optical ground stations, satellite terminals, and emerging optical communication technologies. Your work will bridge laboratory prototypes and field-deployable hardware for terrestrial and space-based platforms.

---

ADDITIONAL DETAILS
What You'll Do


• Build, test and deploy free-space optical links (targeting 1-20 km ranges)
• Handle lasers, beam expanders, telescopes, filters and detectors with safe and precise procedures
• Perform microradian-level optical alignments for long-distance communication links
• Integrate and interface different optical subsystems
• Assemble and integrate optical systems with electronics and mechatronics teams
• Carry out tests and experiments to validate the performance of optical systems
• Support field experiments, PAT (Pointing, Acquisition & Tracking) alignment and on-site troubleshooting
• Characterise lasers, photodetectors, and complete link performance
• Document test procedures, experimental results and system specifications
