DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
The Ola Group is a dynamic conglomerate comprising ANI Technologies, Ola Electric, and Krutrim, each contributing to our mission of redefining mobility, advancing sustainable transportation, and pioneering AI innovation.


At ANI Technologies, we operate Ola Cabs, India's leading ride-hailing platform, serving over 250 million customers across 3 continents, offering employees a fast-paced, tech-driven environment where innovation thrives. Ola Electric is at the forefront of the electric vehicle revolution, with our state-of-the-art Futurefactory in Tamil Nadu, producing over 5 million electric two-wheelers annually and creating thousands of jobs. Krutrim, our AI arm, is dedicated to developing cutting-edge artificial intelligence models and infrastructure, empowering India to lead in the digital age.


Job Summary:
The Model-Based Developer role involves designing and developing control algorithms for EV powertrain, battery management systems (BMS), Charger, Vehicle control system, ABS and its dynamics using MATLAB/Simulink, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to implement high-performance, production-ready embedded software solutions.


Key Result Areas:
Control Algorithm Development: Design and implement model-based control algorithms for EV powertrain, Charger, and Vehicle control systems.
Model-Based Design (MBD): Develop MATLAB/Simulink/Stateflow models for embedded control applications.
Auto Code Generation & Optimization: Generate optimized Embedded C code using Embedded Coder for real-time execution.
Software Integration: Integrated model-based control strategies into vehicle ECU embedded software for production deployment.
Calibration & Parameter Tuning: Develop models with scalability and control parameters tunability for real-world vehicle applications.
Compliance & Standards: Ensure AUTOSAR, ISO 26262, and functional safety compliance in software development.
Documentation & Reviews: Maintain detailed documentation of control models, software architecture, and implementation strategies.


Competency Requirements:
B.Tech/M.Tech in Electrical, Electronics, Mechatronics, or Automotive Engineering.
MATLAB, Simulink, Stateflow – strong expertise in control model development.
Embedded Coder – auto-code generation and model optimization.
Experience in Model-in-loop, Hardware-in-loop, and Python scripting.
Knowledge of control systems and power electronics is essential.
Understanding of the vehicle electrical architecture and behaviour of electrical components in EV.
Good to have knowledge/experience in C/C++ programming, and MISRA C guidelines.
Good to have knowledge about different automotive systems (ECU software) and communication protocols (CAN, LIN, ETHERNET etc).


Perks of working with Ola:
Build technology that impacts millions at massive scale.
Work with top engineers solving complex, high-performance problems.
Exposure to cutting-edge systems across mobility, EV, and AI businesses.
High ownership and visibility with leadership.
Fast-track career growth in a high-velocity engineering environment.
