DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
 Designing and developing mechanical systems and components for various projects. 
 Conducting research and analysis to determine the feasibility, performance, and cost-effectiveness of mechanical designs. 
 Creating detailed engineering drawings, specifications, and documentation for manufacturing and assembly. 
 Collaborating with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless integration of mechanical systems with other disciplines. 
 Performing calculations, simulations, and tests to validate and optimize mechanical designs. 
 Troubleshooting and resolving mechanical issues during the development, testing, and production phases. 
 Participating in design reviews and providing technical expertise and recommendations. 
 Ensuring compliance with industry standards, codes, and regulations in all mechanical designs. 
 Managing and coordinating projects, including scheduling, budgeting, and resource allocation. 
 Keeping up-to-date with advancements in mechanical engineering technology and techniques
