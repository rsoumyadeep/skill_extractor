DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Job Summary
We are looking for an experienced Embedded Engineer who will design, develop, and
maintain embedded systems for spacecraft subsystems.

Key Responsibilities
● Design and implement embedded software/firmware for aerospace systems.
● Develop and test real-time operating systems (RTOS) and applications.
● Integrate and interface microcontrollers, actuators, and sensors with flight
systems.
● Conduct hardware-software integration, debugging, and validation.
● Collaborate with GNC, propulsion, and systems engineering teams for system-level
integration.
● Document designs, test cases, and verification results in line with aerospace quality
Processes.
