DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Join us in developing high-speed electronic systems for optical communication platforms. You will design FPGA-based signal processing systems, power electronics, and high-speed digital circuits across various projects. This role involves hands-on work with hardware development, from circuit design and PCB layout to testing and field deployment for terrestrial and space-based applications.
