DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
About the Role
We are looking for enthusiastic freshers or early‑career professionals who are passionate about data and technology. As a Junior Data Engineer / ETL Developer, you will work with experienced engineers to build, test, and maintain data pipelines that power analytics and business insights.
This role offers hands‑on exposure to real‑world data platforms, cloud technologies, and modern data engineering practices.


What You Will Do
Assist in building and maintaining ETL/data pipelines for Data Warehouses and Data Lakes.
Learn to understand business requirements and help convert them into basic data mappings.
Support data extraction, transformation, and loading using industry‑standard tools.
Perform data validation, unit testing, and debugging to ensure data accuracy.
Help monitor and schedule data jobs and workflows.
Create and maintain technical documentation (data mapping, design notes, test cases).


Required Qualifications
Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, IT, Data Science, Engineering, or related field.
Strong fundamentals in SQL and databases (Relational or NoSQL).
Basic knowledge of ETL concepts and data warehousing.
Exposure to Python through academics, projects, or internships.
Familiarity with Git or any version control system.
Good analytical and problem‑solving skills.
Strong communication skills and a willingness to learn.
Team player with a positive attitude and strong work ethic.
Academic or project exposure to Data Warehousing / Data Lakes.
Awareness of Cloud platforms (Azure preferred).
Exposure to tools like Azure Data Factory, Databricks, Spark, Synapse.
Basic knowledge of Big Data concepts (Spark, Hadoop, Hive, Kafka).
Internship, capstone project, or hackathon experience in data or analytics.
Collaborate with developers, testers, and business teams in an Agile environment.
Continuously learn and apply best practices in data quality, performance, and scalability.


What You’ll Gain
Hands‑on training in enterprise‑scale data engineering.
Mentorship from experienced data professionals.
Exposure to cloud and modern data platforms.
Clear career growth path in Data Engineering & Analytics.
Opportunity to work in a collaborative, global team.
