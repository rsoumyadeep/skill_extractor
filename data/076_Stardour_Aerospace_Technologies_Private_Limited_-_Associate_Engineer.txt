DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Design and develop mechanical structures, mounts, and mechanisms for precision optical telescopes. Work closely with optical and controls engineers to turn optical designs into stable, buildable hardware. This is a hands-on role with real ownership from CAD to assembly and testing.


Key Responsibilities:
- Design opto-mechanical assemblies (mounts, trusses, mirror cells, mechanisms) using CAD tools.
- Perform structural, thermal, and modal analyses (FEA) to ensure stability and performance under gravity, temperature changes, and dynamics.
- Develop drawings and coordinate fabrication and assembly of prototypes.
- Plan and execute alignment procedures and support integration and test activities.
- Collaborate with optical team on tolerances, alignment features, and error budgets.


Preferred:
- Experience with telescope structures, mirror supports, or precision mechanisms.
- Knowledge of STOP analysis (Structural-Thermal-Optical Performance).
- Hands-on experience with prototyping, assembly, or testing of optical hardware.
