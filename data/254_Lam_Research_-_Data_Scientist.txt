DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
As a Data Scientist delving into GenAI-driven solutions at Lam Research, your day-to-day activities will immerse you in the heart of innovation and collaboration.
• Engage with senior Data Scientists and Architects at Lam India to gain insights into real-world business challenges and explore how GenAI technologies can address them.
• Participate in meetings with subject matter experts (SMEs) to understand the technical and practical aspects of proposed solutions.
• Clarify the scope of your assignments with guidance from senior team members, and strategize your contributions based on the current business requirements.
• Assist in drafting functional specification documents that capture business needs and outline solution frameworks.
• Support SMEs with hands-on involvement in the technical development and implementation of GenAI-powered solutions.
• Stay adaptable and open-minded to learning, as you may be tasked with exploring new and evolving use cases in the GenAI landscape.
• Develop your analytical perspective by communicating technical concepts with clarity to both technical and non-technical audiences.
You need to know:
• Basic understanding of Data Science algorithms.
• GenAI technologies.
• Knowledge of LLMs, RAG, Indexing and Vector DBs.
• Good problem solving and analytical/critical thinking skills
• Good oral and written communication skills
• Ability to work on multiple and often competing issues at the same time
• Strong interpersonal skills
• Ability to work independently or with limited direction
