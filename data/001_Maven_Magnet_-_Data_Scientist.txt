DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Job Title: Data Scientist
Location: Mumbai
Type: On-site (Full Time)
We are looking for a creative Data Scientist to join our team! An extremely important role for our success.
As a member of the development team, your responsibilities will include transfer learning and training LLMs. Ideal candidates will have a strong data science background and can help us build/fine-tune LLMs.
You'll have the opportunity to work with large-scale data sets and turn your insights into real products. You'll be an integral part in how we understand user behavior, identify and recommend product opportunities, which are all critical to how we can scale our ever-growing user base.


Responsibilities:
● Fine-tune LLM for Q&A, NLP, multi-labeling, image detection, emotion detection, etc.
● Modifies and analyzes complex data sets using analytics techniques such as machine learning, statistical analysis, Natural Language Processing, Identifies modeling attributes and parameters. Applies algorithms to create descriptive, predictive or prescriptive models to achieve analysis and project deliverables.
● Investigates data characteristics, complexity, quality, and meaning. Through visualizations and summarizations, defines performance, identifies trends, outliers, and drivers.
● Prepares data for analysis, e.g. cleansing, conditioning, transforming, handling missing fields, identifying new feature variables and handling multi-variate data.


Required Skills:
● Experience in fine-tuning and training LLM.
● Experience of training models for Topic Modeling, Multi-labeling data science solutions.
● Experience in relevant programming languages (e.g. Python)
● Experience within the Python Data Science ecosystem - NumPy, SciPy, Jupyter, etc.
● BTech / MTech / MSc in Computer Science, Math, Physics, Engineering, Statistics, or other technical fields. Advanced degrees preferred. Provide a link to a Github that showcases your work.
Bonus Skills:
● Experience building deep neural networks to production.
● Experience in Sage Maker, AWS, Google cloud
● Experience with large volume data processing systems
● Experience with Agile software development
● Good knowledge of Database technologies such as SQL, PL/SQL and relational database schema design


Why join us?
● A team that is invested in your career growth and training
● Competitive salary, based on experience
● Work alongside world-class talent
● Flexible vacation / paid time off policy
● Personal style encouraged
We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for
all employees.
