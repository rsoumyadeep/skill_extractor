DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Role Overview
We are looking for highly motivated M.Tech and Ph.D. graduates with strong fundamentals in Digital Design and Verification to join our ASIC Design Verification team in Bangalore.
As part of this team, you’ll work on verification of next-generation AI compute engines — some of the most complex and high-performance silicon systems being built today.
You’ll collaborate with world-class engineers across RTL design, architecture & emulation to ensure functional correctness, quality, and innovation in every aspect of silicon development.
Shape
Key Responsibilities
Develop SystemVerilog/UVM-based verification environments for complex ASIC/IP blocks and SoCs.
Create test plans, coverage models, and constrained random tests for comprehensive validation.
Work closely with designers and architects to understand microarchitecture and functional intent.
Analyze simulation results, debug issues using waveform tools (e.g., Verdi, DVE), and drive root-cause resolution.
Contribute to automation and scripting to improve regression efficiency and coverage closure.
Participate in code and test plan reviews, contributing to a culture of engineering excellence.
Shape
Required Skills and Background
M.Tech / Ph.D. in Electrical, Electronics, or Computer Engineering.
Strong understanding of digital design fundamentals, computer architecture, and SoC design flow.
Proficiency in Verilog/SystemVerilog, and exposure to UVM is highly desirable.
Solid grasp of testbench architecture, assertions (SVA), and coverage methodology is highly desirable.
Good programming skills in Python, Perl, or Shell scripting for automation.
Excellent analytical and problem-solving ability.
Passion for learning and contributing in a fast-paced, collaborative environment.
Shape
Preferred (Plus Points)
Previous experience in VLSI domain – Design or Verification.
Coursework or project experience in VLSI Design, Verification, or Computer Architecture.
Exposure to high-speed protocols (PCIe, Ethernet, DDR, etc.) or AI/ML accelerators.
Publications, research, or thesis work related to verification methodologies or hardware design.
Shape
Why Join Us
Work on cutting-edge AI hardware used in the world’s fastest AI systems.
Collaborate with some of the brightest minds in the semiconductor industry.
Be part of a fast-growing India team that’s central to Cerebras’ global engineering efforts.
Competitive compensation, learning opportunities, and an exceptional team culture.
