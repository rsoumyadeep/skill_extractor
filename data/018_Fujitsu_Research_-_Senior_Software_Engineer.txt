DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Details:
We are seeking a highly motivated researcher to carry out cutting-edge foundational research at
the intersection of High-Performance Computing (HPC), Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning
(AI/ML) and Scientific Computing, with a clear path to real-world impact through patents,
prototypes and product integration. Our key focus areas at HPC AI MONAKA R&D Lab, FRIPL,
includes the following (but are not limited to):
• Energy Efficient AI models for Generative AI (GenAI), language modelling, and multimodal AI use-cases.
• Advanced compression algorithms for AI and Deep Learning.
• Surrogate modelling using techniques such as model order reduction, Physics-Informed
Neural Networks (e.g., via operator theory).
• Scientific Computation to develop advanced numerical methods for solving linear
system of equations, and deterministic and stochastic differential equations.
• Privacy-preserving computation and Multi-cores Scalability of AI Frameworks.
Main Role:
• Propose and lead the research and development of novel computational models and
algorithms aimed at optimizing performance and scalability while minimizing information
loss (e.g. by leveraging advanced HPC methodologies such as data-level and task-level
parallelism, pipelining, task orchestration, numerical optimization techniques, and data
compression algorithms).
• Design, implement, evaluate and finetune novel AI algorithms and models.
• Drive innovation through patent filings and high-quality publications in leading
conferences/journals.
• Collaborate cross-functionally with other teams to transition research outcomes into
efficient production solutions.
• Mentor junior team members and contribute to building a culture of innovation,
excellence, and continuous learning.
• Stay updated of the latest developments in AI and contribute to the strategic direction of
the research roadmap
Necessary Qualifications:
• PhD or master’s level research degree with expertise in Computational Science/
Computational Physics/ Computational Mathematics, or Computer Science, or
Electronics and Communication Engineering, or any other related field.
• Any prior experience in enterprise R&D and research leadership experience would be
preferred.
Necessary Skillsets:
• Numerical Linear Algebra.
• Numerical methods for discrete and continuous optimization.
• Probability and Stochastic Processes.
• Statistical methods in data analysis.
• Hands-on experience with parallel programming paradigms such as Pthreads, OpenMP,
MPI, vectorization through SIMD/SIMT.
• Familiarity with C++, Python and Latex is assumed.
• Contributions to open-source AI libraries or toolkits.
• Strong collaboration and communication skills
Expertise in some of the following areas is highly desirable:
• Computer architecture and parallel computing.
• Various AI models such as diffusion models, Gen-AI, LLMs, multi-modal AI, State-Space
models, etc.
• Various automation techniques such as control algorithms, reinforcement learning, etc.
• Various data compression algorithms.
• Privacy-preserving confidential computation, and/or Homomorphic Encryption.
• Global and/or stochastic mathematical analysis through foundational concepts
including, but not limited to, stochastic calculus, dynamical systems, and differential
geometry
