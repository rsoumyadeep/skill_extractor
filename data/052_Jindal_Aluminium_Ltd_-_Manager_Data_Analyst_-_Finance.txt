DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
JOB DESCRIPTION – MANAGER – DATA ANALYST-FINANCE
(For company HR records, hiring, and role clarity)
Location Bangalore
Division Corporate
Group (Office/Factory) Office
Department Data Analyst
Sub- Function Finance
1. Job Summary
responsible for driving data-led financial insights, advanced MIS reporting, forecasting, and decision support for senior management. The role focuses on transforming financial data into actionable intelligence, strengthening financial governance, improving cost and revenue visibility, and enabling strategic and operational decision-making. The position acts as a critical analytical partner to Finance leadership and cross-functional stakeholders by ensuring accuracy, consistency, and transparency in financial reporting and analytics.
1.1 Reports to: AVP / AGM – Digital Solutions
1.2 Direct Reportees: Data Analysts / MIS Executives / Project-based analysts
1.3 Working Relationships
Internal: Finance (Accounts, Commercial, Treasury), Sales & Marketing, Exports, Operations, SCM, HR, IT/SAP, Corporate Strategy External: Auditors, Consultants, BI / Analytics Vendors (as required)
   2. Key Responsibilities
• Develop and manage end-to-end financial MIS, dashboards, and management reports for leadership.
• Drive data analytics for revenue, cost, margin, profitability, and working capital performance.
• Support budgeting, forecasting, and variance analysis with data-driven insights.
• Act as SPOC for financial data extraction, validation, and reporting from SAP and other enterprise systems.
• Ensure accuracy, consistency, and integrity of financial data across reports and dashboards.
• Enable decision support for senior management through trend analysis, scenario modeling, and predictive insights.
• Design and standardize financial KPIs, metrics, and reporting frameworks across functions.
• Automate recurring reports and improve reporting efficiency through BI tools and analytics solutions.
• Prepare analytical notes, dashboards, and presentations for Board, MD, and senior leadership reviews.
• Support commercial and business teams with pricing, margin, and profitability analysis.
• Collaborate with IT/SAP teams for data modeling, report development, and system enhancements.
• Ensure audit-ready documentation, data traceability, and compliance alignment in financial reporting.
• Support internal and statutory audits through accurate data analysis and reconciliations.
• Continuously identify opportunities for financial process improvement through analytics and digitization.
3. Required Qualifications
• MBA (Finance) / BE / BTech with strong finance analytics exposure
• 6–10 years of experience in Financial Analytics, MIS, Business Finance, or Data Analytics within a corporate environment
