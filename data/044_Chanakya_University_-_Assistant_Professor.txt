DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Assistant Professor
Summary
The role of an Assistant Professor is to engage in teaching, research, and other academic activities to contribute to the university's academic excellence and knowledge dissemination. They are responsible for nurturing students' learning, conducting research in their field of expertise, and actively participating in university service and development activities.
Responsibilities
Teaching and Curriculum Development:
• Prepare and deliver lectures, tutorials, and practical sessions to undergraduate and/or postgraduate students.
• Designing and developing course curricula that align with the NEP framework, UGC guidelines, and the objectives of the department and university.
• Employing innovative pedagogical methods to engage students actively in the learning process.
• Provide guidance and mentorship to students, encouraging their academic and professional growth.
Research and Scholarly Activities:
• Engaging in rigorous scholarly research and publication in relevant academic areas, demonstrating a commitment to contribute to the advancement of knowledge.
• Pursuing collaborative research projects, both within the department and with other faculty members and institutions, to promote interdisciplinary research and innovation.
• Apply for research grants and funding to support research activities.
Institution Development Activities:
• Participate in departmental and university committees, contributing to academic governance and decision-making processes.
• Engage in community service and outreach activities to promote the university's social impact.
• Promoting the integration of community service and experiential learning opportunities into the academic curriculum.
Skills Required:
• Strong written and verbal communication skills.
• Familiarity with the National Education Policy framework and UGC guidelines.
• Ability to work collaboratively with colleagues and students from diverse backgrounds.
• A passion for teaching, research, and academic pursuits.
• Dedication to maintaining high academic standards and promoting a positive learning environment.
• Commitment to upholding ethical practices in research and teaching.
Reporting Relationship
• Reports to the Dean of the School
