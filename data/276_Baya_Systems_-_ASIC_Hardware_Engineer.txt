DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
As an ASIC Hardware Engineer, you will learn and contribute to the development and
implementation of microarchitectures for complex and configurable IPs. You will gain
practical experience with RTL coding to optimize performance, power, and area. This
role involves collaboration with the verification team to understand test planning and
testbench development, with a specific focus on key chip interconnects and
Network-on-Chip (NoC) designs.
