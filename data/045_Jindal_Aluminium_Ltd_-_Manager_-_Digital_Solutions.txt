DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
JOB DESCRIPTION – MANAGER – DIGITAL SOLUTIONS
(For company HR records, hiring, and role clarity)
Location Bangalore
Division Corporate
Group (Office/Factory) Office
Department Digital Solutions
Sub- Function Digital Solutions
1. Job Summary
Responsible for architecting, implementing, and governing enterprise digital platforms and automation initiatives across business functions. The role focuses on translating business requirements into scalable digital solutions, ensuring system stability, integration, user adoption, data integrity, and compliance. This position acts as the central bridge between business stakeholders, IT teams, and external technology partners to deliver robust, future-ready digital ecosystems that support organizational growth and operational excellence.
1.1 Reports to: AVP / AGM Digital
1.2 Direct Reportees: Digital Analysts / Application Owners / Project-based Technical & Functional Coordinators
1.3 Working Relationships
Internal: Business Operations, Marketing & Exports, HR, Finance, SCM, Manufacturing, IAD, Safety, Admin, Corporate Communications External: Software Vendors, System Integrators, Cloud & Automation Partners, Consultants, Auditors
   2. Key Responsibilities
• Lead design, deployment, and enhancement of enterprise digital solutions including CRM, LMS, DMS, workflow automation, and internal portals.
• Own the end-to-end lifecycle of digital platforms—from requirement gathering and solution design to deployment, stabilization, and optimization.
• Act as primary business and technical SPOC for Salesforce, SAP-integrated applications, and other enterprise systems.
• Drive automation of manual and semi-manual processes to improve efficiency, accuracy, and turnaround time.
• Ensure seamless integration between SAP, CRM, HRMS, LMS, IAD, and other core systems.
• Govern UAT cycles, release management, and post-go-live stabilization for all digital initiatives.
• Monitor system adoption, user behavior, data quality, and compliance with defined digital processes.
• Define and enforce digital governance frameworks, access controls, and data security standards.
• Collaborate with business teams to identify digital improvement opportunities aligned with strategic objectives.
• Prepare functional specifications, BRDs, FSDs, and solution architecture documents.
• Manage vendor performance, SLAs, SoWs, and commercial alignment for digital solutions.
• Ensure platforms are audit-ready with proper documentation, logs, and compliance alignment.
• Support leadership dashboards, analytics, and MIS systems for data-driven decision-making.
• Stay updated on emerging digital technologies and recommend innovations relevant to the organization.
3. Required Qualifications
• BE / BTech / MCA / MBA (Systems, IT, Operations, or equivalent)
• 7–12 years of experience in Digital Solutions, Enterprise Applications, or IT Business Partnering roles
• Hands-on exposure to CRM, ERP integrations, workflow automation, and enterprise platforms
