Core AI skills:
1. Deep Learning Expertise: Strong theoretical and practical understanding of modern neural network
architectures, including Transformers, Attention techniques, Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), Recurrent
Neural Networks (RNNs / LSTM) etc. Hands-on exposure to training and evaluation of AI models is required.
2. Hands-on exposure to DL frameworks: Pytorch (Preferred)/Jax/Tensorflow. Exposure to scaling to multi-GPU
and usage of tools like DeepSpeed, FSDP etc is preferable.
3. Generative AI: Hands-on experience with generative models like GANS, VAEs and contemporary Generative
AI models, specifically Diffusion Models, Flow-Matching, multi-modal models and advanced techniques in
LLMs. In-depth understanding of the internals of transformer architectures, attention mechanism required.
4. Reinforcement Learning: Understanding of Deep Reinforcement Learning (DRL) fundamentals with RL-based
project exposure and familiarity of common RL algorithms (Policy Gradient, DQN, GRPO variants etc). Should
have the ability to formulate real-world business scenarios in terms of MDP.
5. Agentic AI: Familiarity with the concepts and implementation of AI Agents, memory, planning, intelligent
function calling, and reasoning in autonomous systems. Ability to understand and adapt latest research
advances in the field for use cases like complex task automation.
6. Working knowledge of Geometric Deep Learning, GNNs. Exposure to working on protein, molecular, and
point cloud data would be a plus.
7. Working knowledge of representation learning models like deep latent models, contrastive learning
techniques etc
8. Programming Proficiency: Expertise in one or more major deep learning frameworks such as PyTorch
(preferred), JAX, TensorFlow. Strong coding skills in Python. Exposure to data engineering, model training on
multiple GPUs would be preferable.
Project / Internship experience in ML/DL, demonstrating any of the above skills, is preferable.
Life Sciences Domain + AI Skills (Optional):

data).
AI for Simulations (PDE / CFD) & Materials Design (Optional):

electronic structure basics, DFT, Molecular Dynamics (MD) & Monte Carlo Methods is required.

DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
As a Research Engineer(Applied AI) for LTIMindtree Research, you will be working on the application of
cutting-edge AI (deep learning, reinforcement learning, generative AI) to solve challenging problems in
domains like life sciences, defense, automotive, energy, finance, etc.
The role involves doing applied research, and you will be instrumental in transforming state-of-the-art AI
research into scalable, real-world solutions.
Candidates should have strong foundational skills in computer science, algorithms and mathematics as
required for deep learning, reinforcement learning, as well as hands-on exposure to leading deep learning
frameworks.
Candidates pursuing MTech-AI, MTech-CS, MTech (Computational Data Sciences) are well aligned for this
position.
