DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Job Title: Quantum Security Engineer
Position Overview
This role sits at the intersection of AI safety, cybersecurity, and research, offering hands-on experience in evaluating and securing advanced AI systems. You’ll experiment, prototype, and push boundaries while learning from industry mentors. The focus is on building defenses, analyzing vulnerabilities, and contributing to the development of safe and ethical AI solutions.
Key Responsibilities
• Run AI safety evaluations, adversarial testing, and red-teaming on LLMs and multimodal AI systems.
• Build tools and defenses against prompt injections, hallucinations, and unsafe model behaviors.
• Prototype quantum-safe cryptography and post-quantum security solutions for next-generation systems.
• Analyze vulnerabilities across AI pipelines—from model training and data ingestion to API access and deployment.
• Collaborate with cybersecurity and privacy teams to validate model reliability, fairness, and privacy guarantees.
• Develop automation and dashboards to monitor model performance, drift, and security health in real time.
• Research emerging AI-driven cyber threats and publish internal insights or contribute to open research forums.
• Participate in capture-the-flag (CTF) inspired AI red teams and safety simulation hackathons.
Required Qualifications & Skills
• Strong coding skills (Python, C++, or similar).
• Familiarity with AI/ML frameworks and MLOps tools (TensorFlow Extended, Hugging Face Hub, Azure ML).
• Understanding of cybersecurity principles and tools (OWASP, Burp Suite, Metasploit).
• Knowledge of quantum-safe cryptography and post-quantum security standards (CRYSTALS-Kyber, NIST PQC).
• Ability to work with automation and containerization tools (Docker, Kubernetes) and monitoring dashboards (Grafana, Prometheus).
• Comfort with adversarial testing, vulnerability analysis, and secure API practices.
Preferred Attributes
• Curiosity about AI ethics and security challenges.
• Strong problem-solving skills and willingness to experiment in uncertain environments.
• Passion for learning and contributing to cutting-edge research.
• Collaborative mindset and ability to thrive in team-based hackathons or red-team exercises.
• Interest in publishing research or contributing to open-source security tools
