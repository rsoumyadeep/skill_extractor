DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
AEROSPACE CONTROL SYSTEM ENGINEER


Job Location: Bengaluru, Karnataka


About Us
We are a fast-growing deep-tech aerospace company building the world’s most advanced aero engines for both military and civilian applications. Our current focus includes jet fuel-based propulsion systems for military UAVs, as well as pioneering green hydrogen–powered aero engine R&D for future aviation. Our team culture revolves around first-principles thinking, scientific rigor, and rapid innovation, and we are looking for engineers who are open-minded, technically curious, and ready to solve some of the toughest challenges in aerospace propulsion.


Your Role
As an aero engine Control Systems Engineer, you will hold a key technical role focused on the development and implementation of the engine's core control logic. You will be responsible for the entire model-based design lifecycle, from creating adaptive control algorithms in MATLAB/Simulink to implementing them on embedded microcontrollers using C. This position requires you to perform rigorous system-level validation through both software-in-the-loop (SIL) and hands-on hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) testing, demanding a strong practical understanding of control theory, real-time programming, and aerospace electronics.


Key Responsibilities
• Design, implement, and validate adaptive control algorithms for jet engine systems under different environmental and operational conditions.
• Develop high-fidelity analytical and simulation models of jet engine control dynamics using MATLAB/Simulink and embedded platforms.
• Collaborate with cross-functional teams including mechanical, thermal, and avionics engineers to ensure robust system integration and performance.
• Conduct system-level testing and validation, including hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) and software-in-the-loop (SIL) testing.
• Provide technical support in debugging embedded hardware, including interfacing sensors, actuators, and micro-controllers.
• Document control strategies, test results, and version-controlled software using industry-standard tools.


Required Qualifications & Skills
• Bachelor's or Master’s Degree in Electrical Engineering or related field, with a focus on Control Systems or Embedded Systems for aerospace applications.
• Proficient in MATLAB/Simulink, Embedded C, and real-time microcontroller programming.
• Experience with model-based design, auto-code generation, and firmware development for aerospace or automotive systems.
• Strong understanding of digital and analogue electronics, including experience in PCB-level debugging, soldering, wiring, and instrumentation.
• Familiarity with communication protocols (e.g., CAN, SPI, UART) and sensor/actuator integration.
• Familiarity with tools like Git, Oscilloscope, Logic Analyzer, Multimeter.
• Enthusiasm for working in experimental environments involving real hardware and field testing.


Preferred Skills
• Direct experience with gas turbine engines or the development of Full Authority Digital Engine Controls (FADEC).
• Experience with flight controllers and associated firmware.
• Knowledge of safety-critical software development standards.
• Familiarity with real-time operating systems (RTOS).


What will you gain from this job:
• End-to-end exposure to real-world jet engine development, including control systems, hardware realization, and flight testing.
• Mentorship from experienced aerospace engineers and hands-on work with cutting-edge propulsion technologies.
• Opportunity to work on advanced aerospace R&D in high-speed transonic regimes, using both scientific and practical engineering approaches.
• A strong stepping stone toward advanced careers in propulsion, flight control, embedded systems, or aerospace research.
• Professional work environment with a five-day work week, standard benefits, and collaboration on world-class projects.
