DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Job Summary:
As part of Ola’s Vehicle Engineering team, you will design, develop, and validate core EV technologies across power electronics, power systems, robotics, embedded controllers, and electromagnetics. You will work end-to-end on system architecture, hardware-software integration, and performance validation to deliver reliable, safe, and scalable systems for next-generation Ola Electric platforms.


Role 1: Power Electronics
Specialization:
Inverters, converters, onboard chargers, switching control, high-voltage EV hardware.
Key Roles & Responsibilities (KRAs):
Deliver high-efficiency inverter, converter, and onboard charging systems for next-gen Ola EVs.
Own end-to-end validation across voltage, load, thermal, and environmental stress conditions.
Optimize switching strategies, control algorithms, and protection logic to enhance range and efficiency.
Collaborate with BMS, motor control, and vehicle integration teams for system-level performance.
Drive component selection, supplier alignment, and cost optimization without compromising safety.
Qualifications & Skills:
M.Tech in Electrical, Electronics, Power Engineering, or related field.
Strong understanding of HV systems, power semiconductors, converters/inverters.
Experience with validation, testing, modelling, and safety compliance for power electronics.


Role 2: Power Systems
Specialization:
Vehicle power architecture, HV/LV systems, power distribution & safety.
Key Roles & Responsibilities (KRAs):
Architect and validate complete vehicle power distribution networks.
Optimise HV/LV architecture for charging, performance, and auxiliary loads.
Lead system modelling, simulations, and FMEA for safety and stability.
Work with software, BMS, and thermal teams to enhance system-level energy efficiency.
Deliver diagnostics, protection logic, and power management strategies.
Qualifications & Skills:
Master’s Degree in Electrical Engineering, Power Systems, or similar.
Expertise in HV/LV architecture, safety standards, and EV power distribution.
Strong background in simulations, modelling, and failure analysis.


Role 3: Robotics & Humanoids
Specialization:
Industrial robotics, humanoids, automation systems, motion control.
Key Roles & Responsibilities (KRAs):
Develop robotics and humanoid systems for automation and intelligent operations at the Futurefactory.
Build motion planning, perception, and actuation modules with high accuracy and responsiveness.
Integrate multi-sensor fusion, control algorithms, and safety mechanisms into production-ready systems.
Collaborate with mechanical, controls, and AI teams to scale automation throughput.
Conduct data-driven tuning, testing, and failure-mode analysis.
Qualifications & Skills:
M.Tech in Robotics, Mechanical, Mechatronics, AI, or related fields.
Strong expertise in robotics algorithms, perception, control, and sensor fusion.
Experience deploying robotics systems in real environments is preferred.


Role 4: Embedded Systems
Specialization:
Embedded firmware, ECU development, automotive-grade software.
Key Roles & Responsibilities (KRAs):
Develop production-grade firmware for vehicle ECUs ensuring deterministic and reliable performance.
Own feature development, board bring-up, driver integration, and firmware optimization.
Collaborate with hardware, software, and diagnostics teams to ensure seamless system integration.
Implement safety, cybersecurity, and OTA-ready frameworks.
Improve memory efficiency, latency, power usage, and fault recovery mechanisms.
Qualifications & Skills:
Master’s Degree in Electronics, Computer Engineering, Embedded Systems, or similar.
Strong knowledge of C/C++, RTOS, microcontrollers, and automotive communication protocols.
Experience in embedded testing, debugging, and safety-compliant firmware development.


Role 5: Electromagnetics
Specialization:
Motor EM design, antenna systems, EMC/EMI simulation & compliance.
Key Roles & Responsibilities (KRAs):
Lead EM design, simulation, and validation for motors, antennas, and wireless systems.
Ensure EMC/EMI compliance and regulatory adherence across all vehicle operating conditions.
Build EM models and run optimisation to improve performance and reduce noise/EM interference.
Collaborate with powertrain, connectivity, and integration teams to resolve EM issues.
Deliver test procedures, mitigation strategies, and documentation for certification.
Qualifications & Skills:
M.Tech in Electromagnetics, Electrical Engineering, RF Engineering, or similar.
Experience with EM simulation tools, EMC/EMI testing, and regulatory standards.
Strong understanding of motor design, wireless systems, and interference mitigation.


Perks of working with Ola:
Work on cutting-edge EV and robotics technologies that shape the future of mobility.
Collaborate with top engineers and leaders across world-class R&D and manufacturing teams.
Own high-impact charters with direct visibility to senior leadership.
Access advanced labs, testing infrastructure, and next-gen engineering tools.
Accelerated growth in a fast-paced, innovation-first environment.
