DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Job Summary:
We are seeking a highly motivated and talented individual to join our team as a Post Graduate Engineer Trainee. In this role, you will work under the guidance of experienced engineers to develop your skills and gain hands-on experience in the field. This is an excellent opportunity for recent graduates to kickstart their career in the engineering industry.


Responsibilities:
• Collaborate with senior engineers to assist in the development and implementation of engineering projects.
• Conduct research and analysis to support engineering design and problem-solving activities.
• Assist in the testing and validation of engineering systems and processes.
• Participate in technical meetings and contribute ideas to optimize project outcomes.
• Document project progress and prepare reports for review by the engineering team.
• Follow established protocols and procedures to ensure compliance with safety and quality standards.
• Stay updated on industry trends and advancements to enhance engineering knowledge and skills.


Required Skills and Qualifications:
• Master's degree in Engineering or related field.
• Strong diagnostic skills and a solid understanding of engineering principles.
• Proficient in relevant software and computer-aided design (CAD) tools.
• Excellent problem-solving and analytical thinking abilities.
• Effective communication and interpersonal skills.
• Ability to work well in a team and adapt to changing priorities.
• Attention to detail and a commitment to producing high-quality work.


Preferred Skills:
• Experience with project management tools and methodologies.
• Knowledge of regulatory requirements and standards relevant to the industry.


Additional Information:
• This is a full-time position 
• Travel may be required for project-related activities.
• Competitive salary and benefits package offered.
• We are an equal opportunity employer and encourage applicants from all backgrounds to apply.
