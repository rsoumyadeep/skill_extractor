DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
The primary purpose of this role is to provide advanced analytical capabilities to support data science initiatives. This
position gains experience in various areas including, but not limited to: predictive modeling; personalization and
recommendation algorithms; natural language processing and text mining; search recall, precision, ranking, and related
problems; optimization and mathematical programming with applications in merchandising like assortment
optimization, retail space planning, and product description,. A Data Scientist solves complex problems that do not yet
have solutions in the research. Therefore, the individual in this role must be an expert in his/her field and confident in
interacting and proposing solutions to the business. This role influences the thought process and methodology for
other team members.


Core Responsibilities:
• You will build and deploy state of the art predictive models for use cases such as assortment optimization,
automated item listing validation and retail space optimization.
• Develop and maintain pipelines for training and deploying machine learning models
• Develop and maintain forecasting models and tools using frameworks such as TensorFlow
• Design and deploy scalable API services for distributing model output data
• Support enterprise forecasting applications
• Collaborate with Data Scientists and Data Engineers to ensure that specifications are translated into flexible,
• scalable, and maintainable solutions
• Stay abreast of state-of-the-art machine learning technologies; follow code standards and best practices
• Mines and extracts data and applies statistics and algorithms necessary to derive insights for Lowe's
• Supports the generation of an automated insights generation framework for Lowe's business partners to
effectively interpret data
• Provides actionable insights through data science on Merchandising priorities
• Tracks success of Lowe's through the development of dashboard reports that measure financial results,
customer satisfaction, and engagement metrics
• Conducts deep statistical analysis, including predictive and prescriptive modeling in order to provide a
competitive advantage at Lowe's
• Maintains expert-level knowledge on industry trends, emerging technologies, and new methodologies and
applies it to projects as well as using it to guide team members
• Translates or provides guidance to more junior level scientists to translate requirements into an analytical
approach; asks the right questions to understand the problem; validates understanding with Stakeholder or
Manager
• Builds or provides guidance for building the analytic approach to solving a business problem; helps identify the
sources, methods, parameters, and procedures to be used; clarifies expectations with stakeholders and plans
resources accordingly
• Leverages deep understanding of statistical techniques and tools to analyze data according to the project plan;
communicates with stakeholders to provide updates and manage scope
• Maintains knowledge of techniques, technology, industry trends, best practices, and emerging methodologies
