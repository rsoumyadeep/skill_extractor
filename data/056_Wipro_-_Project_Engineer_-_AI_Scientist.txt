DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Job Role: AI Scientist
Position Overview:
As an AI Scientist at Wipro Innovation Network, you will work on next-generation AI systems — from generative models and LLMs to agentic AI, multimodal learning, and advanced optimization techniques.
You will experiment, prototype, and build AI systems that solve real-world problems across industries, collaborating with researchers, engineers, and product teams.
This role gives you exposure to research-grade models, large datasets, AI frameworks, and WIN Labs’ frontier-tech ecosystem.
Key Responsibilities
• Build and experiment with AI/ML models: LLMs, SLMs, generative models, NLP, CV, and multi-modal AI.
• Develop algorithms and prototypes to solve real-world problems across different domains.
• Train and fine-tune models on large datasets; experiment with optimizations to improve performance.
• Work on agentic AI workflows: tool-using agents, retrieval, planning, reasoning, multi-agent orchestration.
• Translate business problems into scalable AI/ML solutions with guidance from senior engineers.
• Explore approaches for AI safety, robustness, red-teaming, & alignment.
• Contribute to internal IP, accelerators, or experiments.
• Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate models into production-grade systems.
• Stay updated on the latest AI research trends and apply them in experiments.
Required Qualifications & Skills
• Strong foundation in data structures, algorithms, probability, statistics, and linear algebra.
• Solid programming skills in Python; familiarity with ML frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow) is ideal.
• Coursework or project experience in:
o Machine Learning
o Deep Learning
o Natural Language Processing
o Computer Vision
• Familiarity with Transformers, LLM architectures, attention mechanisms, or generative AI concepts.
• Basic understanding of cloud environments (AWS/Azure/GCP) and distributed computing.
• Strong analytical, mathematical, and problem-solving skills.
• Curiosity to explore research papers, experiment with ideas, and build prototypes.
Preferred Attributes
• Experience from internships, ML competitions (Kaggle), research labs, or college robotics/AI clubs.
• Understanding of:
o Reinforcement learning
o Multi-agent systems
o Retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)
o LangChain or similar frameworks
• Exposure to LLM fine-tuning, quantization, optimization, or prompt engineering.
• Experience with GitHub, open-source contributions, or personal AI projects..
