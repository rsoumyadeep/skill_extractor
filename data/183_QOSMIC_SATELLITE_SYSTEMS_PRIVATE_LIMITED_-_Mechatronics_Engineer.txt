DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Join us in developing precision mechanical systems and motion control platforms for optical communication hardware. You will design and build electromechanical systems including positioning mechanisms, servo control systems, and mechanically stable platforms across various projects. This role involves hands-on work integrating mechanics, electronics, and control systems for field-deployable platforms supporting terrestrial and space-based applications.
