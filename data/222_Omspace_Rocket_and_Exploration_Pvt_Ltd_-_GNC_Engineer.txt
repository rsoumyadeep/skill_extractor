DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Job Title: GNC Engineer


Job Summary
We are seeking an experienced Guidance, Navigation, and Control (GNC) Engineer for aerospace industry. The ideal candidate will design, implement, and validate GNC algorithms for launch vehicles, satellites, and spacecraft.


Key Responsibilities
Develop and optimize GNC algorithms for trajectory guidance, orbit control, and attitude determination.
Perform modeling, simulation, and analysis of aerospace vehicles across various mission phases.
Integrate and validate navigation sensors (IMU, GPS, star trackers, gyros) with control systems.
Prepare technical documentation, reports, and reviews in compliance with aerospace standards.
Support mission analysis, pre-flight reviews, and anomaly investigations.

Knowledge of standards and practices in aerospace mission assurance.
Strong teamwork, problem-solving, and communication skills.
