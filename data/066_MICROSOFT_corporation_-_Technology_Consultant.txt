DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Overview
As a Technology Consultant, you will contribute to pre-sales processes, engage with customers and partners to understand business needs, and deliver technical solutions that drive value. You will leverage your knowledge of Microsoft technologies and intellectual property, support project planning and delivery, and ensure operational excellence while building strong customer relationships.
Microsoft’s mission is to empower every person and every organisation on the planet to achieve more. As employees, we come together with a growth mindset, innovate to empower others, and collaborate to realise our shared goals. Each day, we build on our values of respect, integrity, and accountability to create a culture of inclusion where everyone can thrive at work and beyond.


Responsibilities
Participate in project planning by identifying risks, dependencies, and proposing mitigations, ensuring alignment with customer business goals.
Implement technical solutions, coordinating with customers and partners to deliver quality outcomes and support business transformation.
Cultivate strong relationships with customers, partners, and stakeholders to identify satisfaction drivers and establish recovery actions to improve experience.
Contribute to pre-sales activities by sharing technical expertise and helping to scope projects in your specialty domain.
Stay current with Microsoft technologies, industry trends, and best practices; share knowledge within the team and technical communities.
Support operational excellence by adhering to Microsoft policies, compliance procedures, and ensuring timely completion of readiness tasks.
Provide feedback and contribute to the development and improvement of intellectual property (IP), tools, and methodologies.


Qualifications
Required Qualifications
B.Tech degree in Computer Science or a related field.
Currently pursuing & in final year of M.Tech degree in Computer Science or a related field.
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to learn new technologies quickly.
Excellent communication and collaboration skills with diverse stakeholders.


Preferred Qualifications
Technical certifications in Azure, Security, or Dynamics.
Exposure to consulting engagements and solution delivery projects.
