DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
About Us
We are driving innovation in next-generation automotive platforms, delivering cutting-edge solutions for ADAS, Fusion, and zonal architectures. Our team works on Renesas R-Car SoCs, enabling high-performance, low-power automotive systems with advanced AI capabilities.
Role Overview
As an Embedded Software Developer, you will design, develop, and optimize software across the entire stack—from secure bootloaders to AI-driven applications—on R-Car SoCs.
This role demands strong expertise in embedded systems, performance tuning, and AI optimization for automotive-grade platforms.
Key Responsibilities
• Develop Bootloader, Linux, Android and QNX enablement for multi-core SoCs.
• Implement fast boot strategies and optimize boot time for automotive use cases.
• Design and implement software for heterogeneous compute units (CPU, GPU, DSP, NPU).
• Optimize low-level drivers and middleware for performance and reliability.
• AI Optimization & Applications
• Port and optimize AI models for R-Car accelerators using Renesas AI toolchains.
• Implement quantization, pruning, and performance tuning for real-time inference.
• Develop reference applications for ADAS and cockpit domains leveraging CNN, ISP, and vision processing pipelines.
• System Integration & Validation
• Collaborate with hardware teams for SoC bring-up and validation.
• Conduct benchmarks and profiling using toolchains and simulators.
• Ensure compliance with automotive standards (ISO26262, ASPICE).
Required Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or M.E./M.Tech in Electronics, Computer Science, or related field.
• Strong programming skills in C/C++ and embedded Linux.
• Hands-on experience with embedded system development.
• Familiarity with AI/ML frameworks (TensorFlow Lite, PyTorch Mobile) and hardware acceleration.
• Knowledge of python is an added plus.
• Knowledge of automotive communication protocols and working knowledge with Linux/Android/FreeRTOS/Zephyr.
Preferred Skills
• Understanding of SoC architecture and multi-threaded programming.
• Experience with AI compiler toolchains and performance optimization.
What We Offer
• Opportunity to work on industry-first 3nm automotive SoCs with advanced AI capabilities.
• Access to Renesas AI toolchains and cutting-edge development platforms.
• Mentorship and global collaboration across automotive innovation teams.
• Competitive compensation, flexible work options, and career growth in automotive technology.
