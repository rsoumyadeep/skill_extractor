DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Electronics and Communication / Electrical / Instrumentation Engineering
Role Overview – Avionics Engineer We are seeking highly motivated and technically strong graduates to join our Avionics team. You will work on end-to-end development of flight-critical electronic systems, embedded software, and electrical integration that power our space launch vehicles. This role provides an exceptional opportunity to apply your electronics and software expertise to real-world aerospace applications in a fast-paced, innovation-driven environment. 
Roles and Responsibilities
Contribute to the design and development of embedded systems, electronics hardware, and software for flight and ground systems.
Work on hardware design, PCB development, firmware programming, and system testing.
Support integration and validation of avionics systems including sensors, controllers, and power distribution.
Participate in data acquisition, telemetry, and control system development for launch vehicles.
Collaborate across teams (mechanical, propulsion, systems) to ensure seamless system-level performance.
Ensure compliance with aerospace quality, safety, and reliability standards.
  Must-Have Qualifications
B.Tech / B.E. (Final Year or Recent Graduate) in Electronics and Communication, Electrical, or Instrumentation Engineering.
Strong fundamentals in electronics, control systems, embedded systems, and circuit design.
Proficiency in C/C++ or Python, and exposure to microcontrollers, FPGAs, or embedded platforms.
Knowledge of electrical schematics, PCB tools, or simulation software (MATLAB, LTSpice, etc.).
Strong analytical, problem-solving, and debugging skills.
Excellent teamwork, communication, and a passion for technology and aerospace innovation.
Why Join Skyroot Aerospace?
Work on cutting-edge avionics that power India’s private space missions.
Collaborate with experienced engineers, scientists, and ISRO alumni.
Opportunity to see your designs and code literally take flight.
A learning-driven, collaborative culture focused on engineering excellence and innovation.
 Location: Hyderabad, Telangana
Employment Type: Full-time
