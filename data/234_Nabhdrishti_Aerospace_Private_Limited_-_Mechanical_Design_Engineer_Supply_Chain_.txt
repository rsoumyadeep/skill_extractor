DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Qualifications & Skills:
• Bachelor’s, Master’s or Dual Degree in Mechanical, Aerospace Engineering or closely related
fields.
• Strong fundamentals in machine design, structural mechanics, structural dynamics FEA.
• Working knowledge of FEA/CAD tools like ANSYS, Siemens-NX is a plus.
• Supply Chain Engineer role requires basic understanding of material properties, manufacturing
processes, and supply chain principles.
• Strong analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.
• Ability to work collaboratively, adapt in a dynamic environment, and drive innovation.
Key Responsibilities:
• Mechanical design and analysis of gas turbine components and systems (Combustor, Turbine,
Compressor, Casings, Rotors, etc.).
• Perform structural and thermal analysis using simulation tools (ANSYS, Abaqus) to assess
component life.
• Support in preparation of detailed 2D drawings, 3D CAD models, and tolerance stack-ups
(Siemens NX, AutoCAD).
• Support rig and engine test development, instrumentation planning, and data analysis.
• Supply Chain Engineer: Oversee procurement and manufacturing operations related to gas
turbine components.
