DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Vervesemi Microelectronics requires Analog IC Design Engineer. The requirement involves design of various Analog and Mixed Signal blocks like Regulators, Bias References, PLLs, ADCs, DACs, and Other Analog Blocks like Operational amplifiers, Comparators, Oscillators, Voltage and Current Reference circuits etc. The job involves Circuit Design, Simulation and to work in the complete project cycle until tape-out
