DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Research Scientist – Materials & Innovation
Applied Science Works, Bengaluru
________________________________________
About Us
Applied Science Works is a U.S.-based company currently in the process of establishing its knowledge center in India, with its headquarters in Bangalore. The organization’s mission is to address ab initio materials design challenges by integrating physics-based modelling, AI-driven heuristics, and human expertise, thereby reducing exascale search spaces to practical, experimentally actionable outcomes. With a strong focus on R&D and innovation, Applied Science Works envisions combining AI-led discovery with applied sciences to accelerate materials research and technological innovation.
What are we looking for?
We are seeking for the right candidates to join our R&D team as Research Scientist & Engineer.
In this role, the selected candidate will work at the intersection of materials research, technology development, and innovation and will be responsible for designing and executing experiments, optimizing processes, and collaborating closely with multidisciplinary teams to translate scientific discoveries into practical, real-world solutions.
The Responsibilities
• Design and execute research projects in materials synthesis, characterization, and process optimization
• Develop novel experimental techniques and integrate innovative technologies into research workflows
• Analyze experimental data and develop predictive models using computational and AI-based techniques
• Automate initiatives, dataset development, and laboratory infrastructure setup
• Troubleshoot technical challenges and ensure effective delivery
• Maintain rigorous safety and compliance standards


Required Qualification:
• Master’s or higher degree (M.Sc,M.Tech,PhD) in Chemistry, Chemical Engineering, Materials Science, Physics, Computer Science, Mechanical Engineering, or related field.
• Hands-on R&D experience (freshers to 5+ years depending on role specialization)
• Strong problem-solving, analytical, and learning skills
• Expertise in one or more areas: materials synthesis, characterization techniques (microscopy, spectroscopy, diffraction), process development, or data analysis
• Laboratory experience with complex instrumentation and experimental design
• Effective teamwork and communication skills
• Strong safety mindset and responsibility
Preferred:
• PhD in Chemistry, Physics, or Materials Science
• Advanced synthesis expertise (PVD, sputtering, PLD, MBE, CVD)
• High-throughput or AI-driven materials discovery experience
• Publications or recognized field contributions
• Automation scripting, LIMS, or Python/MATLAB experience
• Work with national user facilities (synchrotrons, neutron sources)
• Startup or fast-paced R&D environment experience


Our Offer
• Competitive salary, aligned with industry standards and commensurate with experience
• Comprehensive employee benefits package, including health insurance and statutory benefits
• Cutting-edge research and innovation projects with real-world impact
• Collaborative and supportive team environment that values knowledge sharing
• Rapid skill development across diverse technical and scientific domains
• Dynamic startup culture with flexibility, ownership, and a strong learning focus
• Strong commitment to safety, well-being, and long-term professional growth
• Bengaluru-based role with access to a vibrant and globally connected technology ecosystem


How to apply
Email resume, cover letter, and brief description of research interests/technical expertise. Highlight relevant projects and techniques. Share link: LinkedIn, GitHub, scholar.google.com
