DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Job Title: Quantum Developer
Position Overview
As a Quantum Developer, you will design and implement quantum algorithms, build prototypes, and develop production-ready components that integrate quantum and classical systems. This role involves working on scalable, maintainable codebases and optimizing quantum circuits for performance and reliability. You’ll be at the forefront of quantum computing innovation, contributing to solutions that push the boundaries of computational possibilities
Key Responsibilities
• Build prototypes and demos by implementing quantum algorithms and hybrid models.
• Develop production or near-production quantum software components across front-end, back-end, and middleware layers.
• Collaborate with architects to ensure scalability, testability, and maintainability of the codebase.
• Optimize algorithms through circuit optimization, error mitigation, and resource estimation.
• Utilize cloud quantum platforms and SDKs; integrate quantum solutions with classical infrastructure.
• Support performance profiling, testing, debugging, and contribute to asset and accelerator development.
Required Qualifications & Skills
• Proficiency in quantum programming frameworks (Qiskit, Cirq, Pennylane) and simulation tools.
• Strong foundation in mathematics, linear algebra, and probability as applied to quantum computing.
• Excellent coding skills in languages such as Python and C++.
• Familiarity with cloud-based quantum platforms and integration techniques.
Preferred Qualifications
• Innovative mindset with a passion for emerging quantum technologies.
• Strong problem-solving skills and ability to optimize complex algorithms.
• Collaborative approach with attention to scalability and maintainability.
• Adaptability to work across multiple layers of technology stack.
• Interest in continuous learning and contributing to cutting-edge research.
