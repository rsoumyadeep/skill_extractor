DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Qualifications & Skills:
• Bachelor’s, Master’s or Dual Degree in Mechanical or Aerospace Engineering or closely related
fields.
• Strong foundation in Thermodynamics, Fluid Mechanics, Heat Transfer, and Turbomachinery.
• Familiarity with CFD tools (ANSYS Fluent, CFX) is an added advantage.
• For Testing roles: Exposure to experimental methods and hands-on lab/test setup experience is
essential.
• Strong problem-solving, teamwork, and data-driven decision-making skills.
Key Responsibilities:
• Aerothermal design and performance analysis of gas turbine flowpath components.
• Conceptual 1D/2D flowpath design and optimization.
• Conduct CFD and heat transfer simulations to improve engine performance.
• Collaborate with CAD designers on 2D/3D model development.
• Design and execute rig/engine tests, define instrumentation, and analyze test results.
• Performance Engineer: Develop the engine performance model by integrating the performance
characteristics of various sub-systems. Executes engine verification and certification strategy.
• Thermofluids Engineer: Develop Thermo-mechanical and Secondary Air Systems (SAS) model to
ensure thermal performance and cooling, sealing, and pressurization requirement met for the
engine.
• Testing Engineer: Plan and execute engine tests, define sensor layout, and interpret
experimental data.
