DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
The position focuses on Finite Element Analysis (FEA) and AI/ML simulation model development for HVAC&R product lines.
Main Responsibilities:
Conduct FEA simulations and integrate outcomes into AI/ML model development.
Verify, validate, and extract data from simulation studies.
Perform data analysis, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and feature engineering.
Execute parametric optimization using simulation and data-driven approaches.
Develop, train, and deploy AI/ML models to enhance simulation accuracy and efficiency.
Collaborate with CAD Designers, FEA Analysts, and IT teams to ensure smooth data integration and toolchain development.
Knowledge and capabilities required:
Ability to independently manage and execute projects with minimal supervision.
Strong command of Solid Mechanics, Mechanical Vibrations, Programming concepts.
Excellent communication and cross-functional collaboration skills, especially in global environments.
Aptitude for learning emerging technologies and applying them effectively.
Demonstrated ability to automate workflows and identify process improvements.
Team-oriented mindset with strong problem-solving and analytical skills.
