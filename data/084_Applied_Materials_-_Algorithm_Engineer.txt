DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
About the Role
We are seeking highly motivated Algorithm Engineers to design and develop cutting-edge algorithms that power our next-generation products. This role involves solving complex problems in areas such as optimization, machine learning, signal processing, and computational mathematics. Ideal candidates will have strong theoretical foundations and the ability to translate them into practical, scalable solutions.


Key Responsibilities
• Research, design, and implement advanced algorithms for real-world applications.
• Analyze problem requirements and propose innovative solutions.
• Develop prototypes and production-ready implementations.
• Optimize algorithms for performance, scalability, and robustness.
• Collaborate with software engineers and domain experts to integrate algorithms into products.
• Stay current with emerging trends in algorithms, AI/ML, and computational techniques.


Required Qualifications
• Education:
oMaster’s degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Applied Mathematics, or related field.
o Exceptional NCG candidates with strong academic background will also be considered.
• Strong foundation in data structures, algorithms, and complexity analysis.
• Expertise in one or more areas:
o Machine Learning / Deep Learning
o Optimization Techniques
o Signal/Image Processing
o Numerical Methods
• Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, C++, or MATLAB.
• Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
