DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Job Title: Avionics Engineer


Job Summary
We are seeking an Avionics Engineer for aerospace core hardware development. The role focuses on the design, development, and integration of avionics hardware systems, including power distribution, communication interfaces, and flight control electronics.

Key Responsibilities
Design and develop avionics hardware systems for spacecraft and launch vehicles.
Work on schematic design, PCB layout, and hardware integration for avionics subsystems.
Develop power systems, control units, and communication interfaces for aerospace applications.
Collaborate with embedded software, GNC, and systems teams for hardware-software integration.
Ensure compliance with aerospace-grade standards and environmental requirements.
Prepare and maintain hardware design documentation, schematics, and technical reports.
Support component selection, reliability analysis, and system architecture design.

ADDITIONAL DETAILS
Preferred Skills
1.Experience with fault-tolerant avionics architectures.

4.Exposure to thermal and structural constraints in hardware design.
5.Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work in cross-functional aerospace teams.
