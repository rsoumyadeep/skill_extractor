DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
JOB DESCRIPTION – MANAGER – MARKETING-EXTRUSION
(For company HR records, hiring, and role clarity)
Location Bangalore
Division Corporate
Group (Office/Factory) Office
Department Marketing
Sub- Function Extrusion
1. Job Summary
responsible for driving sales growth, market development, customer engagement, and revenue realization for the aluminium extrusion business. The role focuses on managing key accounts, developing new markets and applications, coordinating with production and supply chain, and ensuring effective execution of pricing, order management, and customer satisfaction. The position plays a critical role in translating business strategy into market-facing actions while strengthening the company’s presence across domestic and export markets.
1.1 Reports to: AGM / AVP – Marketing-Extrusion
1.2 Direct Reportees: Marketing Executives / Product Marketing
1.3 Working Relationships
Internal: Production, Planning, Quality, Dispatch & Logistics, Commercial, Finance, Design & Die Shop, Supply Chain, Digital/CRM, HR External: Customers, Dealers, Channel Partners, Architects/Consultants, Export Agents, Industry Bodies
   2. Key Responsibilities
• Drive marketing and sales operations for aluminium extrusion products across assigned regions or segments.
• Develop and manage key customer accounts to achieve volume, value, and margin targets.
• Identify new market opportunities, applications, and customer segments for extrusion products.
• Coordinate with production and planning teams to ensure timely order execution and delivery commitments.
• Prepare and manage quotations, pricing proposals, and commercial negotiations in line with company guidelines.
• Ensure accurate demand forecasting and order pipeline management.
• Monitor customer satisfaction, address complaints, and coordinate with quality and operations teams for resolution.
• Support development of new profiles, alloys, finishes, and value-added extrusion solutions based on market needs.
• Track market trends, competitor activities, and pricing movements to support strategic decisions.
• Ensure adherence to credit policy, payment follow-ups, and coordination with finance for collections.
• Maintain structured MIS on sales performance, order status, customer profitability, and market insights.
• Support export marketing activities including customer coordination, documentation alignment, and compliance inputs (where applicable).
• Participate in exhibitions, customer visits, and technical discussions to strengthen brand presence.
• Ensure compliance with company policies, safety norms, and audit requirements across marketing operations.
3. Required Qualifications
• MBA (Marketing / Sales / Operations) or BE / BTech (preferably Mechanical / Metallurgy)
• 6–10 years of experience in Marketing & Sales, preferably in Aluminium Extrusion or Manufacturing / Industrial Products
