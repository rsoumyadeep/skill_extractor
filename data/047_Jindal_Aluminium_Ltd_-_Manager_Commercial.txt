DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
JOB DESCRIPTION – MANAGER – COMMERCIAL
(For company HR records, hiring, and role clarity)
Location Bangalore
Division Corporate
Group (Office/Factory) Office
Department Commercial
Sub- Function
1. Job Summary
Responsible for managing end-to-end commercial operations including contract administration, pricing coordination, order-to-cash governance, statutory compliance, and risk mitigation. The role ensures commercial discipline across domestic and export transactions, supports business growth through structured commercial processes, and enables leadership with accurate MIS, documentation, and compliance readiness. The position plays a critical role in safeguarding organizational interests while ensuring seamless coordination with Sales, Finance, Supply Chain, and Legal functions.
1.1 Reports to: AGM / AVP – Commercial
1.2 Direct Reportees: Commercial Executives / Officers
1.3 Working Relationships
Internal: Sales & Marketing, Exports, Finance & Accounts, Supply Chain, Logistics, Legal, Taxation, Compliance, IT/SAP, Internal Audit External: Customers, Vendors, Transporters, Clearing & Forwarding Agents, Banks, Insurance Providers, Statutory Authorities, Consultants
   2. Key Responsibilities
• Oversee end-to-end commercial operations covering domestic and export transactions.
• Manage contract review, pricing approvals, credit terms, and commercial risk assessment.
• Ensure accurate execution of order-to-cash processes including invoicing, documentation, and collections coordination.
• Govern statutory and regulatory compliance related to GST, customs, export-import regulations, and commercial laws.
• Act as the commercial SPOC for Sales, Exports, and Finance for deal structuring and transaction validation.
• Ensure adherence to approved pricing, discount structures, and commercial policies.
• Drive standardization of commercial SOPs, policies, templates, and approval workflows.
• Coordinate with SAP / IT teams for commercial module enhancements, validations, and UAT activities.
• Monitor commercial exposure, receivables, and risk indicators; escalate deviations proactively.
• Prepare and present structured MIS, dashboards, and periodic reports for leadership review.
• Support audits (internal, statutory, and customer audits) with complete and accurate documentation.
• Manage and validate export documentation including contracts, LC, BG, shipping documents, and compliance filings.
• Enable process improvements through digitization, automation, and workflow optimization.
• Support strategic commercial initiatives driven by senior leadership and business expansion plans.
3. Required Qualifications
• MBA (Finance / Operations / Marketing) or CA / ICWA / BE with relevant experience
• 6–10 years of experience in Commercial Operations, Contracts, or Order-to-Cash roles
• Exposure to manufacturing / B2B / export-oriented organizations preferred
