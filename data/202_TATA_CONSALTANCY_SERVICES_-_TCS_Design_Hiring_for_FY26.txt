DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Junior Designer

Profile:
 As Junior Designer, you will be involved in some key phases
of the design process, assisting senior members of the team
as needed. Your role will be to support other senior
designers and creative leads with the design work by
creating deliverables and assets, whilst being exposed to the
latest ways of working within the industry.
We work in cross-functional teams so you will need to
demonstrate experience or an interest in visual, UI/UX,
animation design, along with the practical & technical
knowledge to support ideas.
Our designers are enthusiastic and detail oriented, able to
produce outstanding visual design work for a variety of
projects. They are conceptual thinkers, problem solvers,
collaborative, technically aware and able to manage
multiple projects with ease — those being qualities you
should also strive for.

Responsibilities:
• Create high quality deliverables ready for
implementation or that require minimum oversight, from
either presentations to final digital product application
• Efficiently produce design, UI/UX, creative work from
discussion, concepts and wireframes across multiple
media and digital products
• Have confidence when presenting your work to your
team, and the studio.
• Can work independently and in a team, to take on board
direction and action effectively
• Have a 'Can-do' attitude that always delivers on time and
to the highest quality
• Work with motion designers and creative technologists
to ensure effectiveness and feasibility of your designs

Preferences:
• A portfolio demonstrating form and function across a
variety product
• Good understanding of design fundamentals (type,
space, form)
• Good knowledge of Design Tools such as Figma and
Adobe CC
 • Self-motivated, enthusiastic, with excellent time
management
• The ability to be able to communicate your ideas clearly
• Take on constructive criticism and use this to enhance
deliverables
• A quick learner possessing a positive 'can do' attitude
