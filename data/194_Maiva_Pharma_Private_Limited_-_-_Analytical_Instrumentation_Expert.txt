DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
ABOUT MAIVA PHARMA
Maiva Pharma is a leading manufacturer of sterile injectables with USFDA, EU-GMP, Health Canada, and PIC/S approved facility in Hosur, Tamil Nadu. We specialize in pharmaceutical development and manufacturing of sterile formulations for global markets.
POSITION OVERVIEW
We are seeking an Analytical Instrumentation Expert with PhD in Chemistry who possesses deep expertise in analytical instruments. The ideal candidate should have mastery in at least one major analytical technique (e.g., GC, HPLC, FTIR, AAS, ICP-MS, IC, etc.) and serve as the technical authority for analytical instrumentation.
KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
• Instrument Expertise & Troubleshooting:
• Serve as subject matter expert for analytical instruments with deep understanding of operational principles
• Troubleshoot complex instrument issues and implement solutions to minimize downtime
• Perform root cause analysis for instrument failures and analytical anomalies
• Optimize instrument performance and recommend upgrades or modifications
• Provide technical leadership in analytical method robustness
• Collaborate with cross-functional teams (R&D, Production, QA, QC, Regulatory) to ensure seamless technology transfer
• Drive data-driven decision-making using statistical tools, QbD, and risk-based approaches
• Stay abreast of emerging technologies and regulatory trends in injectable manufacturing
• Interact with external labs when needed
• Method Development & Innovation:
• Develop and optimize analytical testing methodologies for pharmaceutical products
• Design novel analytical approaches for complex analytical challenges
• Develop and implement best practices, technical standards, and process excellence frameworks
• Stay updated with latest analytical techniques and implement improvements
• Training & Knowledge Transfer:
• Train and mentor laboratory personnel on instrument operation, maintenance, and troubleshooting
• Develop SOPs, training materials, and technical documentation
• Conduct workshops on analytical techniques and instrument principles
• Build analytical capabilities and enhance scientific understanding within the team
• Technical Support & Collaboration:
• Provide technical guidance to R&D, QC, and production teams
• Support method validation, technology transfer, and CAPA investigations
• Collaborate with instrument vendors for advanced technical support
• Identify and execute continuous improvement projects aligned with company goals
REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS
• Education:
• PhD in Chemistry / Analytical Chemistry
• Technical Expertise:
• Expert-level knowledge of at least ONE analytical instrument (GC/HPLC/UPLC/FTIR/MS/AAS/ICP-MS/IC, etc.)
• Deep understanding of instrument operating principles, detection mechanisms, and theory
• Proven troubleshooting skills with analytical instruments
• Experience in analytical method development and optimization
• Skills:
• Strong problem-solving and analytical thinking
• Proficiency in data analysis, statistical software, and technical documentation
• Excellent training and communication abilities
• Ability to work independently and lead technical initiatives
PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
• Multi-instrument expertise across different analytical techniques
• Experience in pharmaceutical/sterile injectable industry
• Publication record in analytical chemistry
• Experience with USFDA/EU-GMP regulatory environments
