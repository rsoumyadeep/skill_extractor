DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
About Skyroot Aerospace
Skyroot Aerospace is India’s leading private space launch vehicle company, founded with the vision of “Opening Space for All.” We are pioneering India’s private space revolution by developing cutting-edge launch vehicle technologies that enable cost-effective and reliable access to space. Our team is driven by innovation, technical excellence, and a shared passion for space exploration and engineering breakthroughs.
Role Overview
We are looking for highly motivated engineering graduates to join our dynamic engineering teams. You will work on challenging, multidisciplinary projects that contribute directly to the design, development, testing, and production of next-generation space launch systems.
This opportunity provides a platform to apply your core engineering knowledge to cutting-edge, real-world aerospace applications, while working alongside some of the best minds in the industry.
Roles and Responsibilities
· Participate in design, analysis and validation of advanced space systems and components.
· Support development, manufacturing, integration, and testing activities across multiple domains.
· Work collaboratively with cross-functional teams to solve complex engineering challenges and deliver innovative solutions.
· Contribute to continuous improvement, optimization, and innovation in product development.
· Ensure compliance with aerospace quality, safety, and reliability standards.
Must-Have Qualifications
· M.Tech/M.E/PHD/MS/B.Tech/B.E. (Final year or recent graduate) in Mechanical, Aerospace, Aeronautical, or Industrial Engineering.
· Strong fundamentals in core engineering subjects such as Solid Mechanics, Thermodynamics, Heat Transfer, Material Science, and Fluid Dynamics.
· Proficiency in engineering tools and software (CAD, FEA, CFD, C/C++, Python or similar)
· Strong analytical mindset, innovation-driven problem-solving, and attention to detail.
· Excellent teamwork, communication skills, and a proactive, self-driven learning attitude.
· Genuine passion for aerospace and a strong drive to build technologies that shape the future of space exploration.
Why Join Skyroot Aerospace?
· Be part of India’s first private company to build and launch space vehicles.
· Work on end-to-end development of real space systems with a young and dynamic team.
· Mentorship from experienced engineers and scientists, including ISRO alumni.
· Collaborative, high-growth environment where your ideas and work have real impact.
