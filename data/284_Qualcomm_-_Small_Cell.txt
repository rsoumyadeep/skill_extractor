DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
As a member of the team, you will be responsible for defining the System Architecture and algorithms in the NR baseband modem receiver and transmitter for PHY SW operating on a multi-core architecture. We are seeking candidates with a strong understanding of communication system fundamentals, including a clear comprehension of the performance of various NR physical channels under channel impairments.
Skills required : It is expected that the engineers:
• Are adept at C-programming and can develop real-time embedded
software for proprietary DSPs
• Understand capabilities of hardware blocks like codecs interfaces with software
• Develop signal processing algorithms which are essential for modem
performance
Knowledge of Digital Signal Processing Concepts Digital Communications
Theory/C and MATLAB programming are essential.
