DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Faculty Recruitment – CSE, AIML, DATA SCIENCE and ELECTRICAL DEPARTMENTS


Professor – 
Qualifications:
 Ph.D. with relevant experience in academia, research, or industry.
 At least one or two degrees (UG/PG/Ph.D.) in CSE, ISE, or AIML streams preferred.
Key Responsibilities:
 Lead high-impact research initiatives and mentor faculty members.
 Guide Ph.D. scholars and postgraduate research.
 Drive national/international collaborations and MoUs.
 Contribute to curriculum innovation and departmental leadership.


Associate Professor –
Qualifications:
 Ph.D. with substantial academic/research/industry experience.
 Strong research orientation and a consistent publication record.
 At least one or two degrees in CSE, ISE, or AIML preferred.
Key Responsibilities:
 Deliver advanced-level AIML courses effectively.
 Supervise student projects and research activities.
 Publish in reputed journals and conferences.
 Actively participate in curriculum development and department growth.


Assistant Professor – 
Qualifications:
 M.Tech/M.E. with First Class in AIML/CSE/ISE/Data Science or related fields.
 Ph.D. (completed or pursuing) preferred.
 Preference for candidates with at least one degree in CSE, ISE, or AIML.
Key Responsibilities:
 Teach core and elective AIML courses with a focus on hands-on, project-based learning.
 Mentor students and promote innovation and research.
 Support departmental activities and contribute to academic excellence
