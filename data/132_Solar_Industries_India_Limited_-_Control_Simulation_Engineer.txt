DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Key Responsibilities:
• Range Feasibility Studies:
o Assist in developing and refining point mass and 3 Degrees of Freedom (DOF) models.
o Support the modification of trajectory parameters to meet required specifications (range, terminal velocity, impact angle).
o Participate in the dispersion analysis of unguided systems.
• Modeling of Dynamic Systems:
o Learn and apply 6 DOF dynamics and kinematics modeling in MATLAB & Simulink, utilizing the aerospace block set and control system toolbox.
o Assist in modeling environmental disturbances such as wind, thrust misalignments, and center of gravity (CG) offsets.
• Development of Guidance Schemes (Support Role):
o Support the design and analysis of fundamental guidance schemes (e.g., Proportional Navigation (PN)) to meet mission requirements.
o Assist in trajectory shaping analysis for guided systems.
• Designing of Control Systems (Support Role):
o Assist in autopilot design using classical control methods.
o Learn to linearize dynamic systems to design autopilots using transfer function models.
o Participate in analyzing control system performance and stability using tools like Bode and Nyquist Plots.
o Support Hardware-in-the-Loop (HIL) and Software-in-the-Loop (SIL) simulations.
• Perturbation Analysis (Support Role):
o Assist with sensitivity analysis of various parameters.
o Support Monte Carlo simulations to analyze performance within permissible perturbation bands.
