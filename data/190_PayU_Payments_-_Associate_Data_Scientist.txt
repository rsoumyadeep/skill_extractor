DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
The Opportunity:
• Lead and execute Data Science projects for building best-in-class models/solutions for various use cases across the BFSI Industry
• Work with various external partners starting from POC discussions till execution and with internal partners (e.g. Business teams, technology and platform teams) to understand, design and implement the best solutions.
• Opportunity to work on exceptional projects using cutting edge technology
Requirements to be successful in this role:
• A degree in Statistics, Computer Science, Mathematics or other similar quantitative field from a premier institute.
• Should have led Data Science projects 
• Excellent stakeholder management skills – internal and external
• Detail oriented with strong organizational skills. Ability to function in a high-volume setting with tight deadlines
• Good experience in building machine learning models and should have experience in the following languages – Python, SQL. Familiar with cloud computing platforms like AWS, etc.
• Having experience in Pyspark and AutoML is a plus
• The incumbent must be a high-energy individual with a go-getter instinct and positive attitude.
• Should be a great teammate who promotes knowledge-sharing
