DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
JOB Description – Electronic Systems Engineer
Electronics & Industrial Automation Systems
Leumas · Bengaluru
Leumas is seeking an Electronic Systems Engineer to design, develop, and own electronics subsystems for modular and automated manufacturing equipment.
This is a high-ownership engineering role focused on building reliable, scalable, and production-ready electronics platforms. The role spans system architecture, embedded
development, hardware software integration, and industrial communications, working closely with mechanical, process, and software teams in a build–test–iterate environment.


About Leumas
Leumas is a Bangalore-based deep-tech manufacturing company building a software-defined production platform for healthcare manufacturing. Its proprietary, full-stack Manufacturing Operating System orchestrates the entire manufacturing lifecycle - from formulation logic and material flow to production execution, quality control, and traceability - enabling modular, reconfigurable factories that scale with demand. Leumas addresses structural challenges in global supply chains, rigidity, long lead times, and capital
intensive, step function scale - through a modular, distributed manufacturing architecture that enables faster deployment, higher asset utilization, and materially improved business outcomes across product life cycle
Leumas supports two primary deployment models. For enterprise customers, it enables dedicated manufacturing suites that provide high levels of supply-chain control, compliance, and flexibility without the burden of traditional greenfield capex or non-resilient supply chains from traditional CDMOs. For healthcare innovators, the platform allows rapid configuration of production lines through software, enabling faster product launches, tighter quality control, and responsive scale-up or scale
down.
Leumas’ production systems currently support more than a dozen finished formulations and consumer health formats, including semi-solids (ointments, gels, lotions), oral solid dosage forms (tablets, capsules), and liquid formulations, with integrated QA and inspection capabilities.


The Role
As Electronic Systems Engineer, you will be responsible for designing and delivering electronics architectures that power modular manufacturing equipment.
You will own embedded hardware and firmware subsystems, ensure robust integration with mechanical and software layers, and support commissioning and stabilization of deployed systems.
This role requires strong fundamentals, hands-on execution capability, and the ability to translate system requirements into reliable industrial-grade electronics solutions.


What You Will Own
As Electronic Systems Engineer, you will be accountable for the complete lifecycle of electronics subsystems from concept and development to validation and deployment.
1. Electronics Architecture & Subsystem Design
• Design electronics architectures covering power electronics, motor drives, sensors, and industrial I/O.
• Define subsystem interfaces, electrical specifications, and integration boundaries.
• Ensure scalability, reliability, and serviceability of designs.
2. Embedded Systems Development & Integration
• Develop and integrate microcontroller-based systems and firmware.
• Support hardware–software integration and interface validation.
• Debug and resolve system-level issues during development and deployment.
3. Industrial Communication & Interfaces
• Implement and validate industrial communication protocols (CAN, RS485, Modbus, Ethernet).
• Integrate electronics subsystems with control systems and supervisory platforms.
• Support commissioning and field-level connectivity.
4. Build–Test–Iterate Execution
• Execute structured validation and testing of electronics systems.
• Iterate designs based on prototype, fabrication, and commissioning feedback.
• Support performance optimization and stabilization activities.
5. Documentation & Engineering Governance
• Maintain schematics, test reports, release documentation, and validation records.
• Ensure version control, traceability, and audit-ready engineering documentation.
• Participate in design and technical reviews.
