DETAILED INTERNSHIP DESCRIPTION
Main Accountabilities We would like to hear from you if you:
In this role, You will:
• You will be involved in development projects related to next generations of semiconductor chips and power modules
• You are accountable for preparing the test reports and analyzing the test results and developing faultless datasheets.
• You should be able to do all simulation and analyze the results and speculate the best operating conditions of the converter topologies for specific applications.
• Responsible for electrical design of semiconductor-based solutions for AC and DC used in HVDC transmissions and responsible for the design of the optical current transducers.
• Knowledge on Closed-loop control design using Analog and digital PWM controllers
• Understanding the design specification of electrical power systems and Responsible to create the technical specification and reports, specifying the testing according to the standards.
 • M.Tech with specialization in Semiconductors Technology and / or Power Electronics
• 0-1 Years of experience
