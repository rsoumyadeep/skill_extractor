DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Job Summary
We are looking for a highly motivated Data Scientist / AI Engineer to join our advanced analytics and AI team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for developing, deploying, and optimizing machine learning and deep learning models to solve complex business problems. You will collaborate with cross-functional teams, contribute to end-to-end project lifecycles, and deliver scalable, production-ready AI solutions on cloud platforms such as Azure or AWS.


Key Responsibilities
- Contribute to all phases of data science projects — from business understanding, data preparation, and exploratory analysis to model development, evaluation, and deployment.
- Translate complex business problems into analytical frameworks and communicate insights effectively to clients and internal stakeholders.
- Develop, train, and validate machine learning and deep learning models across domains such as NLP, Computer Vision, and Applied AI.
- Deploy and productionize AI/ML models using Azure / AWS cloud services, integrating them into scalable APIs and applications.
- Collaborate with clients to gather requirements, define project scope, and present analytical outcomes and technical recommendations.
- Support pre-sales activities including technical workshops, demonstrations, and proof-of-concept (PoC) development.
- Continuously explore and learn new AI tools, libraries, and frameworks to align with evolving business and technological needs.
- Ensure quality, reproducibility, and maintainability across all deliverables.
- Manage project timelines and deliverables independently with minimal supervision.


Required Skills & Qualifications
- Strong understanding of machine learning algorithms, their strengths, and practical trade-offs.
- Hands-on experience with deep learning architectures such as CNNs, RNNs, and Transformers.
- Proficient in Python, including solid understanding of data structures and algorithms.
- Experience with ML/DL frameworks: PyTorch, TensorFlow/Keras, HuggingFace, NumPy, and Scikit-learn.
- Applied experience in at least one of the following domains:
1. Natural Language Processing (NLP)
2. Computer Vision
3. Structured/Tabular Data Modeling
4. Speech Recognition
- Experience deploying models using FastAPI, Gunicorn, Nginx, and building MLOps pipelines.
- Proficiency in Linux environments and version control systems (Git, GitHub).
- Experience with Docker (or equivalent containerization tools such as CRI-O or Podman); knowledge of Kubernetes is a plus.
- Excellent communication skills, able to convey complex technical ideas clearly to both technical and non-technical audiences.
- Strong collaboration, problem-solving, and time management skills.
