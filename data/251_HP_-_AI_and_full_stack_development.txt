DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
Key Responsibilities
AI / Data Engineering
• Build and train machine learning/deep learning models for sensor and IoT data
(time series, image, or audio).
• Apply statistical analysis, signal processing, and ML to derive insights from noisy
data streams.
• Optimize models for real-time inference on edge devices using TensorFlow Lite,
ONNX, or similar.
• Contribute to data pipelines for collecting, cleaning, and validating large-scale
IoT data.
Full Stack Engineering
• Design and develop scalable and responsive web applications and APIs.
• Build dashboard interfaces for visualizing real-time device data, alerts, and
insights.
• Implement backend systems to process, store, and manage IoT data efficiently.
• Collaborate across teams to integrate AI features into the application stack
(frontend/backend/cloud).
What We’re Looking For
• Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Electronics, Mathematics.
• Strong grasp of data structures, algorithms, system design, and core CS
fundamentals.
• Proficiency in full stack technologies including but not limited to Python for
AI/ML, and JavaScript/TypeScript for full stack development.
• Experience or familiarity with:
o AI/ML frameworks: PyTorch, TensorFlow, scikit-learn
o Web technologies: React.js, Node.js, Express, RESTful APIs
o Databases: PostgreSQL, MongoDB, or TimescaleDB
o Dev tools: Git, Docker, CI/CD pipelines
Soft Skills
• Strong problem-solving skills and a product mindset.
• Ability to work in a fast-paced, startup-like environment.
• Curiosity to explore new technologies and cross-functional learning (AI +
connected systems + UX).
• Collaborate as well as lead small groups of engineers to deliver innovative
solutions.
• Ability to learn and leverage the latest technology advances. Drives curiosity in
teams to enable the adoption of latest and the best technologies.
What You’ll Gain
• Exposure to end-to-end product development in AI + connected ecosystems.
• Real-world impact: your code and models will run in live environments.
• Mentorship from senior engineers and AI researchers.
• A collaborative culture that fosters experimentation and learning.
