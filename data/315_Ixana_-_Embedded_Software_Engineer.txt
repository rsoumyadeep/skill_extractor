DETAILED JOB DESCRIPTION
About this role


We're seeking an embedded C/C++ expert with wearable or IoT hardware development experience. You should have a proven track record of excellence and thrive in a dynamic, fast-paced startup environment.


Ixana is developing various reference hardware with Wi-R chips, including smartglasses, earbuds, smartwatches, EEG nodes, EMG nodes, smartphones, heart rate monitors, and smartrings. Your role will be to lead the embedded software development for these reference hardware applications.


Excel in this position, and you'll have the opportunity to help define the new Wi-R standard—meaning your code could be in every device around you.


Minimum Qualifications and Skills:


BS in Electronics, Electrical, Computer Engineering, Computer Science, or equivalent


Hands-on experience with full-stack MCU firmware development


Excellent C/C++ skills for embedded systems


Familiarity with communication interfaces: I2C, SPI, BLE


Experience in low-power MCU development and optimization


Ability to write bare-metal code for MCU interfaces
